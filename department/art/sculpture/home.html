Welcome to the Sculpture Department

The Sculpture Department at Malone Global University explores three-dimensional art as a vessel of memory, innovation, and spirit. Students study traditional African sculpting techniques alongside contemporary practices in metalwork, ceramics, and digital modeling.

Our Mission

The Sculpture Department is devoted to developing artists who understand the power of form and space. By connecting cultural symbolism to craftsmanship, we help students honor the past while shaping the visual languages of the future.
