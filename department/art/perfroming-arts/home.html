Welcome to the Performing Arts Department

The Performing Arts Department at Malone Global University examines the cultural, social, and historical significance of performance across African and African American communities. Students explore theater, dance, music, and multimedia performance to understand art as a living tradition.

Our Mission

Our goal is to train artists and performers who understand the power of expression, storytelling, and movement. By blending traditional African forms with contemporary approaches, we aim to preserve heritage and inspire innovation.

Faculty & Mentors

Our instructors include directors, choreographers, musicians, and performance scholars. They guide students in mastering both technical skills and the cultural context of performance art.
