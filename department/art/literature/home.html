Welcome to the Literature Department

The Literature Department at Malone Global University studies words as both art and weapon. Our courses explore African oral traditions, African American classics, diasporic poetry, and global narratives of identity and liberation.

Our Mission

We cultivate thinkers and writers who understand the power of narrative. The department seeks to preserve indigenous storytelling, challenge colonial perspectives, and uplift the voices of the marginalized through literature.

Faculty & Mentors

Our faculty includes poets, novelists, critics, and historians whose expertise spans African classics, Black feminist theory, postcolonial studies, and creative writing. Together, they help students refine both their craft and consciousness.
