Gospel

Study gospel music through worship, testimony, choir practice, sacred song, vocal power, community, and rhythm.

Overview

Gospel is a sacred musical tradition rooted in Black church life, spirituals, hymns, testimony, choir performance, and communal worship. It has also deeply influenced soul, R&B, rock, pop, and hip-hop.

This page belongs to the Genres and Traditions area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Genre pages should teach musical sound, history, community, performance practice, recordings, artists, instruments, technologies, and cultural context together.

Worship and Community

Gospel music is tied to worship, testimony, congregational participation, preaching traditions, and community expression.

Voice and Choir

Gospel often uses powerful vocal delivery, harmony, call and response, choir arrangements, and expressive performance.

Influence

Gospel has strongly influenced R&B, soul, rock, pop, hip-hop, and many modern vocal styles.
