Pop Music

Study pop music through hooks, choruses, production, performers, image, charts, streaming, dance, and mass audiences.

Overview

Pop music is music shaped for broad popular appeal. It often emphasizes memorable hooks, clear song forms, strong production, vocal identity, media presence, and audience connection.

This page belongs to the Genres and Traditions area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Genre pages should teach musical sound, history, community, performance practice, recordings, artists, instruments, technologies, and cultural context together.
