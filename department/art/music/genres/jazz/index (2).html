Jazz

Study jazz through improvisation, swing, blues feeling, syncopation, ensemble interaction, standards, harmony, and historical change.

Overview

Jazz is a major African American musical tradition that developed through blues, ragtime, brass band music, dance, improvisation, ensemble conversation, and changing urban musical scenes.

Improvisation

Improvisation is central to jazz. Musicians create in the moment while responding to form, harmony, rhythm, style, and other performers.

Swing and Rhythm

Jazz rhythm often uses swing feel, syncopation, groove, walking bass, comping, and flexible phrasing.

Styles

Jazz includes New Orleans jazz, swing, bebop, cool jazz, hard bop, modal jazz, free jazz, fusion, and many contemporary approaches.
