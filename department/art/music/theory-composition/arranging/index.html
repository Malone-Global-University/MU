Arranging

Learn how existing musical ideas are shaped for voices, instruments, ensembles, recordings, and performances.

Overview

Arranging is the craft of adapting or shaping music for a particular performance, ensemble, recording, or style. Arrangers decide who plays what, when, and how.

This page belongs to the Music Theory and Composition area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Theory pages should connect written concepts to listening, performance, creativity, analysis, and musical communication.
