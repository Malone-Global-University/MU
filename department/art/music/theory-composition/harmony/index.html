Harmony

Understand how chords move, support melodies, create tension and release, and shape musical direction.

Overview

Harmony studies how pitches and chords combine. It explains how music creates stability, tension, color, motion, arrival, and surprise.

This page belongs to the Music Theory and Composition area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Theory pages should connect written concepts to listening, performance, creativity, analysis, and musical communication.

Chord Progressions

Chord progressions organize harmonic motion. They can establish style, support melody, and give music a sense of direction.

Cadences

Cadences are points of rest, pause, or arrival in music. They help listeners hear phrase endings and structural boundaries.

Voice Leading

Voice leading studies how individual musical lines move from one chord to the next.
