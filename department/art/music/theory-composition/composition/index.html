Composition

Create original music by shaping rhythm, melody, harmony, texture, form, sound, notation, and musical meaning.

Overview

Composition is the creation of original music. It can involve writing notated scores, songs, instrumental works, electronic pieces, film cues, arrangements, improvisational structures, or recorded productions.

This page belongs to the Music Theory and Composition area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Theory pages should connect written concepts to listening, performance, creativity, analysis, and musical communication.

Musical Ideas

Composition begins with musical ideas such as motives, rhythms, melodies, harmonies, textures, sounds, or concepts.

Development

Composers develop ideas through repetition, variation, contrast, sequence, transformation, expansion, and recombination.

Revision

Composition often requires revision. Composers listen, test, edit, reshape, and refine musical decisions.
