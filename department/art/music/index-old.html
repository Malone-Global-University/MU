Welcome to the Music Department

The Music Department at Malone Global University explores the creative, cultural, and historical dimensions of African and African American music. Our program connects rhythm and resistance, melody and memory — revealing how music shapes identity and liberation across the diaspora.

Our Mission

The department is dedicated to preserving the ancestral sounds of Africa while embracing contemporary innovation. Through study and performance, we inspire students to use music as a tool for cultural expression, healing, and social change.

Faculty & Mentors

Our faculty includes professional musicians, ethnomusicologists, and composers who bring expertise from both traditional and modern disciplines. From drumming and choral studies to digital production and sound engineering.
