Music Promotion

Learn how artists and teams bring music to audiences through promotion, publicity, storytelling, branding, media, campaigns, and community building.

Overview

Music promotion helps audiences discover and care about music. It includes release planning, storytelling, press, social media, playlists, music videos, live shows, partnerships, community engagement, and long-term audience development.

This page belongs to the Music Business and Careers area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Business and career pages should help students connect musical knowledge with professional literacy, rights awareness, practical planning, ethical decision-making, and sustainable creative work.
