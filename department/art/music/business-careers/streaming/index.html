Streaming

Learn how streaming platforms shape music discovery, distribution, royalties, metadata, playlists, audience data, and release strategy.

Overview

Streaming is a major way listeners access music. It connects distribution, metadata, platform rules, royalties, playlists, recommendations, analytics, promotion, catalog strategy, and listener behavior.

This page belongs to the Music Business and Careers area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Business and career pages should help students connect musical knowledge with professional literacy, rights awareness, practical planning, ethical decision-making, and sustainable creative work.
