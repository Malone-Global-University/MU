Music Publishing

Learn how songs and compositions are administered, licensed, credited, registered, and monetized through music publishing.

Overview

Music publishing concerns rights in musical compositions and songs. It involves songwriters, composers, publishers, administrators, performance rights organizations, registrations, splits, licenses, and royalties.

This page belongs to the Music Business and Careers area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Business and career pages should help students connect musical knowledge with professional literacy, rights awareness, practical planning, ethical decision-making, and sustainable creative work.
