Artist Management

Learn how artist managers support career planning, strategy, releases, teams, opportunities, communication, and long-term development.

Overview

Artist management helps artists organize their professional careers. Managers may coordinate strategy, releases, opportunities, team members, communication, touring, promotion, business decisions, and long-term planning.

This page belongs to the Music Business and Careers area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Business and career pages should help students connect musical knowledge with professional literacy, rights awareness, practical planning, ethical decision-making, and sustainable creative work.

Career Strategy

Managers help artists think about goals, audience, releases, touring, collaborators, brand identity, and sustainable growth.

Team Building

An artist's team may include managers, agents, lawyers, publicists, distributors, labels, publishers, producers, accountants, and creative collaborators.

Communication

Strong management depends on clear communication, trust, planning, calendar organization, financial awareness, and professional follow-through.
