Study Notes

When studying this style, listen for the rhythm section, vocal or instrumental phrasing, harmonic language, performance setting, and relationship to earlier Black musical traditions. The strongest music-history pages should connect sound, biography, community, technology, and social context rather than treating artists as isolated names.
