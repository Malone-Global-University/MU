Remy Ma

Bronx rapper known for elite battle rap skill, lyrical precision, and Terror Squad affiliation

Biography

Remy Ma, born Reminisce Smith in the Bronx, New York, is a rapper known for her sharp battle rap skills, aggressive delivery, and commanding presence in East Coast hip-hop. She first gained recognition as part of the Terror Squad collective led by Fat Joe. Her lyrical style is marked by precision, confidence, and competitive battle-oriented writing.

She rose to prominence in the mid-2000s with standout verses and appearances that established her as one of the most respected female lyricists in hip-hop. After a period of incarceration, she returned to music and reestablished herself with new projects and collaborations. Her career includes both solo work and high-profile features that reinforce her lyrical reputation.

Remy Ma is widely regarded as one of the strongest battle rappers in modern hip-hop.

Legacy

Remy Ma’s legacy is defined by elite battle rap performance and East Coast lyrical tradition. She is widely respected as one of the most skilled female emcees in hip-hop history. Her influence continues across battle rap culture, lyrical competition, and modern East Coast rap.
