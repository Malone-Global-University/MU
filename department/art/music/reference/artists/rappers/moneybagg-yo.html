Moneybagg Yo

Memphis rapper known for melodic trap style and street-focused storytelling

Biography

Moneybagg Yo, born Demario DeWayne White Jr. in Memphis, Tennessee, is a rapper known for his melodic trap style and consistent output in modern Southern hip-hop. He emerged in the mid-2010s Memphis rap scene, gaining attention for his raw storytelling and emotional delivery. His music often reflects themes of street life, ambition, and personal growth.

He gained mainstream recognition through mixtapes before releasing commercially successful albums that established him as a major voice in trap music. Collaborations with artists like Yo Gotti helped elevate his career and connect him to a broader Southern rap network. His sound blends melodic hooks with hard-hitting trap production.

Moneybagg Yo has remained one of the most consistent charting artists in modern hip-hop, building a strong presence in both streaming and live performance circuits.

Legacy

Moneybagg Yo’s legacy is defined by modern Memphis trap and melodic street rap. He is widely recognized as one of the leading voices in current Southern hip-hop. His influence continues across trap music, streaming-era rap, and melodic street storytelling.
