Immortal Technique

Peruvian-American rapper known for political lyricism, underground influence, and socially conscious storytelling

Biography

Immortal Technique, born Felipe Andres Coronel in Lima, Peru, is a rapper known for his politically charged lyricism and uncompromising social commentary. He grew up in New York City and developed a reputation in underground hip-hop for his aggressive delivery and deeply analytical writing. His music often addresses systemic inequality, global politics, and historical injustice.

He built his career independently, gaining recognition through mixtapes and grassroots distribution rather than mainstream industry support. His breakout work established him as a leading voice in conscious rap, with a strong following in underground hip-hop communities worldwide. He is also known for speaking engagements and activism outside of music.

Immortal Technique is widely regarded as one of the most politically intense voices in hip-hop.

Legacy

Immortal Technique’s legacy is defined by political hip-hop activism and independent artistry. He is widely respected as one of the most influential underground conscious rappers of his era. His influence continues across political rap, activist hip-hop culture, and independent music movements.
