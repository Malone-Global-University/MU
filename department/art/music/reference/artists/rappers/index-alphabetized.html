List of American Rap and Hip-Hop Artists

A reference directory of major American rap and hip-hop artists, organized alphabetically. Each name links to a biography page.

Overview

American artists shaped rap and hip-hop from its earliest roots in block parties, DJ culture, MCing, spoken-word traditions, funk, soul, disco, gospel, reggae sound systems, street dance, visual art, and urban youth culture.

This page is organized alphabetically for quick reference. Some artists appear in more than one era, region, or style, but each name is listed once here for navigation.
