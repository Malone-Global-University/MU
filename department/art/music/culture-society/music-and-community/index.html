Music and Community

Study how music builds, reflects, remembers, and organizes communities.

Overview

Music and community studies how people make music together and how music supports shared life. Music can gather people, teach values, preserve memory, organize action, mark events, and create belonging.

This page belongs to the Music, Culture, and Society area of the Malone Global University Music Department. These pages should connect musical sound to people, communities, histories, institutions, media systems, memory, identity, public life, and social meaning.
