Beatmaking

Learn how beats are built from rhythm, drums, samples, bass, loops, groove, sequencing, sound choice, and arrangement.

Overview

Beatmaking is the creation of instrumental tracks, grooves, or rhythmic foundations for songs, rap, R&B, pop, electronic music, dance music, and other styles.

This page belongs to the Music Technology and Production area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Technology pages should connect sound, creativity, tools, workflows, production decisions, listening, distribution, and musical meaning.

Samples and Sounds

Beatmakers choose and shape drums, samples, synths, basses, textures, loops, and effects to create a distinctive sound.

Arrangement

A beat may include intro, verse, chorus, bridge, breakdown, drops, fills, transitions, and space for vocals.
