Mixing

Learn how mixing shapes balance, space, tone, dynamics, effects, clarity, emotion, and listener focus.

Overview

Mixing combines recorded and produced tracks into a coherent listening experience. The mix controls volume, panning, EQ, compression, reverb, delay, automation, effects, and musical focus.

This page belongs to the Music Technology and Production area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Technology pages should connect sound, creativity, tools, workflows, production decisions, listening, distribution, and musical meaning.
