Strings

Study bowed and plucked string instruments through tone, bowing, fingering, intonation, ensemble playing, and expressive technique.

Overview

String performance includes instruments such as violin, viola, cello, double bass, harp, guitar, and many global string instruments. This page focuses especially on string-family technique and ensemble practice.

This page belongs to the Performance Studies area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Performance pages should connect musical knowledge to physical skill, listening, interpretation, collaboration, rehearsal, and expressive communication.
