Guitar

Study guitar as a fretted string instrument for chords, riffs, melody, rhythm, accompaniment, improvisation, and amplified sound.

Overview

Guitar performance appears across classical, folk, blues, jazz, rock, country, pop, gospel, funk, R&B, reggae, and many global traditions. Guitarists use fingers, picks, frets, strings, tone, rhythm, and amplification to shape sound.

This page belongs to the Performance Studies area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Performance pages should connect musical knowledge to physical skill, listening, interpretation, collaboration, rehearsal, and expressive communication.
