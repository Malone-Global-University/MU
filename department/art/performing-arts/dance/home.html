About the Department

The Dance & Movement Department at Malone Global University examines dance as a vital medium of cultural expression, education, and empowerment. Students explore African and diasporic dance traditions, contemporary choreography, and the role of movement in storytelling, ritual, and performance. Training emphasizes technique, creativity, and interdisciplinary collaboration.
