About the Department

The Theater & Drama Department at Malone Global University studies the power of performance to educate, challenge, and liberate. Students explore African and diasporic theater traditions, contemporary performance practices, and the role of drama in society. The program emphasizes acting, directing, playwriting, and stagecraft, fostering both critical and creative skills.
