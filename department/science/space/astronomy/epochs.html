Era Epoch Time after Big Bang Major Events / Physical Processes
Radiation Era Planck Epoch 0 – 10⁻⁴³ s Quantum gravity dominates; laws of physics not yet unified.
Grand Unified Epoch 10⁻⁴³ – 10⁻³⁶ s Strong force separates from electroweak force.
Inflationary Epoch 10⁻³⁶ – 10⁻³² s Universe undergoes exponential expansion; seeds of structure formed.
Electroweak Epoch 10⁻³² – 10⁻¹² s Electromagnetic and weak forces separate.
Quark Epoch 10⁻¹² – 10⁻⁶ s Universe filled with quarks, leptons, and gauge bosons.
Hadron Epoch 10⁻⁶ – 1 s Quarks combine to form protons and neutrons.
Lepton Epoch 1 – 10 s Leptons dominate; annihilations of particle-antiparticle pairs occur.
Nuclear (Nucleosynthesis) Epoch 3 – 20 min Formation of light nuclei (H, He, Li).
Matter Era Atomic Epoch ~380,000 years Recombination occurs; neutral atoms form; Cosmic Microwave Background released.
Galactic Epoch ~200 million years First galaxies form; large-scale structure begins.
Stellar Epoch ~400 million years onward First stars form; reionization begins; galaxies evolve.
Dark Energy Era Present Epoch ~9.8 billion years onward Dark energy dominates expansion; accelerated expansion; universe structure develops.
Future Epochs Future Possible fates include continued expansion, heat death, or other scenarios depending on cosmological models.
