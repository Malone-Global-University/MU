Biology

The Biology Division examines life as an integrated system—from molecular and cellular mechanisms to whole organisms and ecological interactions. Students study the structure, function, growth, adaptation, and reproduction of living systems while developing a scientific understanding of how biological processes shape organisms and environments.

Home Department Science Biology Division

  • Animal

    Study animal life through anatomy, physiology, behavior, development, and evolutionary adaptation. This track explores how animal organisms function, survive, and interact within broader biological systems.

  • Cellular

    Examine the molecular and cellular foundations of life, including cell structure, genetics, metabolism, signaling, and biochemical processes that govern living organisms at the microscopic level.

  • Plant

    Explore plant biology through the study of structure, growth, reproduction, physiology, and ecological function. Students learn how plant systems sustain ecosystems and support life across the planet.
