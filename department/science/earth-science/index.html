Earth Science

The Earth Science Division studies the physical systems that shape the planet, including its structure, atmosphere, oceans, climate, and environmental processes. Students examine how geological, atmospheric, and ecological systems interact to form the dynamic world in which human civilization operates.

  • Geology

    Study the composition, structure, and history of the Earth through rocks, minerals, tectonic processes, landforms, and geologic time.

  • Meteorology

    Examine atmospheric systems, weather formation, storm dynamics, and climate behavior to understand the forces governing Earth’s atmosphere.

  • Oceanography

    Explore the physical, chemical, and biological systems of Earth’s oceans and their role in climate, ecosystems, and planetary regulation.

  • Environmental Science

    Analyze the interactions between natural systems and human activity, including sustainability, resource management, and environmental impact.
