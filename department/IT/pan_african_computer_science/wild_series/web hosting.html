What is Web Hosting?

Web hosting is like renting space for your website to live on the internet. A hosting company stores your files on their computers (servers) so that anyone in the world can visit your site at any time.

Easy Analogy: Website = House

  • The House → Your website files (HTML, images, text)
  • The Land → Web hosting (the place where your site lives)
  • The Address → Your domain name (e.g. maloneuniversity.org)
Your Computer --(upload)--> Hosting Server <--(visit)-- Visitor's Browser

Beginner-Friendly Hosting Services

  • Netlify – free & easy for static sites
  • GitHub Pages – free, great for code-based projects
  • Neocities – simple for personal pages
