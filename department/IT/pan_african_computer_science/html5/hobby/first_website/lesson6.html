Lesson 2: HTML Text Elements

Headings

HTML has six heading levels: <h1> through <h6>.

<h1>Main Title</h1>
<h2>Section</h2>
<h3>Subsection</h3>

Paragraphs & Line Breaks

<p>This is a paragraph.</p>
<p>Another paragraph.</p>
<p>Line one.<br>Line two.</p>

Emphasis

<strong>Bold text</strong>
<em>Italic text</em>
