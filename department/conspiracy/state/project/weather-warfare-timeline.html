A timline of weather warfare related events.

Overview

Weather modification has a long, complex, and often secretive history. While some programs are confirmed historical operations and others remain speculative or unverified, together they form a timeline that documents the evolution of weather control, geoengineering, and military meteorology from early cloud‑seeding experiments to modern strategic concerns. This page provides a chronological overview of major milestones, both officially documented and conspiratorially claimed, to help students understand how weather manipulation entered scientific research, military doctrine, and global geopolitical debates.

Weather Warfare Timeline (1900s–Present)

Early Concepts & First Experiments (1900–1945)

<1900s–1930s — Early Theories of Artificial Rain Inventors, scientists, and aviation pioneers propose methods to force rainfall using explosions, electrical charges, and atmospheric dust. 1940s — Birth of Cloud Seeding Scientists discover that silver iodide can trigger ice formation in clouds. Cloud‑seeding experiments begin in academic settings. The Dawn of Militarized Weather Modification (1946–1959) 1946 — Project Cirrus First major U.S. cloud‑seeding experiment (General Electric + U.S. Army + Navy). A hurricane is seeded; it unexpectedly changes direction, sparking controversy and fueling later conspiracy narratives. 1950s — Cold War Research Expands Both the U.S. and the USSR investigate atmospheric manipulation. Weather control is viewed as a potential strategic weapon. 1958 — President Eisenhower Briefed on Weather Control Possibilities Documents reveal government interest in artificial rainfall, fog dissipation, and hurricane steering. Weather Control Goes Mainstream (1960–1966) 1960 — Establishment of NASA Satellites begin providing global weather data, enabling the first real-time analysis useful for large-scale modification attempts. 1960s — Cloud Seeding Becomes Widespread Over 40 U.S. states and numerous foreign governments attempt weather modification for: Rain enhancement Hail suppression Fog clearing 1966 — “Interdepartmental Committee for Atmospheric Sciences” Report U.S. government outlines long-term goals for weather modification research, including: Storm steering Climate modification Large-scale precipitation control This report becomes a major item cited in conspiracy analyses. The Era of Confirmed Weather Warfare (1967–1972) 1967–1972 — Operation Popeye (Vietnam War) Verified U.S. military weather warfare operation. Cloud seeding used to extend monsoon season, flood enemy supply routes. Mission motto: “Make mud, not war.” Program kept classified until 1974. 1969–1971 — Project Stormfury U.S. attempts to weaken hurricanes through cloud seeding. Results largely inconclusive, but demonstrates serious government interest in storm manipulation. International Response (1973–1979) 1972 — Popeye Exposure Jack Anderson’s reporting reveals the secret operation. 1977 — ENMOD Treaty UN Convention on the Prohibition of Military or Any Other Hostile Use of Environmental Modification Techniques Bans weather modification for warfare. Acknowledges potential for harmful manipulation of: Climate Oceans Atmosphere Weather patterns The treaty itself becomes major evidence cited in conspiracy theories because it implicitly confirms the possibility of environmental manipulation. The Modern Era of Weather Control Research (1980–1995) 1980s — U.S. & USSR Continue Research Although officially non-military, cloud seeding and atmospheric manipulation experiments continue globally. 1990 — Weather Satellites Improve New technology allows ultra-precise monitoring of storms, making hypothetical steering more feasible. 1993 — Project Stormfury Ends Determined to be scientifically ineffective, though still historically significant. Rise of Conspiracy Interpretations (1996–2005) 1996 — “Owning the Weather in 2025” Published U.S. Air Force think‑piece describes hypothetical future battlefield weather control. Though not a program, it becomes one of the most frequently cited weather warfare documents online. Late 1990s — Chemtrails Enter Popular Culture Claims emerge that unidentified jets release chemical aerosols for weather manipulation. 1999–2001 — HAARP Gains Attention High‑frequency research facility becomes center of theories about: Weather manipulation Earthquake triggering Ionospheric heating weapons Large‑Scale Geoengineering Research (2006–Present) 2006 — Geoengineering Re-Emerges Publicly Scientists begin discussing climate intervention technologies openly: Stratospheric Aerosol Injection (SAI) Marine Cloud Brightening Solar Radiation Management (SRM) 2008 — China Deploys Cloud Seeding for Olympics One of the largest weather modification operations ever conducted. 2010s — Expansion of National Weather Programs Many countries operate cloud-seeding programs: UAE China United States (state-level) India Russia 2015–Present — Climate Engineering Becomes Mainstream Science Weather modification research shifts toward: Climate mitigation Wildfire management Drought relief Solar radiation control Present — Ongoing Debates The intersection of: confirmed historical operations modern climate engineering research military weather documents …keeps weather warfare theories active.
