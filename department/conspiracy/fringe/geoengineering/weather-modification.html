Cloud seeding is the most widespread form of weather modification. It involves dispersing substances such as:

Military Weather Modification

Weather has always been a strategic military factor. Documents show that the U.S., USSR, and later China explored weather manipulation as a tactical advantage.

Countries can still use weather modification for peaceful purposes, but not to harm other nations.

Why Weather Modification Sparks Controversy

Weather modification sits at the crossroads of proven science, military secrecy, and climate politics. Public distrust stems from several factors:

  • Lack of transparency over military research budgets
  • Visible cloud seeding aircraft and particulate releases

Malone University Approach

Our goal is to help students distinguish between verified atmospheric technologies and speculative or unproven claims.
