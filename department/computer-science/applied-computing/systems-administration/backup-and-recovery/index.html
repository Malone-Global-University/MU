Backup and Recovery

Course Overview

Backup and Recovery teaches students how organizations protect digital systems and data against loss, corruption, failure, and disaster. Learners develop practical competency in backup strategies, retention policies, disaster recovery planning, restoration workflows, recovery testing, redundancy design, and business continuity fundamentals.

Backup and Recovery Topics

  • Backup and Recovery Overview

    Introduction to why backup and recovery systems are critical for operational resilience and data protection.

  • Backup Types and Strategies

    Instruction in full, incremental, differential, snapshot, and hybrid backup methodologies.

  • Retention and Versioning

    Practical guidance on retention policies, version control, archival planning, and data lifecycle management.

  • Disaster Recovery Planning

    Training in designing recovery plans for major failures, outages, corruption, and catastrophic system events.

  • Restore Testing and Validation

    Instruction in verifying backup integrity and regularly testing restoration procedures for operational readiness.

  • Redundancy and High Availability

    Guidance on designing systems for resilience through redundancy, failover, replication, and availability planning.

  • Backup Best Practices

    Best practices for building secure, reliable, and maintainable backup and recovery operations.
