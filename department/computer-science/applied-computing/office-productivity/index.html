Branch Overview

The Office Productivity branch develops practical workplace computing skills for learners who must create, organize, communicate, and manage information in professional environments. Students learn to use productivity software, collaboration platforms, administrative tools, and workflow systems to execute business, academic, and organizational tasks efficiently and professionally.

Office Productivity Courses

  • Spreadsheets

    Instruction in spreadsheet software for organizing, calculating, analyzing, and presenting structured data. Topics include formulas, functions, formatting, tables, charts, sorting, filtering, and workbook management.

  • Documents and Formatting

    Practical instruction in professional document creation, editing, formatting, layout, and publishing. Students learn how to produce clean, structured written materials suitable for academic, administrative, and business use.

  • Presentations

    Training in designing and delivering effective slide decks and presentation materials. Students learn visual communication, slide design, presentation software operation, and professional presentation workflow practices.

  • Email and Calendar

    Instruction in professional communication and scheduling systems, including inbox management, calendar organization, meeting coordination, appointment scheduling, and workplace communication best practices.

  • Workflow Automation

    Practical methods for reducing repetitive office work through templates, rules, integrations, automation tools, and workflow optimization. Students learn how to improve efficiency in routine digital business processes.
