Overview

The Cyber Security Department at Malone University prepares students to defend digital systems, analyze threats, test security weaknesses, and detect malicious activity across modern networks and computing environments.

This program combines information security theory, hands-on labs, and real-world incident response exercises following modern frameworks such as NIST, MITRE ATT&CK, and zero-trust architecture.
