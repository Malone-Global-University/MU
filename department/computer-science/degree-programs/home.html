Overview

The Information Technology Department at Malone Global University offers a suite of structured degree pathways designed to prepare students for advanced technical careers. Each program blends theoretical foundations, hands-on laboratories, research, and professional preparation. Graduates emerge with practical expertise and industry-recognized competencies aligned to global workforce demands.

Available Degree Tracks

  • Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity & Network Administration (B.S.)

    Focused on enterprise defense, threat analysis, digital forensics, and network architecture. Ideal for students pursuing careers in SOC operations, network engineering, and security infrastructure management.

  • Bachelor of Science in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence (B.S.)

    A mathematically intensive program preparing learners for careers in predictive analytics, machine learning, AI systems engineering, and large-scale data engineering.

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology & Systems (B.S.)

    A comprehensive systems-focused degree covering enterprise IT infrastructure, cloud operations, virtualization, and business information systems.

  • Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering & Fullstack Development (B.S.)

    Emphasizes front-end, back-end, and systems architecture competencies. Students prepare for real-world application development roles, DevOps workflows, and scalable systems engineering.

  • Associate of Science in Computer & Information Technology (A.S.)

    A flexible entry-level program designed to introduce core technical knowledge, applicable across networking, databases, cybersecurity, and software fundamentals.
