Course Overview

Cybersecurity Theory studies the conceptual foundations of secure systems, trust, risk, authentication, cryptography, attack surfaces, and defensive design. Learners examine how systems fail, how threats are modeled, how trust is established, and how secure architecture reduces exposure.
