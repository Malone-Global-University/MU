Course Overview

Data Science Theory provides the conceptual foundation for reasoning with data, models, uncertainty, inference, experimentation, and interpretation. Learners study probability, statistics, regression, methodology, experimental design, bias, and ethical data use as the theoretical basis for responsible analytical work.
