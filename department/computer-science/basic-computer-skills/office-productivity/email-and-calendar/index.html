Course Overview

Email and Calendar teaches students how to manage digital communication and scheduling systems effectively in professional environments. Learners develop practical competency in inbox organization, email etiquette, scheduling workflows, calendar coordination, meeting management, and digital communication best practices for workplace and academic operations.

Email and Calendar Topics

  • Email Platform Basics

    Introduction to modern email interfaces, inbox structures, folders, labels, settings, and the basic operation of email platforms.

  • Professional Email Etiquette

    Best practices for writing clear, respectful, effective, and professionally appropriate email communication.

  • Inbox Organization and Management

    Practical instruction in sorting, labeling, filtering, archiving, prioritizing, and maintaining manageable inbox workflows.

  • Calendar Platform Basics

    Introduction to digital calendar systems, event creation, recurring appointments, reminders, shared calendars, and scheduling interfaces.

  • Meeting Coordination and Scheduling

    Instruction in organizing meetings, inviting attendees, checking availability, coordinating schedules, and managing event logistics.

  • Notifications, Reminders, and Follow-Ups

    Training in using alerts, reminders, follow-up systems, and notification management to maintain responsiveness and scheduling discipline.

  • Digital Communication Workflow

    Guidance on integrating email and calendar systems into broader professional productivity and communication workflows.
