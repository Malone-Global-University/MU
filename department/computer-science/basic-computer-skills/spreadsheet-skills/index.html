Course Overview

Spreadsheet Skills teaches practical, nontechnical computer skills for everyday use. Learners build confidence through clear explanations, simple workflows, common examples, and repeated practice designed for beginners, office workers, returning learners, small business users, and anyone who needs reliable computer literacy.
