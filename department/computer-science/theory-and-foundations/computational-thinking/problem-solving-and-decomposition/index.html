Course Overview

Problem Solving and Decomposition introduces students to a focused theoretical area within Computational Thinking. Learners examine the major concepts, vocabulary, structures, reasoning patterns, and design implications associated with this topic so they can connect abstract computer science principles to later applied coursework, labs, and professional technical practice.
