Course Overview

Artificial Intelligence Foundations introduces students to the theoretical structure of intelligent systems, search, optimization, machine learning, neural networks, symbolic reasoning, and AI system design. Learners study the foundational ideas that support modern AI tools while distinguishing conceptual understanding from tool usage.
