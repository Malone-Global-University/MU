Division Overview

The Research and Experimental Division serves as the department’s exploratory environment for prototyping, experimentation, unconventional technical inquiry, and emerging technology development. This division supports iterative design, speculative systems work, proof-of-concept engineering, and advanced technical investigation beyond the boundaries of standard curriculum pathways.

Research and Experimental Categories

  • Prototype Labs

    Structured environments for designing, building, and testing early-stage technical concepts, proof-of-concept systems, and prototype implementations. Prototype labs emphasize rapid iteration, validation, and engineering experimentation.

  • Experimental Lab

    An open exploratory workspace dedicated to unconventional technical experiments, interdisciplinary innovation, and nonstandard development initiatives. The Experimental Lab supports creative risk-taking, emerging technology exploration, and technical discovery.

  • Archived Tests

    Historical records of previous experiments, deprecated prototypes, retired technical trials, and concluded developmental initiatives. This archive preserves institutional experimentation history for reference, review, and future analysis.
