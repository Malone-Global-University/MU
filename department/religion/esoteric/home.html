Esoteric Traditions

Explore mysticism, occult practices, alchemy, and spiritual symbolism across cultures and historical periods.

Home Department Religion Esoteric Traditions

Course Overview

This course introduces students to esoteric and mystical traditions, including symbolic systems, ritual practices, alchemy, divination, and the study of hidden knowledge across cultures. Emphasis is placed on historical, cultural, and philosophical contexts.

Topics Covered

  • Western Esotericism

    Study Hermeticism, Kabbalah, Rosicrucianism, and European mystical traditions.

  • Alchemy & Mysticism

    Explore alchemical symbolism, transformation rituals, and philosophical mysticism.

  • Occult Practices

    Examine ceremonial magic, divination, astrology, and spiritual disciplines.

  • Comparative Esotericism

    Analyze similarities and differences between esoteric traditions across cultures.

  • Modern Influence

    Understand the impact of esoteric thought on contemporary spirituality, literature, and art.
