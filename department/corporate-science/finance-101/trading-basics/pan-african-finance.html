💰 Pan-African School – Finance Curriculum Tree

The Finance Department provides a rigorous education in monetary systems, banking structures, investment strategy, and financial sovereignty. Students learn to build and preserve wealth over generations through intelligent capital deployment. Topics include banking, asset classes, risk, portfolio theory, capital markets, and dynastic financial planning.

Level 1 – Foundations
  • Introduction to Finance – basic concepts, financial systems, role of finance in the economy
  • Principles of Economics – microeconomics & macroeconomics essentials
  • Mathematics for Finance – time value of money, basic algebra, percentages
  • Accounting Fundamentals – financial statements, double-entry bookkeeping
  • Business Communication – professional writing, presentations, workplace etiquette
Level 2 – Core Finance
  • Corporate Finance I – capital structure, cost of capital, dividend policy
  • Investment Principles – risk & return, portfolio basics, asset classes
  • Financial Markets & Institutions – stock markets, bond markets, banking systems
  • Managerial Accounting – budgeting, cost analysis, performance measurement
  • Financial Modeling – spreadsheets, cash flow projections, scenario analysis
  • Statistics for Finance – descriptive statistics, probability, regression analysis
Level 3 – Applied & Specialized
  • Corporate Finance II – mergers & acquisitions, leveraged buyouts, restructuring
  • Advanced Investments – derivatives, fixed income, alternative investments
  • Banking & Financial Services – retail, corporate, and investment banking operations
  • International Finance – exchange rates, trade finance, global markets
  • Risk Management – identifying, measuring, and mitigating financial risks
  • Public Finance – government revenue, expenditure, debt management
Level 4 – Advanced / Elective Specializations
  • Financial Engineering – structured products, quantitative strategies
  • Wealth Management – financial planning, retirement, estate planning
  • Behavioral Finance – psychology of investing, market anomalies
  • Fintech & Digital Finance – blockchain, cryptocurrencies, payment systems
  • Private Equity & Venture Capital – funding strategies, deal structuring
  • Advanced Risk Modeling – credit risk, market risk, operational risk models
  • Ethics in Finance – corporate governance, compliance, fiduciary duty
Practical Training & Projects
  • Financial Statement Analysis – real company case studies
  • Portfolio Management Simulation – stock and bond portfolio creation
  • Corporate Valuation Projects – DCF, comparable analysis, precedent transactions
  • Internship / Industry Placement – hands-on experience in finance roles
  • Capstone Project – comprehensive financial strategy for a real or simulated business
Electives & Cross-Disciplinary Options
  • Entrepreneurial Finance – startup funding, scaling strategies
  • Development Finance – financing infrastructure and social projects
  • Sustainable & Green Finance – ESG investing, climate finance
  • Data Science for Finance – machine learning, big data applications
  • Negotiation & Deal-Making – business negotiation strategies
