Finance Department

Build expertise in financial analysis, investing, corporate finance, and personal wealth management to succeed in the financial world.

Home Department Corporate Science Finance

Department Units

  • Unit 1: Financial Statements

    Understand income statements, balance sheets, cash flow statements, and financial ratios.

  • Unit 2: Time Value of Money

    Learn present value, future value, compound interest, annuities, and discounted cash flows.

  • Unit 3: Investments

    Explore stocks, bonds, mutual funds, ETFs, and risk versus return.

  • Unit 4: Corporate Finance

    Study capital budgeting, cost of capital, debt versus equity, and dividend policies.

  • Unit 5: Personal Finance

    Learn budgeting, credit management, retirement planning, and long-term wealth strategies.
