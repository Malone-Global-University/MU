Finance Department

Build expertise in financial analysis, investing, corporate finance, and personal wealth management to succeed in the financial world.

Home Departments Finance

Department Units

Unit 1: Financial Statements – Understand income statements, balance sheets, cash flow statements, and financial ratios.

Unit 2: Time Value of Money – Learn present value, future value, compound interest, annuities, and discounted cash flows.

Unit 3: Investments – Explore stocks, bonds, mutual funds, ETFs, and risk vs return.

Unit 4: Corporate Finance – Study capital budgeting, cost of capital, debt vs equity, and dividend policies.

Unit 5: Personal Finance – Learn budgeting, credit, retirement planning, and wealth-building strategies.
