Unit 2: Microeconomics

Understand how markets function through supply, demand, pricing, and competition.

Home Department Corporate Science Economics-101 Unit-2

Unit Overview

This unit explains how individual markets operate and how prices coordinate economic activity.

Learning Objectives

  • Explain how supply and demand determine prices.
  • Measure elasticity and interpret responsiveness.
  • Understand consumer decision-making.
  • Identify different market structures.

Lessons in This Unit

  • Lesson 2.1: Demand

    Law of demand and shifts.

  • Lesson 2.2: Supply

    Law of supply and production decisions.

  • Lesson 2.3: Market Equilibrium

    Price formation and adjustment.

  • Lesson 2.4: Elasticity

    Responsiveness of buyers and sellers.

  • Lesson 2.5: Consumer Behavior

    Utility and choice.

  • Lesson 2.6: Costs & Production

    Firm decision-making.

  • Lesson 2.7: Perfect Competition

    Competitive market outcomes.

  • Lesson 2.8: Monopoly & Oligopoly

    Market power and strategic behavior.
