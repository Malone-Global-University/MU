Unit 5: Global Economics

Understand international trade, currency markets, and global development.

Home Department Corporate Science Economics-101 Unit-5

Unit Overview

This unit examines how countries interact through trade and financial markets, and how global forces influence economic growth and development.

Learning Objectives

  • Explain comparative advantage and gains from trade.
  • Analyze the impact of trade policies such as tariffs and quotas.
  • Understand how exchange rates are determined.
  • Evaluate global capital flows and financial systems.
  • Identify key factors influencing economic development.

Lessons in This Unit

  • Lesson 5.1: Comparative Advantage

    Why nations specialize and trade.

  • Lesson 5.2: Trade Policy

    Tariffs, quotas, and protectionism.

  • Lesson 5.3: Exchange Rates

    Currency markets and exchange rate systems.

  • Lesson 5.4: Global Financial Flows

    International investment and capital mobility.

  • Lesson 5.5: Economic Development

    Growth challenges in developing economies.
