Definition

Capital Resources are the physical, financial, and human resources used to produce goods and services. This includes machinery, tools, buildings, money, and skilled labor that help create economic output.

Why It Matters

Capital resources are essential for production and economic growth. They allow businesses to increase efficiency, expand output, and innovate. Proper management of capital resources ensures sustainability and competitiveness in the market.

Example

A factory uses machinery (physical resources), investment funds (financial resources), and trained workers (human resources) to produce cars. The combination of these resources enables efficient production and supports the company's growth.
