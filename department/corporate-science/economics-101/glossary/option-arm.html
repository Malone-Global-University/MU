Option ARM (Pay Option ARM)

Economics Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Corporate Science Economics Glossary Option ARM (Pay Option ARM)

Definition

An adjustable-rate mortgage allowing multiple payment choices, including minimum payments that may not cover interest, causing loan balance growth.

Why It Matters

These loans contributed to housing-bubble risks due to negative amortization.

Example

A borrower makes the minimum payment and the unpaid interest is added to the principal.

Related Terms

  • Mortgage
  • Negative amortization
  • Housing bubble
