Technological Advance

Economics Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Corporate Science Economics Glossary Technological Advance

Definition

The development or improvement of tools, systems, or processes that enhance efficiency, productivity, or capabilities.

Why It Matters

Technological advances drive economic growth, competitiveness, and innovation across industries.

Example

The invention of the internet was a major technological advance that transformed global communication and commerce.
