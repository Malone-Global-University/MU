Office Of The Comptroller Of The Currency (OCC)

Economics Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Corporate Science Economics Glossary Office Of The Comptroller Of The Currency (OCC)

Definition

U.S. federal agency that regulates and supervises national banks (Office of the Comptroller of the Currency).

Why It Matters

Ensures safety, soundness, and compliance of the national banking system.

Example

A national bank must meet OCC capital requirements.
