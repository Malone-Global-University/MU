Underemployment

Economics Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Corporate Science Economics Glossary Underemployment

Definition

Underemployment is the broader condition where workers are employed in ways that do not match their skills or desired hours, including involuntary part-time work and jobs below qualification level.

Why It Matters

It provides a fuller picture of labor market health than unemployment alone and helps explain stagnant wages and weak household finances.

Example

Many workers want full-time jobs but can only find part-time roles, raising underemployment.

Related Terms

  • Involuntary part Time
  • Skills mismatch
  • Labor force
  • Wages
