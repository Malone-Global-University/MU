Money Multiplier

Economics Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Corporate Science Economics Glossary Money Multiplier

Definition

The ratio showing how an initial deposit in the banking system can lead to a larger total increase in the money supply through repeated lending.

Why It Matters

The money multiplier explains how banking systems amplify central bank reserves into broader credit expansion.

Example

A $1,000 deposit may ultimately support several thousand dollars of total deposits after multiple rounds of lending.
