Stimulus Packages

Economics Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Corporate Science Economics Glossary Stimulus Packages

Definition

Stimulus packages are sets of coordinated government measures—such as direct payments, expanded benefits, tax relief, and public spending—designed to increase economic activity during downturns.

Why It Matters

They can speed recovery, support households, and prevent deeper recessions, but may increase budget deficits or inflation pressures depending on conditions.

Example

A package includes unemployment expansions and funding for public projects to raise demand.

Related Terms

  • Fiscal stimulus
  • Government spending
  • Tax cuts
  • Economic recovery
