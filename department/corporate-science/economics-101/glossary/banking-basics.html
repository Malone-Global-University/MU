Definition

Banking Basics covers the core concepts of banking, including how banks accept deposits, provide loans, manage money, and facilitate payments in the economy.

Why It Matters

Understanding banking is essential for personal finance, business operations, and economic policy. Banks provide a secure place for savings, offer credit to businesses and individuals, and help maintain economic stability.

Example

When you deposit money in a savings account, the bank may lend a portion to a business seeking a loan. The bank earns interest, you earn interest on your deposit, and the economy benefits from increased investment and economic activity.
