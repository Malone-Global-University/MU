Economics Department

Explore microeconomics, macroeconomics, monetary and fiscal policy, and global economics to understand how resources are allocated worldwide.

Home Department Corporate Science Economics

Department Units

  • Unit 1: Economic Foundations

    Learn scarcity, opportunity cost, production possibilities, and incentives.

  • Unit 2: Microeconomics

    Study supply and demand, elasticity, consumer behavior, and market structures.

  • Unit 3: Macroeconomics

    Understand GDP, inflation, unemployment, and business cycles.

  • Unit 4: Monetary & Fiscal Policy

    Explore central banking, interest rates, government spending, and taxation.

  • Unit 5: Global Economics

    Learn trade theory, exchange rates, comparative advantage, and development economics.
