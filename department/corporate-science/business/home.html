Develop expertise in entrepreneurship, management, operations, and strategic leadership within Corporate Science.

Department Units

  • Unit 1: Introduction to Business

    Learn what business is, types of organizations, entrepreneurship, and nonprofit vs for-profit models.

  • Unit 2: Management & Leadership

    Explore planning, organizing, leading, decision-making, and organizational structures.

  • Unit 3: Marketing Fundamentals

    Understand supply and demand, market research, branding, pricing, and positioning.

  • Unit 4: Operations & Strategy

    Study production systems, quality control, competitive advantage, and business models.

  • Unit 5: Business Ethics & Law

    Learn about contracts, corporate responsibility, ethical decision frameworks, and regulations.
