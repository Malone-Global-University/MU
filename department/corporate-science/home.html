Corporate Science Department

Our three core tracks of Economics, Business, and Finance deliver a deep understanding of markets, organizations, and financial strategy.

Home Departments Corporate Science

Corporate Science Tracks

  • Economics Track

    A rigorous program exploring microeconomics, macroeconomics, and global economic systems. Students develop analytical skills to understand market behaviors, monetary and fiscal policies, and societal resource allocation. The curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, data interpretation, and economic modeling for practical decision-making.

  • Business Track

    Focused on management, marketing, operations, and entrepreneurship, this program prepares students to lead organizations effectively. Coursework emphasizes organizational strategy, operational efficiency, market research, innovation, and competitive positioning, equipping students with the skills to drive growth and innovation in diverse business environments.

  • Finance Track

    An applied curriculum covering corporate finance, investments, portfolio management, and securities trading. Learners gain practical experience in capital management, risk assessment, financial analysis, and strategic asset allocation. The track integrates real-world simulations to prepare students for roles in banking, investment, and corporate finance.
