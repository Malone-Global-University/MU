Political Revolutions

Trace the political upheavals that redefined sovereignty, liberty, and governance from the 18th to the 20th century.

Overview

Political revolutions marked profound shifts in human governance, driven by demands for liberty, equality, and national identity. These movements redefined global political frameworks and inspired generations of reform.

Key Themes

  • The American Revolution and constitutional democracy
  • The French Revolution and Enlightenment ideals
  • Latin American independence movements
  • The spread of nationalism and liberalism
  • Revolutions of 1848 and their global influence
