Decolonization & Global Change

Analyze the collapse of empires, emergence of independent states, and global realignment of political and economic power.

Overview

Decolonization defined the mid-20th century, as empires dissolved and former colonies asserted sovereignty. The process restructured international relations and redefined global economic dynamics.

Key Themes

  • Post-war independence movements in Africa and Asia
  • Role of ideology and Cold War influence
  • Non-Aligned Movement and global diplomacy
  • Economic dependency and neo-colonialism
  • Cultural identity and nation-building
