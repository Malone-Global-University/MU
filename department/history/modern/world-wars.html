World Wars I & II

Examine the causes, conduct, and consequences of the twentieth century’s defining global conflicts.

Overview

The World Wars reshaped geopolitics, economies, and societies. They accelerated technological innovation and spurred the creation of new international systems of peace and reconstruction.

Key Themes

  • Militarization and alliances
  • Trench warfare and mechanized combat
  • Total war and civilian mobilization
  • Post-war reconstruction and the League of Nations
  • Rise of the United Nations and Cold War geopolitics
