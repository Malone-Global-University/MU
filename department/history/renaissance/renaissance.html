Key Themes

Humanism

Intellectual movement emphasizing classical learning, individual potential, and human-centered subjects.

Art and Architecture

Artists like Leonardo da Vinci, Raphael, and Michelangelo advanced realism, perspective, and classical inspiration in their work.

Science and Innovation

New discoveries in astronomy, anatomy, and physics challenged traditional beliefs and laid the foundation for modern science.

Literature and Philosophy

Writers such as Machiavelli, Petrarch, and Erasmus explored politics, ethics, and human experience in new ways.
