Origins

Adab emerged in southern Mesopotamia around 2500 BCE, benefiting from fertile land and its location between major Sumerian centers like Kish and Lagash.

Rise and Influence

Adab became a center for religious worship and local administration. Its strategic position allowed participation in trade networks linking Sumerian city-states.
