Comparison: Amorites vs. Assyrians

Although the Amorites once ruled Assyria under Shamshi-Adad I, they were not the same people. The Amorites were western Semitic tribes who migrated into Mesopotamia, while the Assyrians were eastern Semitic natives centered on the city of Assur. Their cultures blended over time, but their origins and identities remained distinct.
