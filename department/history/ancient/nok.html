Nok Civilization

One of the earliest known West African civilizations, noted for ironworking and terracotta artistry.

Introduction

The Nok civilization existed in what is now Nigeria, beginning around 1000 BCE. It is renowned for its sophisticated terracotta sculptures and early iron technology, laying the foundations for later West African societies.

Timeline

  • c. 1000 BCE – Nok culture begins
  • c. 500 BCE – Nok ironworking techniques spread
  • c. 300 CE – Nok civilization declines

Culture and Achievements

The Nok people created life-like terracotta sculptures, practiced early iron smelting, cultivated crops, and developed trade networks across West Africa.
