Early Asian Civilizations

From the Indus Valley to Shang China and Vedic India, early Asian civilizations laid the foundations for culture, trade, and technology in the region.

Introduction

Early Asian civilizations developed along major rivers, creating urban centers, writing systems, and cultural traditions that influenced later empires in Asia.
