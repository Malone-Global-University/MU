Overview

Kush emerged from the earlier Kerma culture and became a major political, economic, and military power in the Nile Valley. Its rulers at times conquered Egypt, establishing the **25th Dynasty**, blending Nubian and Egyptian traditions.

Major Cities & Sites

  • Kerma: Early capital and religious center.

  • Napatan Sites: Religious and administrative centers connecting trade with Egypt.

Culture & Achievements

- Developed **Meroitic script** for writing.
- Advanced **ironworking and metallurgy**.
- Artistic synthesis of Egyptian and Nubian styles.
- Agriculture, trade, and control of Nile routes.

Legacy

Kushite innovations influenced later African kingdoms. The kingdom demonstrated the political and cultural power of Sub-Saharan civilizations and left a rich archaeological record in Nubia.
