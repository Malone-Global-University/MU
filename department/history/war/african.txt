<h2>Ancient & Classical Periods</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>3100–1000 BCE:</strong> Unification of Upper and Lower Egypt; Nubian and Kushite wars.</li>
  <li><strong>800 BCE–400 CE:</strong> Carthaginian Empire; Punic Wars; Axumite expansion.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Medieval & Pre-Colonial Periods</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>700–1500:</strong> Ghana, Mali, and Songhai empires; Swahili city-states; Great Zimbabwe conflicts.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Early Modern Era (1500–1800)</h2>
<ul>
  <li>European coastal forts, slave raids, Ashanti–Dahomey–Benin wars.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Modern & Contemporary (1800–Present)</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Scramble for Africa; Zulu wars; decolonization wars (Algeria, Kenya, Angola); modern coups and insurgencies.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Key Empires</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Egyptian, Nubian, Mali, Songhai, Benin, Ethiopian, Zulu</li>
</ul>
