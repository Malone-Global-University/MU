Key Themes

Feudalism

A hierarchical system where kings granted land to nobles in exchange for loyalty and military service, while peasants worked the land under strict obligations.

Religion

Christianity dominated daily life, politics, and culture. The Church influenced kings, law, education, and art across Europe.

Kingdoms and Empires

Powerful states and rulers emerged, including the Holy Roman Empire, Anglo-Saxon England, and the Capetian dynasty in France.
