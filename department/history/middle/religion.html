Medieval Religion

Christianity dominated daily life, politics, and culture. The Church influenced kings, law, education, and art across Europe.

Church Structure & Power

Popes, bishops, and clergy directed spiritual and political life. Monasteries preserved knowledge and guided local communities.

Religious Life

Cathedrals, monasteries, and pilgrimages shaped culture. Festivals, saints, and liturgy structured society.
