Unit 4: Systems of Equations & Inequalities

In this unit, you will learn how to solve systems of linear equations and inequalities. You will explore multiple solution methods‚Äîgraphing, substitution, and elimination‚Äîand apply them to real-world problems. By the end of this unit, you will be able to:

  • Graph linear inequalities and determine solution regions.
  • Solve systems of equations using graphing, substitution, and elimination.
  • Apply systems of equations to word problems and real-life scenarios.

Unit Goals

  • Solve systems of linear equations by graphing, substitution, and elimination.
  • Interpret solution types: one solution, no solution, or infinitely many solutions.
  • Solve and graph systems that include linear inequalities.
  • Formulate and solve real-world problems using systems of equations.
  • Check solutions and explain whether they satisfy each equation in a system.
