Unit 10: Exponents, Exponential & Logarithmic Functions

Unit Overview

This unit explores the properties and applications of exponents, exponential functions, and logarithms. You will learn how to apply laws of exponents, handle negative and zero exponents, convert numbers to scientific notation, graph exponential functions, solve exponential and logarithmic equations, and model real-world growth and decay scenarios.
