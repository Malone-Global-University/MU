Unit 11: Sequences & Series

In this unit, we explore sequences and series, including arithmetic and geometric sequences, infinite geometric series, and the Binomial Theorem. You will learn how to find terms, sums, and apply these concepts to problem-solving.

Unit Goals

  • Recognize and generate arithmetic and geometric sequences.
  • Use formulas for nth terms and sums of finite sequences.
  • Understand and evaluate infinite geometric series when they converge.
  • Apply the Binomial Theorem to expand binomial expressions and use binomial coefficients.
  • Translate between sigma notation and expanded sums.
