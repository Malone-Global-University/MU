Unit 8: Rational Expressions & Functions

In this unit, we study rational expressions, equations, and functions. You will learn to simplify, multiply, divide, add, and subtract rational expressions, solve rational equations, understand extraneous solutions, and graph rational functions including asymptotes and holes.

Unit Goals

  • Simplify rational expressions by factoring and cancelling common factors.
  • Perform multiplication, division, addition, and subtraction with rational expressions.
  • Solve rational equations and identify extraneous solutions.
  • Apply rational equations to rate and proportion problems.
  • Graph rational functions and identify vertical/horizontal/oblique asymptotes, holes, and intercepts.

Lessons

  • Simplifying Rational Expressions
  • Multiplying and Dividing Rational Expressions
  • Adding and Subtracting Rational Expressions
  • Rational Equations and Extraneous Solutions
  • Applications of Rational Equations (rates, proportions)
  • Graphing Rational Functions (asymptotes, holes, intercepts)
