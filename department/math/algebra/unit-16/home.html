Unit 16: Advanced Trigonometry

In this unit, we expand on basic trigonometry to explore advanced topics such as the Law of Sines, the Law of Cosines, sum and difference formulas, double-angle and half-angle identities, and real-world applications. These skills are essential for solving complex triangles and modeling periodic phenomena.

Learning Goals

  • Apply the Law of Sines and Law of Cosines to solve oblique triangles.
  • Use sum and difference identities to simplify expressions and solve equations.
  • Understand and apply double-angle and half-angle formulas.
  • Analyze and model real-world periodic phenomena using trigonometric functions.
  • Develop problem-solving strategies for advanced trigonometric applications.
