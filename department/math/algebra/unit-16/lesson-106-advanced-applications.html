Strategy for Solving Problems

  1. Identify whether the triangle is right or oblique.
  2. Label known sides, angles, and distances.
  3. Use appropriate formulas: right triangle ratios, Law of Sines, or Law of Cosines.
  4. Set up equations to solve for unknowns.
  5. Interpret the results in the context of the problem.
  6. Check solutions for feasibility and accuracy.
