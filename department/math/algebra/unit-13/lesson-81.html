Lesson 81: Applications of Nonlinear Systems

Introduction

Nonlinear systems are used to model a variety of real-world problems including geometry, physics, economics, and engineering. Solutions often represent intersection points of curves such as parabolas, circles, and ellipses.

Steps for Solving Word Problems

  1. Identify the variables and relationships in the problem.
  2. Write a nonlinear equation for one relationship and a linear or nonlinear equation for the other.
  3. Solve algebraically (substitution or elimination) or graphically.
  4. Interpret the solution in the context of the problem.
  5. Check the solution in both equations.
