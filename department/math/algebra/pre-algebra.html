Pre-Algebra

Main Takeaways

Pre-algebra is an advanced study of arithmetic to prepare for learning algebra and other higher math. The main things to study are:

  • Variables
  • Operations and their notation
  • The use of the numberline
  • Pemdas and order of operation
  • Properties, Laws, and Rules
  • Set Theory and later in algebra Set Notation
