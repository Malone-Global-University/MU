Unit 15: Introduction to Trigonometry

This unit introduces trigonometric functions and their applications. You will learn about angles, the unit circle, basic trigonometric functions, graphing, identities, and solving simple equations. Trigonometry is widely used in physics, engineering, architecture, and computer graphics.

Learning Goals

  • Understand angles in degrees and radians and how to convert between them.
  • Use the unit circle to evaluate sine, cosine, and tangent functions.
  • Graph sine, cosine, and tangent functions and identify amplitude, period, and phase shifts.
  • Apply fundamental trigonometric identities to simplify expressions.
  • Solve basic trigonometric equations within specified intervals.
