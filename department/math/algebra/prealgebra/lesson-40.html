Lesson 40 — Dividing Radicals

🎯 Goal

Learn to divide radicals using the quotient rule.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Quotient rule: \( \frac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{b}} = \sqrt{\frac{a}{b}} \)
  • Example: \( \frac{\sqrt{50}}{\sqrt{2}} = \sqrt{25} = 5 \)
  • Always simplify radicals when possible

✏️ Practice

  1. Compute \( \frac{\sqrt{18}}{\sqrt{2}} \)
  2. Compute \( \frac{4\sqrt{12}}{2\sqrt{3}} \)
  3. Compute \( \frac{\sqrt{45}}{\sqrt{5}} \)
