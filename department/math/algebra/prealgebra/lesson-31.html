Lesson 31 — Ratio and Proportion

🎯 Goal

Understand and solve problems involving ratios and proportions.

📖 Lesson Description

  • A ratio compares two quantities (e.g., 3:4)
  • A proportion states two ratios are equal (e.g., 3/4 = 6/8)
  • Cross-multiply to solve proportions

🔎 Examples

  • Ratio of 2 apples to 3 oranges = 2:3
  • Proportion: 2/3 = x/9 → 2×9 = 3x → x = 6

✏️ Practice

  1. Solve 4/5 = x/20
  2. Write the ratio of 7 boys to 5 girls
  3. Check if 6/9 = 8/12 forms a proportion
