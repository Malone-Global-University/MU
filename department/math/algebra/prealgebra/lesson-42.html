Lesson 42 — Powers of 10

🎯 Goal

Understand and apply powers of 10 in calculations.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Power of 10: \( 10^n \) means 10 multiplied by itself n times
  • Positive exponent → large numbers: \( 10^3 = 1000 \)
  • Negative exponent → small numbers: \( 10^{-2} = 0.01 \)

✏️ Practice

  1. Compute \( 10^4 \)
  2. Compute \( 10^{-3} \)
  3. Write \( 0.001 \) as a power of 10
