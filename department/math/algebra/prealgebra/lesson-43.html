Lesson 43 — Scientific Notation

🎯 Goal

Represent very large or very small numbers using scientific notation.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Scientific notation: \( a \times 10^n \), where \(1 \le a < 10\)
  • Large number: 45000 → \( 4.5 \times 10^4 \)
  • Small number: 0.0032 → \( 3.2 \times 10^{-3} \)

✏️ Practice

  1. Write 120000 in scientific notation
  2. Write 0.00056 in scientific notation
  3. Convert \( 7.2 \times 10^5 \) to standard form
