Lesson 18 — Adding and Subtracting Fractions

🎯 Goal

Learn to add and subtract fractions with like and unlike denominators.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Same denominator: add/subtract numerators, keep denominator
  • Different denominators: find LCM of denominators, rewrite fractions, then add/subtract

🔎 Examples

  • 2/5 + 1/5 = 3/5
  • 1/3 + 1/4 → LCM of 3 & 4 = 12 → 4/12 + 3/12 = 7/12
  • 5/6 − 1/3 → 5/6 − 2/6 = 3/6 = 1/2

✏️ Practice

  1. 3/8 + 1/4
  2. 7/10 − 3/5
  3. 2/3 + 5/6
