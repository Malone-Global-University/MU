Lesson 36 — Power Rule for Exponents

🎯 Goal

Apply the power rule: \( (a^m)^n = a^{m \cdot n} \).

📖 Lesson Description

  • Raising a power to another power multiplies exponents
  • Example: \( (2^3)^2 = 2^{3\cdot2} = 2^6 = 64 \)
  • Example: \( (5^2)^4 = 5^{8} \)

✏️ Practice

  1. Compute \( (3^2)^3 \)
  2. Compute \( (4^3)^2 \)
  3. Compute \( (2^5)^4 \)
