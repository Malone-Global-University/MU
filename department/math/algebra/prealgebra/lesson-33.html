Lesson 33 — Unit Multipliers

🎯 Goal

Learn to convert units using multiplication factors.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Unit multiplier = conversion factor (e.g., 1 m = 100 cm → multiplier = 100)
  • Multiply original value by factor to convert
  • Example: 5 m → 5 × 100 = 500 cm

🔎 Examples

  • 3 km → meters? 3 × 1000 = 3000 m
  • 2.5 hours → minutes? 2.5 × 60 = 150 min

✏️ Practice

  1. 7 m → cm
  2. 4.5 kg → grams
  3. 2.5 hours → seconds
