Lesson 29 — Repeating Decimals

🎯 Goal

Understand and represent decimals that repeat infinitely.

📖 Lesson Description

  • A repeating decimal has one or more digits that repeat infinitely
  • Notation: bar above repeating digit(s) (e.g., 0.333… = 0.\overline{3})
  • Can convert to fraction: 0.\overline{3} = 1/3

🔎 Examples

  • 0.\overline{6} = 2/3
  • 0.1\overline{6} = 1/6
  • 0.\overline{142857} = 1/7

✏️ Practice

  1. Convert 0.\overline{3} to fraction
  2. Convert 0.2\overline{5} to fraction
  3. Write 1/9 as a repeating decimal
