Lesson 34 — Exponents

🎯 Goal

Understand exponents and their role in repeated multiplication.

📖 Lesson Description

  • An exponent shows how many times a number (base) is multiplied by itself.
  • Notation: \( a^n = a \times a \times \dots \times a \) (n times)
  • Examples: \( 2^3 = 2×2×2 = 8 \), \( 5^2 = 25 \)

✏️ Practice

  1. Compute \( 3^4 \)
  2. Compute \( 6^2 \)
  3. Compute \( 2^5 \)
