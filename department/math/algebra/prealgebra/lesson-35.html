Lesson 35 — Rules of Exponents

🎯 Goal

Learn the basic rules for working with exponents.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Multiplying powers: \( a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n} \)
  • Dividing powers: \( \frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n} \)
  • Power of a power: \( (a^m)^n = a^{m\cdot n} \)
  • Power of a product: \( (ab)^n = a^n b^n \)

✏️ Practice

  1. Compute \( 2^3 \cdot 2^4 \)
  2. Compute \( 5^6 ÷ 5^2 \)
  3. Compute \( (3^2)^3 \)
