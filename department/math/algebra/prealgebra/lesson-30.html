Lesson 30 — Rounding

🎯 Goal

Learn to round decimals to the desired place value.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Look at the digit to the right of the place to round
  • If ≥ 5 → increase target digit by 1
  • If < 5 → leave target digit
  • Drop all digits to the right

🔎 Examples

  • Round 4.678 to hundredths → 4.68
  • Round 3.14159 to thousandths → 3.142
  • Round 7.85 to nearest whole → 8

✏️ Practice

  1. Round 2.346 to tenths
  2. Round 5.6789 to hundredths
  3. Round 9.49 to nearest whole number
