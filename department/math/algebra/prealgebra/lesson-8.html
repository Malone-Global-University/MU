Lesson 8 — Review of Numbers & Signed Operations

🎯 Goal

Review and consolidate understanding of absolute value, addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division of signed numbers.

📖 Summary

  • Absolute value = distance from zero.
  • Adding same signs: add and keep sign.
  • Adding different signs: subtract and keep larger sign.
  • Subtracting: change to adding the opposite.
  • Multiplying/Dividing: same signs = positive, different signs = negative.
