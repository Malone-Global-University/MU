Unit 12: Conic Sections

This unit explores the four types of conic sections: circles, parabolas, ellipses, and hyperbolas. You will learn their equations, properties, and applications, including the focus-directrix definition for parabolas.

Unit Goals

  • Identify and graph the four conic sections: circles, parabolas, ellipses, and hyperbolas.
  • Derive and use standard equations for each conic and locate key features (center, vertices, foci, asymptotes).
  • Understand the focus-directrix definition of a parabola and apply it in problems.
  • Apply conic sections to geometric and physical contexts (reflective properties, orbits, optics).
