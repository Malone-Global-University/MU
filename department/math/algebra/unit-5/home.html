Polynomials & Factoring

In this unit, you will explore polynomials, their operations, and factoring techniques. You will learn how to add, subtract, multiply, and divide polynomials, as well as factor using greatest common factors, special products, and trinomial techniques. By the end of this unit, you will be able to:

  • Understand the structure and degree of polynomials.
  • Add, subtract, multiply, and divide polynomials.
  • Factor polynomials using GCF, special products, and trinomial factoring.
  • Solve quadratic equations by factoring.

Unit Goals

  • Identify polynomials and perform addition, subtraction, and multiplication (including FOIL).
  • Recognize and apply special products (perfect square trinomials, difference of squares).
  • Factor polynomials using GCF, factoring trinomials, and special-case techniques.
  • Use long division and synthetic division to divide polynomials.
  • Solve quadratic equations by factoring and verify solutions.
