Unit 9: Radicals & Rational Exponents

Unit Goals

  • Understand principal roots and simplify radical expressions.
  • Perform arithmetic with radicals (add, subtract, multiply, divide) and rationalize denominators.
  • Work with rational exponents and convert between radical and exponent forms.
  • Solve radical equations and check for extraneous solutions.
  • Graph basic radical functions and describe domain, range, and transformations.
