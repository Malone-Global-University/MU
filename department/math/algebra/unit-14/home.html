Unit 14: Probability & Statistics

This unit introduces the fundamentals of probability and statistics. You will learn to calculate probabilities, interpret data, analyze distributions, and understand measures of central tendency and variability.

Lessons in Unit 14

  • Introduction to Probability
  • Counting Principles and Combinatorics
  • Probability Rules (addition, multiplication)
  • Independent and Dependent Events
  • Random Variables and Expected Value
  • Descriptive Statistics (mean, median, mode, range, vce, standard deviation)
  • Probability Distributions (binomial, normal)
