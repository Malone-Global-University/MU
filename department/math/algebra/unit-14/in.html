Unit 14: Probability and Statistics

Overview

In this unit, you will learn how mathematics helps us understand uncertainty, randomness, and data. Probability gives us tools for predicting outcomes of random events, while statistics allows us to summarize and interpret real-world data. Together, these topics are used in science, business, medicine, and everyday decision-making.

Unit Goals

  • Understand the basic concepts of probability and random events.
  • Apply counting principles and combinatorics to determine the number of outcomes.
  • Use addition and multiplication rules of probability.
  • Distinguish between independent and dependent events.
  • Define and compute random variables and expected value.
  • Calculate descriptive statistics (mean, median, mode, variance, standard deviation).
  • Explore common probability distributions, including binomial and normal.
  • Develop skills for interpreting and communicating results of statistical analysis.
