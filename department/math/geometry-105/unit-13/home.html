Unit 13: Vectors in Coordinate Plane

Learn how vectors represent direction and magnitude, perform vector operations, and apply vectors to physics and engineering problems.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 13: Vectors in Coordinate Plane

Lessons

  • Lesson 13.1: Vector Representation & Magnitude Calculations
    Learn how vectors are represented in the coordinate plane and how to compute vector magnitude and direction.
  • Lesson 13.2: Vector Addition, Subtraction & Applications Applied Problems
    Perform vector addition and subtraction and solve applied coordinate and motion problems using vectors.
  • Lesson 13.3: Dot Product & Angle Between Vectors Calculations & Proofs
    Use dot products to compute angles between vectors and solve geometric and algebraic vector proofs.
  • Lesson 13.4: Applications in Physics & Engineering Word Problems
    Apply vectors to real-world physics and engineering scenarios involving forces, motion, and directional analysis.
