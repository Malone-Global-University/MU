Unit 7: Similarity

Explore triangle similarity, proportional reasoning, indirect measurement, and scale factors to solve real-world geometric problems.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 7: Similarity

Lessons

  • Lesson 7.1: Similarity Criteria
    Learn and apply AA, SAS, and SSS similarity criteria to determine when triangles are similar and justify proportional relationships.
  • Lesson 7.2: Proportions & Indirect Measurement Applied Problems
    Use proportional reasoning and similar triangles to solve indirect measurement problems involving shadows, heights, and distances.
  • Lesson 7.3: Scale Factors & Real-World Applications Word Problems
    Analyze scale factors in geometric figures, maps, models, and real-world applications to determine missing side lengths and area relationships.
