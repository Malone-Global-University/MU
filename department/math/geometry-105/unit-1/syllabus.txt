Malone Global University: Online Geometry Course Skeleton

Course: Foundations & Advanced Geometry
Target: High school / early college students
Format: Self-paced, modular, interactive

Unit 1: Foundations of Geometry – 2–3 lessons, 1–1.5 hours total
Lesson	Type	Content	Practice / Quiz	Time
1.1 Points, Lines, Planes	Video + Text	Definitions, diagrams, intersection concepts	5 MCQs + label diagram activity	15 min
1.2 Segments & Angles	Text + Interactive Diagram	Measuring, bisecting, angle relationships	Drag-and-drop angle labeling	20 min
1.3 Basic Constructions	Video Demo	Compass & straightedge: bisectors, perpendiculars	Guided construction exercises	25–30 min
Unit 2: Logic and Geometric Reasoning – 3 lessons, 1–1.5 hours
Lesson	Type	Content	Practice / Quiz	Time
2.1 Conditional Statements	Text + Video	If-then statements, converse, inverse	MCQs + identify statements	20 min
2.2 Biconditionals & Logical Equivalence	Video + Interactive	“If and only if”, truth tables	Truth table exercises	25 min
2.3 Introduction to Proofs	Guided Tutorial	Paragraph proofs, two-column proofs	Practice short proofs	25–30 min
Unit 3: Parallel and Perpendicular Lines – 2 lessons, ~1 hour
Lesson	Type	Content	Practice / Quiz	Time

Additional Course Features

Unit Summaries: Printable cheat sheets for formulas, theorems, and proof structures

Progress Tracking: Students see completed lessons, quizzes, and mastery

Discussion Forums: Peer Q&A for proofs and constructions

Capstone Project: Analyze a complex figure combining transformations, vectors, proofs, and circles
