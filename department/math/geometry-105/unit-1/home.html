Unit 1: Foundations of Geometry

Build a strong foundation in geometry by learning the basics of points, lines, planes, segments, angles, constructions, and geometric notation.

Home Department Geometry Course Unit 1: Foundations of Geometry

Lessons

  • Lesson 1.1: Points, Lines, Planes
    Understand fundamental geometric elements: points, lines, and planes.
  • Lesson 1.2: Segments & Angles
    Learn about line segments, rays, and types of angles.
  • Lesson 1.3: Basic Constructions
    Use compass and straightedge to construct basic geometric figures.
  • Lesson 1.4: Geometric Notation & Symbols
    Learn standard geometric symbols and how to read/write them.
  • Lesson 1.5: Measurement Tools & Precision
    Use rulers, protractors, and other tools to measure accurately.
