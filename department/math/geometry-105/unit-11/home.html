Unit 11: Transformations

Understand how geometric figures move and change through translations, reflections, rotations, dilations, symmetry, and matrix transformations.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 11: Transformations

Lessons

  • Lesson 11.1: Translations & Reflections Interactive Graph
    Explore translations and reflections using coordinate graphs and interactive visualization techniques.
  • Lesson 11.2: Rotations & Dilations Applied Problems
    Apply rotational transformations and dilations to solve real-world and coordinate-based geometric problems.
  • Lesson 11.3: Composition & Symmetry Proof-style Exercises
    Analyze compositions of multiple transformations and develop proof-based reasoning involving symmetry.
  • Lesson 11.4: Transformation Matrices & Coordinates Calculations
    Use transformation matrices to calculate coordinate changes and understand algebraic representations of transformations.
