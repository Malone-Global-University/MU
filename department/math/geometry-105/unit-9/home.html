Unit 9: Area & Volume

Apply geometric formulas to calculate area, surface area, and volume, and solve multi-step composite figure problems.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 9: Area & Volume

Lessons

  • Lesson 9.1: Area of Polygons & Circles Exercises
    Practice calculating area of triangles, quadrilaterals, regular polygons, and circles using algebraic and geometric formulas.
  • Lesson 9.2: Surface Area & Volume Applied Problems
    Solve real-world problems involving prisms, cylinders, pyramids, cones, and spheres using surface area and volume formulas.
  • Lesson 9.3: Composite Figures Problem Set
    Decompose complex shapes into simpler components to determine total area and volume in multi-step problem scenarios.
