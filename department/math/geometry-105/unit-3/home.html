Unit 3: Parallel & Perpendicular Lines

Learn the properties and proofs of parallel and perpendicular lines, angle relationships formed by transversals, and how to apply these concepts in geometric reasoning.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 3: Parallel & Perpendicular Lines

Lessons

  • Lesson 3.1: Parallel Lines & Angle Relationships
    Explore angles formed by parallel lines and a transversal, including alternate interior, alternate exterior, and corresponding angles.
  • Lesson 3.2: Perpendicular Lines & Slopes
    Understand perpendicular lines, slopes, and how to determine perpendicularity in coordinate geometry.
  • Lesson 3.3: Proving Lines Parallel & Perpendicular
    Learn proof techniques for showing lines are parallel or perpendicular using postulates, theorems, and algebraic methods.
  • Lesson 3.4: Review & Practice Problems
    Consolidate learning through review exercises and challenging practice problems involving parallel and perpendicular lines.
