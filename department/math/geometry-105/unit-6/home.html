Unit 6: Polygons & Quadrilaterals

Explore the properties of polygons and quadrilaterals, learn how to calculate angle sums, and discover patterns and tessellations.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 6: Polygons & Quadrilaterals

Lessons

  • Lesson 6.1: Angle Sums
    Understand angle sum properties in triangles, quadrilaterals, and other polygons.
  • Lesson 6.2: Quadrilaterals
    Learn classifications, properties, and relationships of various quadrilaterals.
  • Lesson 6.3: Regular Polygons & Interior/Exterior Angles
    Calculate interior and exterior angles of regular polygons and explore their properties.
  • Lesson 6.4: Tessellations & Patterns
    Discover tessellations and geometric patterns and how they relate to polygons.
