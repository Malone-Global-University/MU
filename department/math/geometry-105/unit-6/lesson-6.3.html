Lesson Description

This lesson focuses on regular polygons, including how to calculate interior and exterior angles, and understanding relationships between the number of sides and angle measures.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify regular polygons and their properties.
  • Calculate interior and exterior angles of regular polygons.
  • Apply formulas to solve polygon problems.

Topics Covered

  • Definition of regular polygons
  • Interior angle formula: \((n-2) \times 180^\circ / n\)
  • Exterior angle formula: \(360^\circ / n\)
  • Relationship between interior and exterior angles
  • Applications and problem solving with polygons

Key Terms

  • Regular Polygon: Polygon with all sides and angles equal.
  • Interior Angle: Angle inside a polygon at a vertex.
  • Exterior Angle: Angle formed outside the polygon, supplementary to the interior angle.
  • n-gon: Polygon with \(n\) sides.
