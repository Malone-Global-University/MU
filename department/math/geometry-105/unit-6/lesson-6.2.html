Lesson Description

This lesson introduces quadrilaterals, their types, and properties. Students will learn to identify squares, rectangles, parallelograms, rhombuses, trapezoids, and kites, and understand their side and angle relationships.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify different types of quadrilaterals.
  • Understand angle and side relationships in quadrilaterals.
  • Apply quadrilateral properties to solve geometric problems.

Topics Covered

  • Definition and properties of quadrilaterals
  • Squares, rectangles, parallelograms, rhombuses, trapezoids, and kites
  • Interior and exterior angles of quadrilaterals
  • Diagonals and symmetry properties

Key Terms

  • Quadrilateral: A four-sided polygon.
  • Square: All sides equal, all angles 90Â°.
  • Rectangle: Opposite sides equal, all angles 90Â°.
  • Parallelogram: Opposite sides parallel and equal.
  • Rhombus: All sides equal, opposite angles equal.
  • Trapezoid: One pair of parallel sides.
  • Kite: Two pairs of adjacent sides equal.
