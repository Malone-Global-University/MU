Unit 10: Circles

Investigate arc relationships, circle angle theorems, tangent and secant properties, and coordinate circle equations.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 10: Circles

Lessons

  • Lesson 10.1: Arcs & Central Angles
    Analyze arc measures and central angles, including proportional relationships and problem-solving applications.
  • Lesson 10.2: Chords, Secants, Tangents Proof Exercises
    Develop formal proofs involving chord relationships, tangent properties, and secant segment theorems.
  • Lesson 10.3: Inscribed Angles & Sector Measures Mixed Problems
    Solve mixed problems involving inscribed angles, intercepted arcs, and sector area calculations.
  • Lesson 10.4: Tangent-Secant & Chord Theorems Proof Exercises
    Apply tangent-secant power theorems and chord product theorems through formal geometric proofs.
  • Lesson 10.5: Circle Equations in Coordinate Plane Applied Problems
    Use coordinate geometry to derive and apply circle equations to real-world and analytic geometry problems.
