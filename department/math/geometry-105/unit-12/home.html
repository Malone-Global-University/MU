Unit 12: Advanced Geometric Constructions

Explore classical compass-and-straightedge constructions, inscribed polygons, locus problems, and the mathematics behind the golden ratio through guided practice and creative construction projects.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 12: Advanced Geometric Constructions

Lessons

  • Lesson 12.1: Compass & Straightedge Basics
    Review essential classical tools and perform step-by-step constructions such as perpendicular bisectors, angle bisectors, parallel lines, and basic geometric figures.
  • Lesson 12.2: Inscribed Polygons & Locus
    Construct polygons inscribed in circles and solve guided locus problems describing sets of points that satisfy geometric conditions.
  • Lesson 12.3: Classical Constructions
    Apply classical construction techniques in open-ended assignments requiring strategic planning, justification, and precise geometric execution.
  • Lesson 12.4: Golden Ratio & Constructible Figures
    Explore the golden ratio and construct geometrically significant figures, culminating in a creative exploration project connecting mathematics and design.
