Unit 8: Right Triangles & Trigonometry

Master right triangle relationships and trigonometric methods to solve real-world and geometric problems with precision.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 8: Right Triangles & Trigonometry

Lessons

  • Lesson 8.1: Pythagorean Theorem & Special Triangles Calculations
    Apply the Pythagorean Theorem and use 45-45-90 and 30-60-90 triangle relationships to calculate missing side lengths.
  • Lesson 8.2: Trig Ratios Word Problems
    Use sine, cosine, and tangent ratios to solve applied problems involving angles of elevation, depression, and distance.
  • Lesson 8.3: Law of Sines & Law of Cosines Applied Problems
    Solve non-right (oblique) triangles using the Law of Sines and Law of Cosines in advanced real-world applications.
