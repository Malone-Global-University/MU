Unit 4: Triangle Congruence

Develop mastery in triangle congruence proofs using SSS, SAS, ASA, AAS, HL, and CPCTC while applying triangle inequality reasoning to solve geometric problems.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 4: Triangle Congruence

Lessons

  • Lesson 4.1: SSS & SAS Proof Exercises
    Practice proving triangle congruence using the Side-Side-Side and Side-Angle-Side theorems in structured geometric proofs.
  • Lesson 4.2: ASA & AAS Proof Exercises
    Use Angle-Side-Angle and Angle-Angle-Side relationships to justify triangle congruence and complete formal diagram proofs.
  • Lesson 4.3: HL & CPCTC Diagram Exercises
    Apply the Hypotenuse-Leg theorem for right triangles and use CPCTC to prove corresponding parts of congruent triangles.
  • Lesson 4.4: Triangle Inequalities Problem Set
    Solve problems involving the triangle inequality theorem, side length constraints, and geometric reasoning about possible triangles.
