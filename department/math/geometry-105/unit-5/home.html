Unit 5: Properties of Triangles

Explore triangle structure and internal geometry including medians, altitudes, angle bisectors, triangle centers, and special triangle types.

Home Department Mathematics Geometry 105 Unit 5: Properties of Triangles

Lessons

  • Lesson 5.1: Medians, Altitudes, Angle Bisectors
    Learn the definitions and geometric properties of triangle medians, perpendicular altitudes, and angle bisectors, and how each creates important triangle centers.
  • Lesson 5.2: Concurrency Points Applied Problems
    Apply centroid, circumcenter, incenter, and orthocenter properties to solve geometric and real-world triangle problems.
  • Lesson 5.3: Special Triangles
    Study important triangle families including isosceles triangles, equilateral triangles, and right triangles with special proportional relationships.
