Lesson Description

This lesson focuses specifically on the Power Rule of Exponents, which explains how to simplify an exponent raised to another exponent.

Learning Objectives

  • Apply the power rule correctly.
  • Simplify expressions like (a^m)^n.
  • Combine power rule with other exponent rules.

Topics Covered

  • Power of a power rule
  • Multiplying exponents
  • Simplifying exponential expressions

Key Terms

  • Power Rule: When raising a power to a power, multiply the exponents.
  • Exponent: Indicates repeated multiplication.
  • Base: The number being raised to a power.
