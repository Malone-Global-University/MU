Unit 13: Word Problems & Problem Solving

This unit focuses on translating real-world situations into mathematical models using multi-step reasoning, fractions, decimals, percents, variables, and equations.

Unit Summary

Unit 13 synthesizes prior mathematical skills into structured problem-solving strategies. Students learn how to:

  • Break complex problems into manageable steps.
  • Translate verbal descriptions into numerical expressions and algebraic equations.
  • Apply fractions, decimals, and percents in real-world contexts.
  • Use variables to model unknown quantities.
  • Engage in higher-order reasoning through challenge problems.

Mastery of this unit demonstrates algebra readiness and the ability to reason quantitatively in applied scenarios.
