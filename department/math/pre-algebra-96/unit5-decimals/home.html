Unit 5: Decimals

In this unit, you will learn about decimal numbers, including place value, arithmetic operations, rounding, repeating decimals, and solving real-world problems with decimals.

Unit Summary

This unit focuses on understanding and applying decimal concepts. You will learn to read, write, and compare decimals, perform arithmetic operations, round decimals, identify repeating and terminating decimals, and solve word problems involving decimals. Mastery of these skills is crucial for success in higher-level math courses.
