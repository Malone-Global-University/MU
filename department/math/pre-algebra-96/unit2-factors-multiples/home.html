Unit Summary

This unit focuses on understanding how numbers relate to each other through factors and multiples. You will learn techniques for prime factorization, identify prime and composite numbers, and apply LCM and GCF in real-world problems. Mastery of these concepts is essential for higher-level Pre-Algebra topics.
