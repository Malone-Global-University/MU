❌ All Math Classes Higher than Geometry 105

The math department has finished Pre-Algebra 96, Algebra 101, and Geometry 105. Right now they are text only lessons. Full interactivity and video lessons are planned when location and staffing allows. Math courses have new title codes. For example Algebra is now Algebra 101 listed in databes as MAth 101. In general all courses with follow the format department name followed by course numerical code.

Numerical codes equal to or higher than 100 are considered college level. Codes less than 100 are High School or lower and are aimed at Adult remedial learners but are suitable for all ages if student feels ready. Codes equal to 200 to 299 are graduate level or research heavy. Codes 300 or higher are Self interest, personal enrichment, or hobby specific.
