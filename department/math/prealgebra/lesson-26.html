Lesson 26 — Adding Mixed Measures

🎯 Goal

Learn to add mixed units (e.g., feet & inches, hours & minutes).

📖 Lesson Description

  • Align units (e.g., convert inches to feet, minutes to hours)
  • Add the smaller units first, convert if necessary
  • Add whole numbers separately

🔎 Examples

  • 2 ft 8 in + 3 ft 9 in → (2+3) ft + (8+9) in = 5 ft 17 in → 6 ft 5 in
  • 1 hr 45 min + 2 hr 30 min → 3 hr + 75 min → 4 hr 15 min

✏️ Practice

  1. 5 ft 7 in + 3 ft 9 in
  2. 2 hr 50 min + 1 hr 40 min
  3. 4 ft 11 in + 2 ft 6 in
