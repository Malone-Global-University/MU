Lesson 6 — Multiplying Signed Numbers

🎯 Goal

Understand how to multiply positive and negative numbers and apply the sign rules.

📝 Rules

  • Positive × Positive = Positive
  • Negative × Negative = Positive
  • Positive × Negative = Negative
  • Negative × Positive = Negative

🔎 Examples

  • \\(3 \\times 4 = 12\\)
  • \\((-5) \\times 6 = -30\\)
  • \\((-7) \\times (-2) = 14\\)

✏️ Practice

  1. \\((-3) \\times 8\\)
  2. \\(9 \\times (-4)\\)
  3. \\((-6) \\times (-5)\\)
