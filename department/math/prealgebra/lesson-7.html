Lesson 7 — Dividing Signed Numbers

🎯 Goal

Learn the rules for dividing positive and negative numbers.

📝 Rules

  • Positive ÷ Positive = Positive
  • Negative ÷ Negative = Positive
  • Positive ÷ Negative = Negative
  • Negative ÷ Positive = Negative

🔎 Examples

  • \\(12 \\div 3 = 4\\)
  • \\((-20) \\div 4 = -5\\)
  • \\((-18) \\div (-6) = 3\\)

✏️ Practice

  1. \\((-36) \\div 9\\)
  2. \\(42 \\div (-7)\\)
  3. \\((-30) \\div (-5)\\)
