Lesson 23 — Adding and Subtracting Mixed Numbers

🎯 Goal

Learn to add and subtract mixed numbers by converting to improper fractions.

📖 Lesson Description

  1. Convert mixed numbers to improper fractions
  2. Find LCM of denominators if needed
  3. Add or subtract fractions
  4. Convert result back to mixed number if improper

🔎 Examples

  • 2 1/3 + 1 2/3 → 7/3 + 5/3 = 12/3 = 4
  • 3 1/4 − 1 2/5 → 13/4 − 7/5 → LCM 20 → 65/20 − 28/20 = 37/20 = 1 17/20

✏️ Practice

  1. 1 3/5 + 2 2/5
  2. 4 1/2 − 1 3/4
  3. 2 2/3 + 3 1/6
