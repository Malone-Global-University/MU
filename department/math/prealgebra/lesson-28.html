Lesson 28 — Decimal Arithmetic

🎯 Goal

Learn to add, subtract, multiply, and divide decimals.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Add/Subtract: align decimal points
  • Multiply: ignore decimals, multiply normally, place decimal in product
  • Divide: move decimal to make divisor a whole number

🔎 Examples

  • 2.5 + 3.75 = 6.25
  • 4.8 × 0.5 = 2.4
  • 6.4 ÷ 0.8 = 8

✏️ Practice

  1. 3.2 + 1.45
  2. 7.5 − 2.25
  3. 1.2 × 0.3
  4. 4.5 ÷ 0.5
