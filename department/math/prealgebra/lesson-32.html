Lesson 32 — Unit Price

🎯 Goal

Learn to calculate unit price for comparison shopping.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Unit price = total price ÷ quantity
  • Helps compare prices of different sizes
  • Example: $12 for 4 kg → unit price = $12 ÷ 4 = $3 per kg

🔎 Examples

  • 6 cans for $9 → 9 ÷ 6 = $1.50 per can
  • 8 liters for $24 → 24 ÷ 8 = $3 per liter

✏️ Practice

  1. 5 pens for $7.50 → unit price?
  2. 3 kg apples for $4.20 → unit price?
  3. 10 eggs for $2 → unit price?
