Lesson 15 — Reducing Fractions and Cancellation

🎯 Goal

Learn to simplify fractions to their lowest terms using cancellation.

📖 Lesson Description

A fraction is reduced when the numerator and denominator have no common factors other than 1. Cancellation is dividing both by the same number.

🔎 Examples

  • 8/12 → divide numerator & denominator by 4 → 2/3
  • 15/25 → divide numerator & denominator by 5 → 3/5

✏️ Practice

  1. Reduce 18/24
  2. Reduce 30/45
  3. Reduce 14/28
