Lesson 25 — Relationships of Numbers

🎯 Goal

Understand how mixed numbers, improper fractions, and whole numbers relate to each other.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Mixed numbers = whole + proper fraction
  • Improper fractions = numerator ≥ denominator
  • Any whole number = fraction with denominator 1

🔎 Examples

  • 2 1/3 = 7/3
  • 4 = 4/1
  • 1 1/2 = 3/2

✏️ Practice

  1. Convert 5 2/5 to improper fraction
  2. Convert 7/2 to mixed number
  3. Convert 6 to fraction
