Lesson 10 — Multiples

🎯 Goal

Learn to identify multiples and how to find them efficiently.

📖 Lesson Description

A multiple of a number is obtained by multiplying it by a whole number. For example, multiples of 4: 4, 8, 12, 16…

📝 Examples

  • Multiples of 3: 3, 6, 9, 12, 15…
  • Multiples of 5: 5, 10, 15, 20, 25…
  • 20 is a multiple of 4 because 4 × 5 = 20

✏️ Practice

  1. List the first 5 multiples of 6.
  2. Is 36 a multiple of 9?
  3. Find a multiple of 7 between 20 and 50.
