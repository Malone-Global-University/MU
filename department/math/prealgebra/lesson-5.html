Lesson 5 — Adding and Subtracting Signed Numbers

🎯 Goal

Learn to add and subtract positive and negative numbers correctly using number line reasoning and rules.

📖 Lesson Description

Signed numbers include both positives and negatives. Adding and subtracting them depends on their signs.

📝 Rules

  • Adding same signs: Add their absolute values, keep the sign.
  • Adding different signs: Subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger, keep the sign of the larger.
  • Subtracting: Change subtraction to adding the opposite, then follow addition rules.

🔎 Examples

  • \\(5 + (-3) = 2\\)
  • \\((-4) + (-7) = -11\\)
  • \\(9 - (-2) = 9 + 2 = 11\\)
  • \\((-6) - 8 = -14\\)

✏️ Practice

  1. \\(7 + (-5)\\)
  2. \\((-10) + 3\\)
  3. \\(4 - (-9)\\)
  4. \\((-12) - 6\\)
