Lesson 27 — Place Value

🎯 Goal

Understand the value of digits in whole numbers and decimals.

📖 Lesson Description

  • Digits to the left of decimal: ones, tens, hundreds…
  • Digits to the right of decimal: tenths, hundredths, thousandths…
  • Example: 45.678 → 4 tens, 5 ones, 6 tenths, 7 hundredths, 8 thousandths

✏️ Practice

  1. Write place value of each digit in 123.45
  2. Write place value of each digit in 0.678
  3. Identify the hundredths digit in 56.789
