Unit Summary

This unit introduces students to the coordinate plane as a foundational tool for algebra. Students learn to plot ordered pairs, identify quadrants, calculate slope as rate of change, and connect linear patterns to equations and real-world problem-solving. Mastery of these concepts prepares students for formal Algebra I.
