Unit 10: Equations & Inequalities

In this unit, you will learn to solve equations and inequalities, work with fractions and decimals, graph solutions, and apply concepts to real-world word problems.

Unit Summary

This unit develops algebraic problem-solving skills by teaching students how to solve equations and inequalities of increasing complexity. These skills are foundational for success in algebra, higher mathematics, and real-world quantitative reasoning.
