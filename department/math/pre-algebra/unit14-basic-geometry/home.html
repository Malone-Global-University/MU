Unit 14: Basic Geometry

This unit introduces foundational geometry concepts including perimeter, area, volume, angles, coordinate geometry, triangles, and introductory trigonometry.

Unit Summary

In this unit, students build core geometric understanding by learning formulas for perimeter, area, and volume, applying variables in formulas, working with coordinate geometry, analyzing angles and triangles, and solving real-world geometry problems. The unit concludes with an introduction to right-triangle trigonometry using SOHCAHTOA, preparing students for future algebra and geometry studies.
