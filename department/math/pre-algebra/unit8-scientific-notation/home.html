Unit 8: Scientific Notation

In this unit, you will learn how to express very large and very small numbers using powers of 10, perform operations in scientific notation, estimate efficiently, and apply concepts to real-world situations.

Unit Summary

This unit develops fluency with powers of 10 and scientific notation, enabling students to efficiently represent and compute with extremely large and extremely small numbers. These skills are foundational for algebra, higher mathematics, and scientific disciplines such as physics, engineering, and economics.
