Lesson 47: Decimal Word Problems

Apply your decimal skills to solve real-world word problems involving addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, estimation, and rounding.

Home Department Math Pre-Algebra Unit 5: Decimals Lesson 47: Decimal Word Problems

Lesson Objectives

  • Solve word problems using decimals with all four operations.
  • Apply rounding and estimation to simplify calculations.
  • Interpret real-world problems and identify the correct operation.
  • Check answers for reasonableness using estimation and rounding.

Introduction

Decimal word problems appear in many real-world scenarios like money, measurements, and distances. Understanding how to apply decimal operations accurately is key to solving them efficiently.
