Lesson 45: Repeating & Terminating Decimals

Understand the difference between repeating and terminating decimals, learn how to convert fractions to decimals, and identify patterns in decimal expansions.

Home Department Math Pre-Algebra Unit 5: Decimals Lesson 45: Repeating & Terminating Decimals

Lesson Objectives

  • Differentiate between repeating and terminating decimals.
  • Convert fractions into decimals and identify their type.
  • Recognize patterns in repeating decimals.
  • Apply knowledge of decimal types to solve problems.

Introduction

Decimals can either terminate (end) or repeat a pattern indefinitely. Understanding the difference helps in calculations, rounding, and recognizing fraction equivalents.

Summary
