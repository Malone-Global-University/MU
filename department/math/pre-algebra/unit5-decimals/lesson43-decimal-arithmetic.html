Lesson 43: Decimal Arithmetic

Learn how to add, subtract, multiply, and divide decimals using step-by-step techniques, and apply them in real-world contexts.

Home Department Math Pre-Algebra Unit 5: Decimals Lesson 43: Decimal Arithmetic

Lesson Objectives

  • Add and subtract decimal numbers accurately.
  • Multiply decimal numbers and determine the correct placement of the decimal point.
  • Divide decimals and interpret the quotient correctly.
  • Apply decimal arithmetic to solve real-world problems.

Introduction

Decimal arithmetic allows us to work with numbers that are not whole. Understanding how to operate with decimals is essential for measurement, money, and scientific calculations.
