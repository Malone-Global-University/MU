Lesson 44: Estimating with Decimals

Learn strategies to quickly approximate decimal calculations by rounding and using compatible numbers, helping you solve problems efficiently.

Lesson Objectives

  • Round decimals to the nearest whole number, tenth, or hundredth.
  • Use compatible numbers to estimate sums, differences, products, and quotients.
  • Develop strategies for quick mental calculations with decimals.
  • Apply estimation to check the reasonableness of answers.

Introduction

Estimation is a key skill in mathematics and everyday life. It helps us quickly assess results and ensures our calculations are reasonable. With decimals, rounding and compatible numbers make estimation fast and effective.
