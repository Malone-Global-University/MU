Unit 11: Order of Operations

Master the rules of order of operations (PEMDAS) across whole numbers, negative numbers, fractions, decimals, and nested parentheses, with practical word problems.

Unit Summary

This unit builds fluency in order of operations, emphasizing the correct use of PEMDAS across number types and nested expressions. Students will gain confidence solving both numerical and word-based problems.
