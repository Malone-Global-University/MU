Lesson 5: Adding & Subtracting Signed Numbers

Learn to add and subtract positive and negative numbers using simple rules and examples.

Home Pre-Algebra Unit 1: Numbers & Operations Lesson 5: Adding & Subtracting Signed Numbers

Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches the rules for adding and subtracting signed numbers (numbers with + or ‚àí signs). Mastery of these rules is essential for solving equations and working with integers.

Topics Covered

  • Adding numbers with the same sign
  • Adding numbers with different signs
  • Subtracting signed numbers using the opposite
  • Examples and practice problems

Lesson Examples

Example 1: 7 + 3 = 10 (both positive)

Example 2: -5 + (-2) = -7 (both negative)

Example 3: 8 + (-3) = 5 (different signs)

Example 4: 4 - (-6) = 4 + 6 = 10

Practice Problems

  1. Simplify: 6 + (-4), -7 + 5, -3 + (-9)
  2. Compute: 10 - (-2), -5 - 3, -8 - (-6)
  3. Explain the rule for adding numbers with different signs in your own words.
  4. Give a real-world example involving positive and negative numbers.
