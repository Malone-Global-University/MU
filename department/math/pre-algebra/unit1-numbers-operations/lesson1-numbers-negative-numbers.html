Lesson 1: Numbers & Negative Numbers

Understand numbers, negative numbers, and the foundational concepts that support all future Pre-Algebra learning.

Home Pre-Algebra Unit 1: Numbers & Operations Lesson 1: Numbers & Negative Numbers

Lesson Overview

This lesson introduces the concept of numbers, including positive and negative numbers, and explains how they are used in basic arithmetic operations. You will also learn how to represent numbers on a number line and understand their absolute value.

Topics Covered

  • Understanding numbers: whole numbers, integers, and negative numbers
  • Number line representation
  • Absolute value
  • Basic addition and subtraction with negative numbers

Lesson Examples

Example 1: Represent -3 and 5 on a number line.

Example 2: Calculate -4 + 7 and 5 - 9.

Practice Problems

  1. Write the following numbers on a number line: -2, 0, 4, -5.
  2. Find the absolute value of -8 and 6.
  3. Solve: -3 + 5, -7 + 2, 4 - 9.
  4. Explain why the sum of a negative and positive number can be positive or negative.
