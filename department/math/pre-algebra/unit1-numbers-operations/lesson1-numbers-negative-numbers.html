Lesson 1: Numbers and Negative Numbers

Understanding positive and negative numbers, their placement, and their real-world context.

Pre-Algebra › Unit 1: Numbers & Operations › Lesson 1: Numbers and Negative Numbers

Lesson Content

Numbers are symbols used to represent quantities. Negative numbers represent values less than zero and are used in contexts such as temperatures below zero, debts, or elevations below sea level.

  • Positive numbers: 1, 2, 3, …
  • Negative numbers: -1, -2, -3, …
  • Zero (0) separates positive and negative numbers.

Practice Exercises

  • Identify which numbers are positive and which are negative: -5, 0, 12, -9
  • Draw a number line and place the following numbers: -3, 2, 0, -1, 5
