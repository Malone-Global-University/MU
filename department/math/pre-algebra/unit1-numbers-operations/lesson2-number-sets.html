Lesson 2: Number Sets

Explore the different types of numbers: natural numbers, whole numbers, integers, rational numbers, and irrational numbers.

Pre-Algebra › Unit 1: Numbers & Operations › Lesson 2: Number Sets

Lesson Content

  • Natural Numbers: 1, 2, 3, …
  • Whole Numbers: 0, 1, 2, 3, …
  • Integers: …, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, …
  • Rational Numbers: fractions and integers that can be expressed as a/b
  • Irrational Numbers: numbers that cannot be expressed as fractions (e.g., √2, π)

Practice Exercises

  • Classify the following numbers into their sets: 0, 7, -3, 1/2, π
  • List five integers between -5 and 5
