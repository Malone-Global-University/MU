Unit 9: Variables & Expressions

In this unit, you will learn to work with variables, write and evaluate expressions, combine like terms, and solve word problems using algebraic thinking.

Unit Summary

This unit introduces variables and expressions, guiding students from understanding variable concepts to writing, simplifying, and evaluating expressions, and applying these skills to real-world word problems. Mastery here builds a foundation for algebra and future math courses.
