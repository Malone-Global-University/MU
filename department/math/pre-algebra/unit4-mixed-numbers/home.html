Unit Summary

This unit builds fluency with improper fractions and mixed numbers while strengthening conversion skills between fractions, decimals, and percents. You will perform operations with mixed numbers and apply these concepts to measurement and real-world word problems. Mastery of this unit is essential for algebraic reasoning and proportional thinking in higher-level mathematics.
