Unit Summary

This unit builds a strong foundation in exponent and radical operations. Students learn exponent laws, how to work with negative and fractional exponents, simplify radical expressions, and apply these skills to multi-step and real-world problems. Mastery of this unit prepares students for algebra and advanced mathematical reasoning.
