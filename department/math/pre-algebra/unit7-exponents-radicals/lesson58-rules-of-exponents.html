Lesson Description

This lesson introduces the fundamental rules of exponents that allow us to simplify expressions efficiently without writing out repeated multiplication.

Learning Objectives

  • Apply the product rule of exponents.
  • Apply the quotient rule of exponents.
  • Apply the power of a power rule.

Topics Covered

  • Product rule
  • Quotient rule
  • Power of a power

Key Terms

  • Product Rule: Add exponents when multiplying same bases.
  • Quotient Rule: Subtract exponents when dividing same bases.
  • Power of a Power: Multiply exponents when raising a power to another power.
