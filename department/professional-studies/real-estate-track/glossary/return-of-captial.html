Return of Capital

Real Estate Investing Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Professional Studies Real Estate Track Glossary Return of Capital

Definition

Return of capital is the repayment of an investor’s original invested principal. It is not profit, yield, or gain — it is simply money being returned to the investor that was previously invested.

Return of Capital vs. Return on Capital

  • Return of Capital: Repayment of original investment.
  • Return on Capital: Profit generated from the investment.

Confusing these two concepts leads to inaccurate performance analysis.

Where It Appears in Real Estate

  • Refinance proceeds distributed to investors
  • Partial sale of property
  • Capital events in joint ventures
  • Preferred equity structures
  • Liquidation waterfalls

Tax Implications

Return of capital typically reduces an investor’s tax basis in the investment rather than being taxed as income. However, once basis is reduced to zero, additional distributions may become taxable.

Example

An investor contributes $100,000 to a multifamily acquisition. Two years later, the property is refinanced, and $60,000 is distributed back to the investor.

  • $60,000 = Return of capital
  • Remaining basis = $40,000

Any further distributions beyond basis may be treated differently for tax purposes.

Role in Capital Stack Structures

In many LLC operating agreements, return of capital occurs before promote structures or profit splits activate. It is often prioritized in distribution waterfalls.

Strategic Use

  • De-risk investment while maintaining upside exposure
  • Recycle capital into new acquisitions
  • Improve portfolio liquidity
  • Enhance investor IRR through early principal recovery

Common Investor Mistakes

  • Counting return of capital as profit
  • Ignoring basis reduction
  • Misunderstanding refinance distributions
  • Failing to read waterfall provisions

Investor Checklist

  • Distinguish clearly between capital and profit.
  • Track adjusted basis annually.
  • Review refinance distribution terms carefully.
  • Understand waterfall priorities before investing.

Strategic Insight

Return of capital reduces risk. Early recovery of principal strengthens portfolio durability and improves long-term compounding power.

Related Terms

  • Capital stack
  • Preferred return
  • Waterfall distribution
  • Internal rate of return (IRR)
  • Limited Liability Company (LLC)
