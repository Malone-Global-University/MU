Department of Religion

Explore Abrahamic, Dharmic, Esoteric, Pagan, and Indigenous traditions with a global perspective.

Home Department Religion

Religion Tracks

  • Abrahamic Traditions

    Study Judaism, Christianity, and Islam, exploring theology, history, and cultural influence.

  • African Traditional Religions

    Explore indigenous African spiritual systems, rituals, cosmology, and cultural heritage.

  • East Asian Religions

    Learn about Confucianism, Taoism, Shinto, and other East Asian philosophical and religious systems.

  • Dharmic Traditions

    Explore Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism, focusing on philosophy, ethics, and practices.

  • Esoteric Traditions

    Study mysticism, occultism, alchemy, and other hidden spiritual practices.

  • Indigenous Religions

    Learn about spiritual systems of indigenous peoples worldwide, emphasizing tradition and ecology.

  • Pagan Traditions

    Study polytheistic, neopagan, and contemporary Pagan movements across history and cultures.
