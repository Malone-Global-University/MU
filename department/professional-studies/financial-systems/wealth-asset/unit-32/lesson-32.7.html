Wealth & Asset Operations Track • Unit 32: Operational Reporting and Performance Management

Lesson 32.7: Performance Integrity, Metric Failures, and Continuous Control Improvement

Learn how operational reporting functions as a control system across metrics, reconciliation tracking, timelines, error rates, dashboards, and management reporting. This capstone lesson shows how performance failures propagate into service degradation and control gaps, and how monitoring, analysis, escalation, and improvement cycles form a closed loop system ensuring operational efficiency and performance integrity.
