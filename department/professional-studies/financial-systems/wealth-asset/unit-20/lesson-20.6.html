Wealth & Asset Operations Track • Unit 20: Cash Movement and Payment Processing

Lesson 20.6: Fraud Risks in Cash Movement

Identify the fraud risks embedded in cash movement workflows and examine the verification, authorization, and escalation controls used to prevent unauthorized transfers. Because cash movement is one of the highest risk operational areas in wealth management, firms must detect impersonation attempts, instruction changes, account takeover behavior, and social engineering before funds are released.
