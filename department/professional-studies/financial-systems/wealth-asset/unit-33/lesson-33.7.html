Wealth & Asset Operations Track • Unit 33: Vendor, Custodian, and Platform Relationship Management

Lesson 33.7: Vendor Integrity, Third Party Failures, and Governance Control

Learn how vendor and custodian management operates as a unified control system across relationships, administrators, technology providers, service levels, risk management, and performance monitoring. This capstone lesson shows how third party failures propagate into operational and service risk, and how oversight, escalation, remediation, and governance frameworks form a closed loop system ensuring accountability and control integrity.
