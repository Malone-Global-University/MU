Wealth & Asset Operations Track • Unit 28: Operational Risk and Incident Management

Lesson 28.7: Incident Response Integrity, Failure Propagation, and Risk Control

Learn how operational risk management functions as a unified control system across risk identification, incident logging, root cause analysis, continuity planning, recovery systems, and risk monitoring. This capstone lesson shows how failures propagate into disruptions and control breakdowns, and how detection, escalation, remediation, and feedback loops form a closed loop system ensuring operational resilience and risk control integrity.
