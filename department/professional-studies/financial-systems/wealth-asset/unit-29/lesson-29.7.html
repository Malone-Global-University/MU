Wealth & Asset Operations Track • Unit 29: Fraud Prevention, Cybersecurity, and Access Controls

Lesson 29.7: Security Integrity, Threat Propagation, and Control Enforcement

Learn how fraud prevention, cybersecurity, and access controls operate as a unified control system across risk identification, access management, authentication, monitoring, and incident response. This capstone lesson shows how security failures propagate into unauthorized access and data exposure, and how detection, containment, remediation, and audit processes form a closed loop system ensuring system integrity and asset protection.
