Wealth & Asset Operations Track • Unit 30: Operations Team Structure and Functional Roles

Lesson 30.7: Operational Alignment, Role Dependency Failures, and Control Coordination

Learn how operations team structures function as an integrated system across front, middle, and back office roles, accounting, service teams, and organizational design. This capstone lesson shows how breakdowns in role coordination propagate into processing errors and service failures, and how communication, escalation, and oversight form a closed loop system ensuring alignment, accountability, and operational control integrity.
