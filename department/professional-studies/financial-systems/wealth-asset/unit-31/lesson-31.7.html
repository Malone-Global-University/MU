Wealth & Asset Operations Track • Unit 31: Front to Back Operational Coordination

Lesson 31.7: Workflow Integrity, Bottleneck Propagation, and Coordination Control

Learn how front to back operations function as a unified workflow system across trade lifecycle coordination, advisor interaction, portfolio management, escalation, communication, and dependencies. This capstone lesson shows how bottlenecks and breakdowns propagate into delays and errors, and how monitoring, escalation, resolution, and feedback form a closed loop system ensuring workflow continuity and operational integrity.
