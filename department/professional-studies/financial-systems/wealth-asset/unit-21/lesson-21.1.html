Lesson 21.1: Trade Instruction Flow

Understand how trade instructions originate from portfolio decisions and move through operational systems to execution. This lesson introduces the full trade instruction lifecycle — including how orders are created, routed, validated, allocated, and tracked across order management systems, trading desks, and downstream processing environments — forming the foundation for all portfolio implementation activity.
