Wealth & Asset Operations Track • Unit 21: Trade Support and Portfolio Implementation

Lesson 21.7: Post-Trade Adjustments

Study how trade activity is corrected and refined after execution to maintain accurate portfolio and client records. This capstone lesson brings together the full trade lifecycle by examining how errors are identified, adjustments are processed, allocations are updated, and cancel-and-rebill workflows are executed — ensuring that final records across systems reflect the true economic outcome of portfolio implementation.
