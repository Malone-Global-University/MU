Wealth & Asset Operations Track • Unit 34: Governance, Audit Readiness, and Institutional Oversight

Lesson 34.7: Institutional Integrity, Control Failures, and Oversight Enforcement

Learn how governance and oversight operate as a unified control system across committees, policies, audits, regulatory examinations, and documentation. This capstone lesson shows how control failures propagate into compliance risk and institutional exposure, and how monitoring, audit, enforcement, and accountability frameworks form a closed loop system ensuring transparency, control integrity, and organizational oversight.
