Our Mission

To cultivate engineers who build responsibly, innovate sustainably, and think independently. The department promotes cross-disciplinary education by uniting physics, art, and design to prepare students for real-world challenges. Inspired by innovators like Lewis Latimer, Granville Woods, and Patricia Bath, we aim to restore engineering to its rightful purpose: service to humanity.
