Welcome to the Engineering Department

The Engineering Department at Malone Global University is dedicated to advancing human progress through design, experimentation, and problem-solving. Our courses draw inspiration from history’s greatest African American inventors — pioneers who reshaped transportation, communication, and technology through brilliance and perseverance. Here, students learn not only how things work, but why ethical innovation matters.

Featured Pages

  • African American Inventors

    Explore the inventors, engineers, and innovators whose work advanced American industry, technology, infrastructure, medicine, and scientific development across multiple eras.

  • Mechanical Engineering

    Study the design and analysis of machines, mechanisms, thermodynamic systems, and mechanical structures used throughout industrial and engineered environments.

  • Electrical Engineering

    Examine electrical systems, circuit design, power distribution, electronics, signal processing, and the technologies that drive modern electrical infrastructure.

  • Civil & Structural Engineering

    Learn how engineers design, construct, and maintain buildings, bridges, roads, foundations, and public infrastructure systems.

  • Computer & Software Engineering

    Study the engineering of computational systems including software architecture, hardware integration, embedded systems, and digital infrastructure.

  • Aerospace & Systems Design

    Explore aircraft, spacecraft, propulsion systems, control systems, and the integrated engineering disciplines required for advanced aerospace design.

Our Mission

To cultivate engineers who build responsibly, innovate sustainably, and think independently. The department promotes cross-disciplinary education by uniting physics, art, and design to prepare students for real-world challenges. Inspired by innovators like Lewis Latimer, Granville Woods, and Patricia Bath, we aim to restore engineering to its rightful purpose: service to humanity.
