About Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering program at Malone Global University trains students to understand, design, and optimize systems that transmit and transform energy and information. From circuits and microprocessors to renewable energy systems, our curriculum emphasizes creativity, precision, and responsibility. We explore how electrical power shapes civilization ‚Äî and how engineers must ensure that power remains a tool for human upliftment.

Core Areas of Study

  • Circuit Theory & Electronics: The foundation of all modern devices ‚Äî understanding current, voltage, and signal flow.
  • Power Systems: Study of generation, distribution, and efficient energy management across networks and cities.
  • Digital Systems & Microcontrollers: Programming and designing the logic behind smart machines.
  • Communications Engineering: How data, voice, and images move across the globe through electrical signals.
  • Renewable Energy & Sustainability: Building systems that draw power from nature, not at its expense.
