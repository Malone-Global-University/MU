Control Systems

Control Systems studies automated feedback mechanisms used to regulate machines, industrial processes, vehicles, and dynamic systems. Students learn how systems sense conditions, compare outputs to desired states, and apply corrective action to maintain stability and accuracy.

  • Feedback Control

    Study closed-loop systems that automatically adjust behavior based on measured output and target conditions.

  • PID Controllers

    Learn proportional, integral, and derivative control methods used in practical industrial automation systems.

  • System Stability

    Examine how engineers evaluate whether control systems remain stable under changing inputs and disturbances.

  • Industrial Automation

    Explore automated process control in factories, plants, robotics, and industrial machinery environments.
