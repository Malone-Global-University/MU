Power Systems

Power Systems studies the generation, transmission, distribution, and management of electrical power across modern infrastructure. Students examine how electricity moves from generation sources to end users through complex grid and utility networks.

  • Power Generation

    Study how electrical power is produced through fossil fuel, nuclear, hydroelectric, renewable, and alternative generation systems.

  • Power Transmission

    Examine how high-voltage transmission lines move electricity efficiently across long distances.

  • Power Distribution

    Learn how substations, transformers, and local grids deliver usable electricity to consumers and facilities.

  • Grid Management

    Explore load balancing, grid stability, redundancy, and operational control across large-scale electrical networks.
