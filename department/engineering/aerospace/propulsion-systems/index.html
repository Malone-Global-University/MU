Propulsion Systems

Propulsion Systems studies the mechanisms used to generate thrust and move aircraft, spacecraft, and high-speed vehicles. Students learn how engines convert stored energy into directed force through combustion, expansion, exhaust flow, and reaction mechanics.

  • Jet Engines

    Study turbojets, turbofans, turboprops, and gas turbine propulsion systems used in atmospheric flight.

  • Rocket Propulsion

    Examine rocket engines, propellant systems, nozzle design, and thrust generation in vacuum-capable propulsion systems.

  • Combustion Systems

    Learn how controlled combustion produces the thermal expansion and pressure needed for propulsion.

  • Propulsion Performance

    Explore thrust efficiency, fuel consumption, specific impulse, and performance optimization across propulsion platforms.
