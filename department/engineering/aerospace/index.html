About the Department

The Aerospace & Systems Design Engineering Department at Malone Global University trains students in the art and science of flight, space systems, and robotics. We combine theoretical knowledge with experimental design — from aerodynamics and propulsion to autonomous control and mission systems integration. Our goal is to equip future engineers to push humanity’s reach while maintaining responsibility to Earth and each other.

Our Mission

To advance humanity’s frontier of knowledge through flight and systems innovation grounded in ethics and precision. We believe aerospace engineering represents the highest synthesis of art, mathematics, and vision — and that each design must serve not only the mission, but life itself.
