Systems Engineering

Systems Engineering studies the structured design and management of complex integrated engineering systems composed of multiple interacting subsystems. Students learn how large multidisciplinary projects are planned, coordinated, optimized, and validated from concept through deployment.

  • Requirements Engineering

    Study how system requirements are defined, structured, validated, and traced throughout development.

  • System Architecture

    Examine high-level system decomposition, interface design, and subsystem integration planning.

  • Integration and Testing

    Learn how complex systems are assembled, verified, validated, and tested across development stages.

  • Lifecycle Management

    Explore system planning, sustainment, maintenance, upgrades, and end-of-life engineering considerations.
