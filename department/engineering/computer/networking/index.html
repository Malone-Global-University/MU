Networking Engineering

Networking Engineering studies the infrastructure and protocols that enable digital communication between systems. Students learn how data moves across local and global networks through switching, routing, transmission media, and distributed architectures.

  • Network Protocols

    Study the rules and standards governing data communication across interconnected digital systems.

  • Routing and Switching

    Examine how network devices direct, segment, and manage data traffic across networked infrastructure.

  • Network Infrastructure

    Learn the design and deployment of physical and virtual network architecture supporting communication systems.

  • Distributed Systems Networking

    Explore communication models and infrastructure for distributed, cloud, and large-scale networked systems.
