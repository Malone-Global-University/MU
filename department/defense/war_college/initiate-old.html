Course Overview

The Initiate Tier is the entry point for those who wish to understand power at its roots. Students learn historical patterns of governance, core Malone Doctrine concepts, and practical habits of leadership. This tier establishes the intellectual and moral foundation required to progress to Strategist and Commander levels.

Learning Objectives

  • Grasp the six-stage model of government evolution and its implications for strategy.
  • Internalize Malone values: integrity, transparency, militant truth-to-power, and results.
  • Develop basic organizational, rhetorical, and strategic habits for effective leadership.
  • Begin building a personal plan for sovereignty: financial, social, and intellectual.

Core Modules

  • MD101 — Evolution of Government Systems
  • MD102 — Power, Responsibility & Executive Authority
  • MD103 — Narrative Warfare (Intro)
  • MD104 — Betrayal, Revelation & the Revolutionary Path

Each module contains readings, short exercises, and a practical assignment designed to convert theory into immediate capability.

Starter Assignments

  • Reflective Essay: Describe a personal moment that shaped your view of power and responsibility.
  • Short Analysis: Pick one stage of government evolution and provide two modern examples that fit it.
  • Practical: Draft a 30-day plan to strengthen one pillar of your sovereignty (finance, food, learning, or network).

Requirements & Format

Initiate Tier is self-paced and open-access. No prior coursework required. Students are expected to read assigned texts, complete short written exercises, and participate in optional discussion channels for feedback.
