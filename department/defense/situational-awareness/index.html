Situational Awareness Division

**Perception. Analysis. Decision.**

Home Directory Situational Awareness Division

The Situational Awareness Division develops the ability to observe, interpret, and respond to environments in real time.

This is the foundation of all defensive and operational capability.

Purpose

The purpose of this division is to train individuals to:

  • Recognize threats before they escalate
  • Maintain awareness across environments
  • Make informed decisions under pressure
  • Reduce uncertainty through observation

Core Areas of Study

Environmental Awareness

  • Reading surroundings
  • Identifying anomalies
  • Understanding baseline behavior

Threat Detection

  • Pre-incident indicators
  • Behavioral analysis
  • Risk signaling

Decision-Making

  • Rapid assessment
  • Prioritization
  • Action selection

Operating Principles

  • Awareness prevents escalation
  • Early detection creates options
  • Clarity enables control

Program Tracks

  • Foundational Awareness Training
  • Threat Detection & Analysis
  • Advanced Decision Systems
