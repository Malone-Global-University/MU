Directory of Departments

  • Malone Doctrine

    A strategic and philosophical body of work defining the operating principles, values, and long-term objectives of Malone Global University. This section examines power structures, sovereignty, institutional resilience, and the ethical framework guiding decision-making. Students engage with doctrine as both theory and application, translating abstract policy positions into actionable strategic posture.

  • Martial Arts

    A practical discipline focused on close-quarters combat, physical conditioning, and applied self-defense systems. Training emphasizes efficiency of movement, control under pressure, and adaptability across unpredictable environments. The curriculum integrates traditional martial systems with modern combatives, prioritizing real-world effectiveness over sport-oriented techniques.

  • Private Security

    A professional track centered on independent security operations, risk mitigation, and client protection services. Students develop competencies in contract-based security work, protective detail planning, surveillance, and operational logistics. The program highlights decentralized security models and prepares individuals to operate effectively outside conventional institutional frameworks.

  • Situational Awareness

    A cognitive and observational training program designed to improve threat detection, environmental analysis, and decision-making under uncertainty. Coursework focuses on pattern recognition, behavioral analysis, and real-time risk assessment across both physical and digital environments. The objective is to cultivate continuous awareness and rapid response capability in dynamic conditions.

  • War College

    An advanced study of leadership, strategy, and large-scale operational planning. This section explores military doctrine, historical campaign analysis, asymmetric warfare, and command decision-making under complex constraints. Students develop the ability to think in systems, coordinate multi-layered operations, and execute strategy across tactical and strategic levels.
