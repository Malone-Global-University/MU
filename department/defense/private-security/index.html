Private Security Division

**Independent Protection. Professional Execution.**

Home Directory Private Security Division

The Private Security Division prepares individuals to operate as independent security professionals in civilian environments.

This includes planning, risk management, and real-world operational conduct.

Purpose

The purpose of this division is to train individuals to:

  • Protect people, assets, and information
  • Operate independently with accountability
  • Assess and mitigate risk in real time
  • Execute security functions professionally

Core Areas of Study

Risk Assessment

  • Threat identification
  • Vulnerability analysis
  • Environmental awareness

Protective Operations

  • Personal security
  • Asset protection
  • Movement and escort procedures

Professional Conduct

  • Legal awareness
  • Ethical engagement
  • Client responsibility

Operational Focus

  • Prevention over reaction
  • Presence as deterrence
  • Structured response when required
