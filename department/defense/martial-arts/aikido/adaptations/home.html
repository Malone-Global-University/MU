Adaptations

This page documents how aikido principles are reconstructed into pressure-tested applications within Malone Ryu.

Striking Integration

Entry timing and off-balancing are synchronized with strikes to force reaction rather than rely on compliance.

Clinch Transition

Techniques are modified to survive contact range, prioritizing head position, underhooks, and base control.

Live Resistance Application

Techniques are treated as probabilistic tools rather than fixed outcomes.

Core Principle

If it cannot function under resistance, it is reclassified as a concept rather than a technique.

Reintegration Model

  • Extract viable joint control mechanics
  • Convert redirection into clinch entries
  • Merge with striking pressure systems

Key Rule

Only components that survive live resistance are retained. Everything else is discarded or restructured.
