Aikido Overview

System Context • Functional Interpretation

Definition

Aikido is analyzed here as a system of mechanical principles focused on timing, balance disruption, and directional redirection of force.

Framework Position

Within MGU, Aikido is not preserved as tradition. It is decomposed, tested, and reintegrated into live resistance systems.

Core Use

  • Study of off-balancing mechanics
  • Timing and entry development
  • Structural control analysis
