Karate

Striking • Bunkai • Discipline

Home Department Defense Martial Arts Karate

Welcome

Karate within this system is used as a primary vehicle for developing striking capability under resistance. It emphasizes timing, distance, and impact through live engagement rather than static form alone.

Purpose

The purpose of this division is to:

  • Develop effective striking under resistance
  • Train timing, distance, and positional control
  • Build durability through live sparring
  • Integrate striking into multi-range combat

Core Functions

Striking Development

  • Train punches, kicks, and combinations
  • Develop power generation and delivery
  • Refine accuracy under movement

Live Application

  • Conduct controlled and full sparring
  • Apply techniques against resisting opponents
  • Train adaptability under pressure

Conditioning & Durability

  • Build physical resilience to impact
  • Develop endurance under fatigue
  • Train recovery and composure

Operating Principles

  • Striking must be validated through contact
  • Timing and distance determine effectiveness
  • Technique must survive fatigue and resistance

Program Structure

  • Level I: Foundations
  • Level II: Application
  • Level III: Advanced

Role in the Defense System

Karate functions as the primary striking engine within the Defense system, providing the ability to engage, disrupt, and control opponents through force and timing.

Related Departments
