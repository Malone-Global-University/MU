Welcome to the Martial Arts Library!

This library exists to centralize basic information that applies across many domains of martial arts. The goal is to reduce redundancy and keep canoncial reference points centrally located. Currently the library houses information specific to Japanese martial arts to make the study of aikido and karate under the MaloneRyu system easier. In time the library will grow as new arts are added. “All martial knowledge is theory; the library simply organizes different ways of understanding the same underlying reality.”

Topics

The Martial Arts Library organizes concepts shared across Japanese martial arts into semantic layers. Topics are organized as one connected system of martial ideas, grouped by how they are used and experienced.

How to Use This Library

Each concept exists as part of a connected system. No concept is isolated. Ma-ai connects to timing. Kuzushi connects to structure. Shuhari connects to all training methods.

The library is designed to be referenced during training, not read passively.
