The Doctrine

The Malone Doctrine is a war manuscript, spiritual compass, and constitutional framework that is the canonical text of Malone War College. Instruction is centered on how to implement the various tactics contained within. It defines power as the right to lead and the duty to protect. It outlines the six phases of centralized government and prescribes new governance blueprints to replace broken regimes. This is where leaders are built.
