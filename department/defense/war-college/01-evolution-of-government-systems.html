MD101: Evolution of Government Systems

Home Department Defense War College Curriculum MD101: Evolution of Government Systems

Overview

Track the six phases of centralized government from tribalism to democracy. Understand how each system emerged, consolidated power, and ultimately declined or transformed.

Prolusion

Governments are not accidents, they are designs. From tribal clans to divine monarchies each phase of rule was born from conflict, consolidation, and necessity. This course examines the evolutionary logic behind centralized systems, showing how they rise and fall in response to pressure, ideology, and strategic imperatives.
