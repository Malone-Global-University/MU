The Malone Doctrine

Strategic Framework for African American Autonomy, Protection, and Advancement

Overview

The Malone Doctrine is a codified collection of the political, social, economic, ethical, and strategic teachings of Joseph Malone. It serves as his governing platform for African American family restoration, community stabilization, institutional development, collective protection, political advancement, and economic independence.

The Doctrine does not function as a textbook or conventional academic curriculum. Its purpose is to establish positions, define standards of conduct, identify community problems, prescribe corrective policies, and articulate a long-term program for African American revival. Its volumes organize these teachings into a coherent body of doctrine that can guide individuals, families, community organizations, political institutions, and future Malone-aligned initiatives.
