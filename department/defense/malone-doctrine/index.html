The Malone Doctrine

Strategic Framework for African American Autonomy, Protection, and Advancement

Home Department Defense The Malone Doctrine

Volumes

The Malone Doctrine establishes clear, principled stances on the political challenges facing African American communities. It serves as the university’s official reference for teaching, research, defense strategy, and public communication.

The Malone Doctrine is a four volume collection of political treatises written by Malone Party Founder Joseph Malone. The goal is to provide ethics and code of conduct requirements for all members of the Malone Party.

  • Malone Doctrine: Volume I

    Volume 1 deals with ethics based community engagement

  • Malone Doctrine: Volume II

    Volume 2 deals with ethics based statecraft

  • Malone Doctrine: Volume III

    Volume 3 deals with ethics based warcraft

  • Malone Doctrine: Volume IV

    Volume 4 deals with ethics based economic engineering

Guiding Principles

  • Self-Determination
  • Community Autonomy
  • Economic Empowerment
  • Cultural Continuity
  • Evidence-Based Reasoning
  • Ethical Governance & Accountability
