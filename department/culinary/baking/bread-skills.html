5. Shaping Techniques

Students learn:

  • Pre-shaping
  • Bench rest
  • Final shaping
  • Tension building on dough surface
  • Rolling cylinders for sandwich loaves
  • Boule (round) shaping
  • Batard shaping
  • Baguette shaping (advanced)
  • Braiding (for challah & similar breads)

  • Timing of scoring relative to bake

8. Baking Techniques

Students learn:

  • Steam baking (home & professional)
  • Dutch oven baking
  • Deck oven basics
  • Temperature zoning
  • Oven spring control
  • Internal temperature & doneness
  • Managing crust color
  • Adjusting baking times for enriched vs lean doughs

9. Cooling & Storage

Students learn:

  • Proper cooling racks
  • Staling science
  • Storing crusty vs soft breads
  • Freezing & reheating bread

11. Evaluation & Sensory Skills

Students learn to evaluate:

  • Crumb structure (open, irregular, uniform)
  • Crust quality
  • Aroma
  • Flavor development from fermentation
  • Texture analysis

12. Troubleshooting Skills

Students learn how to fix:

  • Dense loaves
  • Poor oven spring
  • Overproofed dough
  • Shaping collapse
  • Lack of browning
  • Too much/too little gluten
  • Poor fermentation
