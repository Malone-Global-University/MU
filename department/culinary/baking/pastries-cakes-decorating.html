Overview

In this module, students learn to create a variety of pastries and cakes while mastering decorating techniques. Emphasis is placed on both taste and presentation, with practical exercises to enhance creativity and precision.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the techniques behind classic and modern pastries.
  • Develop cake baking skills for layered, sheet, and specialty cakes.
  • Learn essential cake decorating techniques including icing, piping, and fondant work.
  • Apply flavor combinations and textures to enhance desserts.
  • Create visually appealing desserts suitable for professional presentation.

Topics Covered

  1. Pastry Doughs: Shortcrust, Puff, Choux, and Phyllo
  2. Cake Fundamentals: Layered, Sheet, and Specialty Cakes
  3. Frosting and Icing Techniques: Buttercream, Ganache, and Glazes
  4. Decorating Tools and Methods: Piping, Fondant, and Stenciling
  5. Advanced Techniques: Sugar Work, Edible Flowers, and Sculpting
  6. Presentation and Plating of Desserts
  7. Troubleshooting Common Pastry Issues

Assignments & Practice

  • Bake a layered cake and apply at least two different frosting techniques.
  • Create a batch of pastries (e.g., eclairs, tarts) and document dough handling and baking results.
  • Design a decorated dessert plate, integrating color, texture, and presentation principles.
