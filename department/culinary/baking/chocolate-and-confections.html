Overview

This module introduces the techniques and principles behind chocolate and confectionery. Students will learn tempering methods, chocolate molding, and the creation of candies, truffles, and other confections suitable for professional presentation.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the science of chocolate and cocoa processing.
  • Master tempering techniques for smooth, glossy chocolate.
  • Create molded chocolates, truffles, and filled confections.
  • Learn candy-making techniques, including caramels, nougat, and pralines.
  • Apply decorative and finishing techniques for professional presentation.

Topics Covered

  1. Introduction to Chocolate: Types, Quality, and Storage
  2. Tempering Chocolate: Methods and Troubleshooting
  3. Molding and Shaping Chocolate
  4. Truffles, Ganaches, and Filled Chocolates
  5. Candy Techniques: Caramels, Nougat, Fudge, and Pralines
  6. Edible Decorations and Garnishes
  7. Presentation Techniques for Chocolate & Confections

Assignments & Practice

  • Temper and mold a batch of chocolate using proper techniques.
  • Create truffles with flavored ganache fillings and decorative finishes.
  • Experiment with one type of candy (e.g., caramel or praline) and document the process.
