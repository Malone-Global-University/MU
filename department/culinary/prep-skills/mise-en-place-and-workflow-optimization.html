Module Overview

This module equips students with foundational mise en place skills, emphasizing ingredient organization, workstation setup, and workflow optimization to maximize efficiency during service.

Core Learning Objectives

  • Understand and implement professional mise en place principles.
  • Organize ingredients, tools, and equipment for optimal efficiency.
  • Design workflow sequences for multi-station and multi-dish preparation.
  • Ensure consistency, speed, and accuracy in prep activities.
  • Adapt mise en place techniques for varying kitchen environments.

Practical Competencies

  • Set up workstations with all necessary tools, equipment, and ingredients for service.
  • Label, measure, and arrange ingredients for efficient access during cooking.
  • Implement time-saving workflow strategies to maintain pace under service pressure.
  • Coordinate with team members to ensure smooth handoffs and minimized downtime.
  • Evaluate and adjust prep setups for various menu items and kitchen layouts.
