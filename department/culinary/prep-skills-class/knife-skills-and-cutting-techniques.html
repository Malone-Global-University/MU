Enhance precision, speed, and safety in the kitchen with advanced knife handling, cutting, and vegetable and protein preparation techniques.

Module Overview

This module equips students with professional knife handling and cutting techniques necessary for precise, efficient, and safe preparation of ingredients. Techniques cover classical and contemporary cuts, knife maintenance, and workflow integration.

Core Learning Objectives

  • Demonstrate safe handling and control of various culinary knives.
  • Perform fundamental cuts: dice, chop, mince, julienne, batonnet, and chiffonade.
  • Apply advanced cuts for vegetables, fruits, and proteins.
  • Maintain knives with proper sharpening, honing, and storage.
  • Integrate cutting techniques into professional kitchen workflows efficiently.

Practical Competencies

  • Execute a range of precision cuts consistently with speed and accuracy.
  • Sharpen and hone knives to maintain optimal cutting performance.
  • Apply ergonomic techniques to reduce fatigue and risk of injury.
  • Prepare ingredients to standardized sizes for consistent cooking and presentation.
  • Organize workstations to integrate cutting tasks seamlessly into culinary production.
