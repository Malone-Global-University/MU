Module Overview

This module teaches students to identify, select, and store ingredients correctly, emphasizing freshness, seasonality, and quality standards to support professional culinary preparation.

Core Learning Objectives

  • Identify common and specialty ingredients used in professional kitchens.
  • Assess ingredient quality, ripeness, and suitability for dishes.
  • Understand storage requirements for various perishable and non-perishable items.
  • Apply selection techniques to enhance flavor, texture, and presentation.
  • Adapt ingredient choices based on availability, seasonality, and menu design.

Practical Competencies

  • Inspect and select fruits, vegetables, meats, seafood, and dry goods for quality and freshness.
  • Practice proper storage and rotation to maintain ingredient integrity.
  • Evaluate suppliers and sourcing options to optimize ingredient procurement.
  • Demonstrate portioning and measuring techniques for accurate recipe execution.
  • Adjust ingredient substitutions without compromising dish quality or consistency.
