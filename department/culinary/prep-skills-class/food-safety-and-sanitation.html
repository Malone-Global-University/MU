Food Safety & Sanitation

Module Overview

This module introduces students to food safety standards, proper sanitation procedures, and contamination prevention. Students will develop a strong foundation in hygiene practices essential for professional kitchen operations.

Core Learning Objectives

  • Understand foodborne pathogens, cross-contamination, and risk management.
  • Apply proper sanitation, cleaning, and hygiene protocols.
  • Implement temperature control and storage best practices.
  • Maintain a clean and safe work environment at all times.
  • Follow regulatory guidelines and industry standards for food safety.

Practical Competencies

  • Conduct proper handwashing, glove use, and personal hygiene in culinary settings.
  • Sanitize work surfaces, utensils, and equipment following industry protocols.
  • Monitor and control refrigerator, freezer, and hot-holding temperatures.
  • Recognize and manage potential contamination sources, including allergens.
  • Apply cleaning schedules, logs, and hazard analysis procedures in real-world kitchen scenarios.
