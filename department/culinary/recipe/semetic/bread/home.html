About Semetic & Arabic Breads

Bread is central to Semetic food culture. From soft, pocketed pita to thin laffa, from ceremonial Jewish challah to traditional Arab khubz baked in taboon ovens, these breads connect daily life, ritual, and regional history.

Many of these breads are ancient, tied to agricultural traditions of wheat and barley, and shaped by the techniques of pastoral, urban, and desert communities across the Near East.
