About Semetic Side Dishes

Side dishes in Semetic cuisine are essential components of the meal, often built around vegetables, herbs, grains, legumes, olive oil, and sesame-based ingredients. These dishes accompany meats, breads, and stews across Jewish, Hebrew, Arab, and Levantine communities.

From world-famous dips like hummus and baba ghanoush to fresh salads like tabbouleh and Israeli salad, these sides add depth, brightness, and texture to traditional and modern meals.
