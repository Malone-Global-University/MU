About Semetic Sweets

Semetic sweets include the rich dessert traditions of Jewish, Hebrew, Arab, and Levantine cultures. These sweets often feature ingredients such as honey, dates, nuts, sesame, rosewater, citrus, semolina, and delicate doughs.

From festival pastries to everyday tea-time desserts, these confections showcase ancient culinary techniques and flavors shared across the Near East.
