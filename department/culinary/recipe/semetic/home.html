About Semetic (Jewish & Arabic) Recipes

Semetic cuisine represents a rich historical and cultural collection of culinary traditions from Jewish, Hebrew, Arab, Levantine, and other Near Eastern cultures. These traditions share deep historical connections, religious significance, and regional ingredients such as wheat, dates, chickpeas, olive oil, sesame, and lamb.

At MGU, this category brings together authentic classics and contemporary interpretations of dishes spanning Arabian Peninsula cooking, Levantine flavors, Jewish holiday meals, and ancient Semetic food culture. Recipes are presented with clarity for modern home cooks while preserving authenticity and cultural depth of religious customs, and generational cooking practices.
