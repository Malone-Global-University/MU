About Pastry Recipes

Pastry recipes cover sweet and savory baked goods including tarts, croissants, puff pastries, danishes, and turnovers. These recipes focus on techniques for flaky, buttery, and delicious pastries suitable for home bakers and aspiring pastry chefs.

Understanding dough handling, lamination, fillings, and baking temperatures is key to mastering pastry recipes at home.

Understanding Pastry Categories

Pastry is one of the core branches of baking. It includes laminated doughs, shortcrusts, choux (pronounced "shoe"), and enriched dough-based desserts. Students should understand the techniques behind gluten control, steam-based leavening, lamination, and dough temperature management.
