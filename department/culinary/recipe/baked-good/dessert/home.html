What Defines a Dessert?

Desserts encompass all sweet dishes served at the conclusion of a meal, ranging from baked items to chilled creams, custards, fruit preparations, and global sweets. While many desserts involve baking, others rely on stovetop cooking, emulsification, freezing, or delicate heat control. Understanding texture, sweetness, acidity, and plating is essential for mastering dessert craft.
