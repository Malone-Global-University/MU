Buttermilk Cornbread

Classic Southern-style buttermilk cornbread, golden on the outside and tender inside. Perfect for pairing with chili, soups, or as a hearty side for any meal.

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 2 large eggs
