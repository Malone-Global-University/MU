About Artisan Loaves

Artisan loaves are traditionally crafted breads made using slow fermentation, high-quality ingredients, and hands-on techniques. These breads emphasize flavor, texture, crust development, and natural fermentation processes.

Popular Artisan Bread Styles

  • Sourdough: Naturally leavened with a wild starter.
  • Baguette: Long, crusty, French-style bread.
  • Ciabatta: High hydration, open crumb Italian loaf.
  • Pain de Campagne: Rustic French country bread.
  • Focaccia: Olive oil-rich Italian flatbread.
