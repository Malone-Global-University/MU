About Specialty Loaves

Specialty loaves are traditionally crafted yeast breads that are artisanal quality that do not fit in other bread sections. This includes Molasses Bread, Cornbread, Cinnamon Rolls, Dried Fruit Breads, and seasonal holiday breads.

Popular Specialty Bread Styles

  • Coffee Cakes: Yeast-raised sweet breads topped with streusel, cinnamon sugar, or crumb layers; softer and richer than traditional cakes.
  • Fruit Mix-ins: Yeast loaves enriched with raisins, cranberries, dried cherries, orange peel, or mixed dried fruit for added sweetness and texture.
  • Garlic Breads: Knots, twists, and pull-apart loaves flavored with garlic butter and herbs, often served warm as savory specialty breads.
  • Rolls and Buns: Soft enriched doughs shaped into hamburger buns, hotdog buns, dinner rolls, hoagie rolls, and other structured sandwich breads.
  • Sweetened Breads: Molasses loaves, honey-enriched breads, tea loaves, and other lightly sweet yeast breads with deep flavor and soft crumb.
