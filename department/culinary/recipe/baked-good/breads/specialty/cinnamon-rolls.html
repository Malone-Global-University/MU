Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add sugar, melted butter, salt, eggs, and 2 cups of flour. Mix until smooth. Gradually add remaining flour until dough is soft but not sticky.
  3. Knead dough for 5-7 minutes until elastic. Cover and let rise 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Roll dough on a floured surface into a 16x12-inch rectangle. Spread softened butter over the surface.
  5. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle evenly over buttered dough. Roll up tightly and cut into 12 rolls.
  6. Place rolls in a greased 9x13-inch pan. Cover and let rise 30-45 minutes until puffy.
  7. Preheat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C). Bake rolls for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  8. While rolls bake, prepare frosting: beat cream cheese and butter until smooth. Add powdered sugar and vanilla; mix until creamy.
  9. Let rolls cool 5-10 minutes, then spread cream cheese frosting on top. Serve warm.

Recipe Notes

  • For extra fluffiness, use bread flour instead of all-purpose flour.
  • Rolls can be prepared the night before, covered in the fridge, then baked in the morning.
  • Add chopped nuts or raisins to the filling for variation.
