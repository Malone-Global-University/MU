Cinnamon Streusel Bread

Sweet, moist quick bread topped with a crunchy cinnamon-sugar streusel. Perfect for breakfast or snack time.

Overview

Cinnamon Streusel Bread is a moist, sweet quick bread with a rich cinnamon flavor and a buttery streusel topping. Ideal for breakfast, brunch, or a snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • For streusel topping: 1/3 cup brown sugar, 1 tsp cinnamon, 3 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  3. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla.
  4. Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients until just combined; do not overmix.
  5. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  6. Mix streusel ingredients and sprinkle evenly over the top.
  7. Bake 50â€“60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool in pan 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Tips

  • Do not overmix the batter to keep the bread light and tender.
  • For extra flavor, add 1/2 cup chopped nuts to the streusel topping.
  • Serve slightly warm for the best taste and texture.
