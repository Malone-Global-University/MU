Cinnamon Roll Breadtwists

Soft, sweet, and twisted breadsticks with cinnamon sugar and a creamy glaze. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or snack time.

Directions

  1. In a large bowl or electric stand mixer bowl, combine water, yeast, and sugar. Add 1 cup of flour and salt, mix well. Gradually add more flour until a soft, smooth dough forms. Knead for 2-3 minutes until the dough is soft and smooth.
  2. Let the dough rest for 10 minutes. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F (190Â°C) and grease a 12x18-inch baking sheet.
  3. Lightly grease a clean countertop and press the dough into an 8x18-inch rectangle. Spread melted butter over the dough.
  4. Mix granulated sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl, then sprinkle it over the dough and press lightly.
  5. Cut the dough into 1-inch strips, twist each strip a few times, and place them on the baking sheet.
  6. Cover the breadsticks with plastic wrap and let them rise at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes until lightly golden.
  8. Whisk powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla until smooth. Adjust milk for desired consistency.
  9. Let the breadsticks cool for 5 minutes, then drizzle with the glaze. Serve warm.

Notes & Tips

  • These breadstick twists can be reheated in the microwave for a few seconds for a fresh taste.
  • Use room-temperature ingredients for better yeast activation.
  • For extra flavor, add a pinch of nutmeg to the cinnamon sugar mixture.
