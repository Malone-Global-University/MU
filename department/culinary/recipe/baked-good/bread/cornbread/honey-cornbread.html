Honey Cornbread

Sweet and golden honey cornbread with a tender crumb. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a flavorful side with soups, chili, and roasted meats.

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 2 large eggs
