Skillet Cornbread

Skillet Cornbread is the classic Southern preparation of cornbread, baked in a hot cast-iron skillet to create a crisp, golden crust and a soft, tender interior. Simple, rustic, and deeply flavorful, this cornbread pairs beautifully with hearty meals like chili, BBQ meats, beans, and stews.
