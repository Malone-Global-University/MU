Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk or buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F (205Â°C). Grease a baking pan or cast-iron skillet.
  2. Combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a mixing bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk milk, melted butter, egg, and honey.
  4. Stir wet ingredients into the dry mixture until just combined.
  5. Pour batter into the prepared pan and bake 20â€“25 minutes until golden and soft.

Serving Suggestions

Enjoy sweet cornbread warm with honey butter, chili, fried chicken, barbecue, or as a dessert topped with cinnamon sugar.
