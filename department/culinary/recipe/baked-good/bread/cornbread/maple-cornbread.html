Maple Cornbread

Sweet and buttery maple cornbread with golden edges. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a flavorful side with chili, soups, or roasted meats.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1 cup milk or buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F (205Â°C) and grease a cast-iron skillet or baking pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In another bowl, whisk milk, melted butter, eggs, and maple syrup.
  4. Mix wet and dry ingredients until just combined.
  5. Pour batter into the prepared pan and bake 20â€“25 minutes until golden and firm.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve warm with butter or extra maple syrup drizzle.
  • Pairs beautifully with soups, chili, or roasted breakfast meats.
  • Store leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
