Cheddar Cornbread

This moist and cheesy Southern-style cornbread is simple, savory, and ready in under 30 minutes.

Home Culinary Recipe Baked Good Bread Cornbread Cheddar Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup milk or buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F (205Â°C). Grease a baking pan or cast-iron skillet.
  2. In a bowl, mix cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In another bowl, whisk milk, melted butter, and egg.
  4. Combine the wet and dry mixtures. Fold in the shredded cheddar.
  5. Pour the batter into your prepared pan and bake for 20â€“25 minutes, until golden and cooked through.

Serving Suggestions

Cheddar cornbread pairs perfectly with chili, barbecue, fried chicken, soup, or as a snack with honey butter.
