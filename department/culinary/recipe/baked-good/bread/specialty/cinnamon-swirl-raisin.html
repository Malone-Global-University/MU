Cinnamon Swirl Raisin Bread

Soft, sweet, and flavorful enriched bread with a cinnamon-sugar swirl and plump raisins throughout.

Overview

Cinnamon Swirl Raisin Bread is a classic enriched specialty bread, perfect for breakfast, brunch, or afternoon tea. The dough is soft and slightly sweet, enriched with butter and eggs, and rolled with a cinnamon-sugar mixture and plump raisins for a flavorful swirl.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar (for cinnamon swirl)
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Proof yeast in warm milk with a teaspoon of sugar until foamy, about 5 minutes.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Add eggs, butter, and yeast mixture. Knead until smooth.
  3. Let dough rise in a greased bowl until doubled, 1â€“2 hours.
  4. Roll dough into a rectangle, sprinkle with cinnamon and brown sugar, and scatter raisins evenly.
  5. Roll tightly and place in a greased loaf pan. Cover and let rise until doubled.
  6. Bake at 350Â°F (175Â°C) for 30â€“35 minutes or until golden brown and internal temp is 190Â°F (88Â°C).
  7. Cool before slicing to reveal the cinnamon swirl pattern.

Tips for Perfect Cinnamon Swirl Bread

  • Ensure dough is soft but not sticky; add flour gradually if needed.
  • Do not overfill with cinnamon sugar or raisins to prevent leakage.
  • Use room temperature butter and eggs for even mixing.
  • Cool completely before slicing to maintain the swirl integrity.
