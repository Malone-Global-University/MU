Overview

Apple Cinnamon Quick Bread is a sweet and moist loaf featuring chunks of fresh apples and warm cinnamon. This quick bread uses baking powder and/or baking soda for leavening, eliminating the need for yeast or long rising times, making it a convenient and flavorful choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or buttermilk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups peeled and diced apples (Granny Smith or your choice)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. In another bowl, beat eggs, sugar, melted butter, yogurt, and vanilla until combined.
  4. Fold dry ingredients into wet ingredients until just combined; do not overmix.
  5. Gently fold in the diced apples.
  6. Pour batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 50â€“60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool for 10 minutes in the pan, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Tips for Perfect Apple Cinnamon Quick Bread

  • Use slightly tart apples like Granny Smith to balance the sweetness.
  • Do not overmix batter to keep the bread tender.
  • Coat apple pieces lightly with flour if using very juicy apples to prevent sinking.
  • Add chopped nuts or raisins for extra texture and flavor if desired.
