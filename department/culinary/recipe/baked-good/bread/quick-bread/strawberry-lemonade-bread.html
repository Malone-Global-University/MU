Overview

Strawberry Lemonade Quick Bread is a sweet, tender loaf filled with fresh strawberries and topped with a bright lemon glaze. Itâ€™s perfect for a refreshing breakfast, brunch, or afternoon snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk or buttermilk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh strawberries, chopped
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Lemon Glaze: 1 cup powdered sugar, 2â€“3 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Mix sugar, melted butter, eggs, milk, and vanilla in a separate bowl.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed; do not overmix.
  5. Fold in chopped strawberries and lemon zest gently.
  6. Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake 50â€“60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Cool 10 minutes in pan, then transfer to wire rack.
  9. Mix powdered sugar and lemon juice to make glaze and drizzle over cooled bread.

Tips

  • Use fresh strawberries for best flavor; do not overmix to avoid crushing berries.
  • Lightly coat berries with flour to prevent sinking.
  • Adjust glaze consistency with more or less lemon juice.
  • Serve slightly warm for extra comfort and flavor.
