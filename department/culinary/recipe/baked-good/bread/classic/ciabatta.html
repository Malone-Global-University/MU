Foolproof Ciabatta Bread

Soft, airy, and crusty Italian ciabatta bread made easy at home.

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add bread flour and salt; mix until dough forms. Dough will be very sticky.
  3. Knead 8-10 minutes on a floured surface or use a stand mixer with dough hook. Dough should be soft and elastic.
  4. Transfer to a greased bowl, cover, and let rise 1-2 hours until doubled.
  5. Preheat oven to 425Â°F (220Â°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Gently shape dough into a rectangle without deflating. Place on prepared sheet.
  7. Let dough rest 20-30 minutes. Sprinkle flour on top for a rustic look.
  8. Bake 20-25 minutes until golden brown and hollow-sounding when tapped.
  9. Cool on wire rack before slicing. Enjoy fresh or toasted.

Tips for Success

  • Ciabatta dough is wetter than most breads; handle gently to retain air pockets.
  • Use plenty of flour on your hands and surface when shaping.
  • Drizzling olive oil on the baking sheet prevents sticking and adds flavor.
  • Cool completely before slicing for best texture.
