Tips for Success

  • Use a kitchen thermometer to ensure water is warm, not hot, to avoid killing the yeast.
  • Egg wash gives Challah its shiny, golden crust.
  • Bread can be stored in an airtight container for up to 3 days or frozen for longer.
  • Practice braiding with scrap dough to perfect the appearance.
