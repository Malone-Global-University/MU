Foolproof Sourdough Bread

Crusty, tangy, and soft sourdough loaf made easy at home.

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix starter and warm water until combined.
  2. Add salt and gradually mix in flour until dough forms.
  3. Knead 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise 4-6 hours or until doubled.
  5. Shape dough into a loaf, place on parchment-lined baking sheet or Dutch oven, and let rise 1-2 hours.
  6. Preheat oven to 450Â°F (230Â°C). Score the top of the loaf.
  7. Bake 30-35 minutes (cover Dutch oven for first 20 minutes if using) until crusty and golden brown.
  8. Cool on wire rack completely before slicing.

Tips for Success

  • Feed your starter the night before to ensure it's active.
  • Use a sharp blade to score the loaf for controlled expansion.
  • Baking in a covered Dutch oven produces a better crust.
  • Patience is key; long fermentation develops flavor and texture.
