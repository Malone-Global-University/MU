Foolproof Whole Wheat Bread

Soft, hearty, and healthy classic whole wheat loaf anyone can make at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 Â¼ tsp active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 2 tbsp honey or sugar
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter or olive oil, softened
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (plus extra for kneading)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and honey. Let sit 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add softened butter and salt. Gradually mix in whole wheat flour and all-purpose flour until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead on a lightly floured surface 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise 1 hour until doubled in size.
  5. Punch down dough, shape into a loaf, and place in a greased 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  6. Cover and let rise 30-40 minutes until doubled.
  7. Preheat oven to 375Â°F (190Â°C). Bake 30-35 minutes until golden brown and hollow-sounding when tapped.
  8. Cool 10 minutes in pan, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Tips for Success

  • Use warm water to properly activate yeast without killing it.
  • Knead well for strong gluten and a soft, airy loaf.
  • Optional: Brush with melted butter or honey after baking for a soft crust and extra flavor.
