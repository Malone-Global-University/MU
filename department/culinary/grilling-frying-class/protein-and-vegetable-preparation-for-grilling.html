Protein & Vegetable Preparation for Grilling

Master the preparation of meats, seafood, poultry, and vegetables for grilling, focusing on trimming, portioning, marination, and seasoning to achieve consistent results.

Module Overview

This module teaches students to prepare proteins and vegetables for grilling, including trimming, portioning, marinating, seasoning, and pre-cooking treatments. Emphasis is on consistency, flavor development, and safe handling practices.

Core Learning Objectives

  • Trim, portion, and marinate proteins and vegetables to professional standards.
  • Apply seasoning, rubs, and marinades to enhance flavor and texture.
  • Understand pre-grilling treatments such as blanching, par-cooking, or brining.
  • Ensure food safety through proper handling, storage, and temperature management.
  • Coordinate ingredient preparation to support efficient grilling workflows.

Practical Competencies

  • Portion and trim beef, poultry, seafood, and vegetables consistently for even grilling.
  • Prepare and apply marinades, rubs, and brines to proteins and vegetables with balanced flavor.
  • Demonstrate blanching, pre-cooking, and par-grilling techniques for vegetables.
  • Implement safe handling, storage, and hygiene practices during preparation.
  • Assess readiness for grilling based on texture, moisture content, and uniformity.
  • Sequence multi-item preparation to ensure synchronized cooking on the grill.
