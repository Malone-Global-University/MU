Module Overview

This module focuses on indoor grilling and broiling methods for controlled environments. Students practice broiler operation, stovetop grills, and advanced heat management for consistent results on proteins and vegetables.

Core Learning Objectives

  • Operate indoor grills and broilers safely and effectively.
  • Manage heat zones and temperature for even cooking.
  • Apply marinades, rubs, and seasoning for flavor enhancement.
  • Cook a variety of proteins and vegetables to target doneness.
  • Maintain sanitation and prevent cross-contamination in confined environments.

Practical Competencies

  • Setup and operate broilers and indoor grills safely.
  • Control heat zones and broiler temperatures for consistent results.
  • Prepare proteins and vegetables for indoor cooking with proper searing and moisture retention.
  • Execute timing strategies for simultaneous multi-item cooking.
  • Apply rubs, marinades, and seasoning to achieve professional flavor profiles.
  • Assess visual cues for doneness, browning, and moisture levels.
