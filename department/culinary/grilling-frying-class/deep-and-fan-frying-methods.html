Module Overview

Students learn controlled frying methods including deep frying and fan-assisted frying. Focus is placed on temperature regulation, oil management, battering, breading, and producing crisp, evenly cooked products safely and efficiently.

Core Learning Objectives

  • Select appropriate oils and fats for different frying techniques and food types.
  • Control oil temperature for consistent cooking results and safety.
  • Apply breading, battering, and coating techniques for optimal texture.
  • Operate deep fryers and fan-assisted fryers to industry standards.
  • Ensure sanitation, minimize oil degradation, and manage post-cook handling safely.

Practical Competencies

  • Monitor and maintain oil temperatures for deep and fan frying to avoid under- or overcooking.
  • Prepare batters, breadings, and coatings that adhere consistently and produce desired texture.
  • Execute frying techniques for meats, poultry, seafood, and vegetables with even cooking.
  • Manage oil filtration, turnover, and disposal in compliance with safety and environmental standards.
  • Evaluate finished product for texture, moisture retention, and visual presentation.
  • Implement multi-item frying sequences efficiently to coordinate timing and product quality.
