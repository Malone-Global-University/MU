Grilling & Frying Modules

  • Module 11: Charcoal & Gas Grilling Techniques

    A technical deep dive into fuel-based heat systems, including charcoal, hardwood, hybrid grills, and modern gas platforms. Learners develop operational competency in direct vs. indirect heat management, vent control, fire-building strategies, grate seasoning, and smoke modulation. The module emphasizes consistent temperature zones, sear optimization, and throughput-oriented grill station workflow.

  • Module 12: Indoor Grilling & Broiling

    Focuses on controlled-heat indoor solutions such as cast-iron grills, electric grill plates, salamanders, and commercial broilers. The instruction centers on surface heat mapping, high-heat caramelization, rapid turnover execution, and moisture-retention strategies. Students develop a production-grade understanding of broiler positioning, rack staging, and overhead radiant heat dynamics.

  • Module 13: BBQ Smoking & Low & Slow Cooking

    A comprehensive review of smoke chemistry, combustion management, and thermal equilibrium maintenance in offset smokers, pellet units, vertical smokers, and ceramic cookers. Learners explore wood profiles, bark development, humidity control, wrapping techniques, and long-cycle timing. Emphasis is placed on regional barbecue styles, precision doneness indicators, and scaled smoking operations.

  • Module 14: Deep & Fan Frying Methods

    Covers immersion frying, shallow frying, and modern fan-assisted convection frying methodologies. Students analyze oil behavior, smoke points, batter systems, breading structures, and crisp-retention mechanics. Operational training includes fryer safety, filtration cycles, oil-life extension, batch scheduling, and output consistency under commercial volume conditions.

  • Module 15: Protein & Vegetable Preparation for Grilling

    Addresses pre-grill prep workflows including trimming, marinating, brining, skewering, par-cooking, and flavor-base development. Learners master crosshatch marking, surface drying, fat-rendering control, vegetable caramelization, and heat-zone allocation. Module delivers full production readiness for mixed-menu grill service.
