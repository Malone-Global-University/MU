Module Overview

This module focuses on the strategic creation of menus and recipes for professional culinary environments. Students learn to integrate flavor theory, seasonal ingredients, dietary considerations, and operational efficiency into cohesive culinary offerings.

Core Learning Objectives

  • Develop balanced and appealing menus for diverse service types.
  • Create original recipes integrating flavor, texture, and visual presentation.
  • Analyze ingredient sourcing, seasonality, and dietary restrictions.
  • Apply costing and pricing strategies for menu profitability.
  • Adapt menus for special events, catering, and thematic culinary concepts.
