Module Overview

This module introduces students to the art and science of plating and presentation. Emphasis is placed on visual balance, color theory, textural contrast, and proper use of garnishes and edible elements to create memorable culinary experiences.

Core Learning Objectives

  • Understand principles of visual composition and color harmony.
  • Apply symmetry, asymmetry, and focal points to plating.
  • Incorporate texture and layering for multisensory appeal.
  • Select appropriate garnishes and plating materials to enhance presentation.
  • Adapt presentation techniques for various service styles and cuisines.

Practical Competencies

  • Create visually balanced plates using professional plating techniques.
  • Execute precise portioning and placement of ingredients for aesthetic impact.
  • Design plating layouts that complement flavor, texture, and temperature.
  • Apply finishing techniques such as drizzles, foams, and micro-garnishes.
  • Critically evaluate presentations and refine based on sensory feedback and aesthetics.
