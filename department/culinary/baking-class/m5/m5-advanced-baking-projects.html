Overview

In this final module, students consolidate all previous skills to tackle complex baking projects. Emphasis is placed on precision, creativity, and professional-level presentation to prepare students for real-world culinary challenges.

Learning Objectives

  • Apply advanced techniques across breads, pastries, cakes, and chocolate.
  • Develop original recipes and creative plating concepts.
  • Manage multi-step baking projects efficiently and accurately.
  • Refine skills in decorating, flavor balance, and texture combination.
  • Prepare desserts suitable for professional or competition-level standards.

Topics Covered

  1. Complex Bread Projects: Multi-grain, Laminated, and Sourdough Creations
  2. Advanced Pastry Techniques: Entremets, Mille-feuille, and Specialty Tarts
  3. Layered and Sculpted Cakes: Wedding and Showpiece Designs
  4. Chocolate & Confection Challenges: Center-filled Chocolates, Sculptures, and Advanced Decorations
  5. Recipe Development and Flavor Pairing
  6. Professional Presentation and Plating Techniques
  7. Time Management and Project Planning in the Kitchen

Assignments & Practice

  • Complete a multi-component dessert incorporating at least two baking disciplines.
  • Create a showpiece cake or plated dessert demonstrating advanced techniques and creativity.
  • Submit a detailed recipe and process documentation for evaluation.
