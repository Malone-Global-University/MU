Texture and Handling Differences

Dough Characteristics

  • Firm, thick, and moldable
  • Can be kneaded, shaped, rolled, or cut
  • Develops strong gluten structure
  • Used for breads, rolls, pretzels, dumplings, and some cookies

Batter Characteristics

  • Loose, pourable, or spoonable
  • Cannot be kneaded
  • Minimal gluten development
  • Used for cakes, muffins, waffles, cornbread, and fritters
