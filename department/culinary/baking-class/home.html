Baking & Pastry Modules

The Baking & Pastry course provides a structured, competency driven learning sequence designed to build professional readiness across foundational, intermediate, and advanced baking disciplines. Each module establishes measurable outcomes aligned with industry standards and culinary-school operational frameworks.

  • Module 1: Introduction to Baking

    Establishes the foundational chemistry, measurement standards, ingredient behaviors, and heat-transfer principles governing all baking disciplines. Learners develop a baseline for accuracy, mise en place, and production sequencing while executing introductory quick-bread and simple dessert formulas.

  • Module 2: Artisan Breads & Yeast Techniques

    Provides deep-dive exposure to fermentation science, preferments, dough development, gluten structure, and advanced shaping methods. Students execute lean, enriched, and laminated doughs with a focus on consistency, scaling accuracy, and bakery-grade production flow.

  • Module 5: Advanced Baking Projects

    Culminating capstone focused on independent project execution, menu development, advanced plating, multi-component dessert architecture, and operational efficiency. Learners demonstrate mastery through a comprehensive final portfolio suitable for industry placement.
