Cake Pans, Tart Pans & Muffin Tins

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Module 1 Cake Pans, Tart Pans & Muffin Tins

Introduction

Proper bakeware ensures your cakes, tarts, and muffins come out evenly baked with ideal texture and presentation.

Types of Bakeware

  • Cake pans: round, square, bundt, and specialty shapes
  • Tart pans: fluted, removable bottom, and mini tart pans
  • Muffin tins: standard, jumbo, and mini sizes

Materials & Care

  • Metal, nonstick, silicone, and glass options
  • Proper greasing, lining, and maintenance
  • Preventing warping and sticking
