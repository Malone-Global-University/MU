Spatulas, Scrapers & Bench Tools

Essential tools for mixing, folding, scraping, and shaping dough or batter to achieve precise baking results.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Module 1 Spatulas, Scrapers & Bench Tools

Introduction

Spatulas, scrapers, and other bench tools are essential for professional and home bakers alike. They ensure precision in handling dough, batter, and other ingredients.

Common Tools

  • Offset spatulas for spreading and frosting
  • Dough scrapers for dividing and cleaning work surfaces
  • Bench knives for portioning and shaping dough
  • Silicone spatulas for folding and scraping bowls

Tips for Use

  • Choose the right tool for the task to prevent damage to dough or pans
  • Clean thoroughly to maintain hygiene and longevity
  • Store tools properly to avoid warping or breaking
