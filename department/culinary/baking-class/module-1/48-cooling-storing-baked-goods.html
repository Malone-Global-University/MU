Cooling & Storing Baked Goods

Master techniques for cooling, storing, and preserving baked goods to maintain texture, flavor, and freshness.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Module 1 Cooling & Storing Baked Goods

Introduction

This lesson covers how to properly cool baked goods after baking and the best methods to store them. Proper handling preserves texture, flavor, and safety while extending shelf life.

Objectives

  • Understand the importance of proper cooling techniques for baked goods.
  • Learn the best practices for storing breads, cakes, and pastries.
  • Prevent spoilage, staleness, and moisture loss.
  • Identify suitable storage containers, temperatures, and shelf life considerations.

Step-by-Step Instructions: Cooling & Storing

Step 1: Initial Cooling

Remove baked goods from the oven and place on wire racks to allow air circulation. Avoid covering while still hot to prevent condensation.
