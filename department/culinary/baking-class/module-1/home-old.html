Overview

This module introduces the essential concepts of baking, including understanding ingredients, basic techniques, and proper use of baking tools. By the end of this module, you will have a solid foundation to tackle more advanced baking challenges.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify key ingredients used in baking and their functions.
  • Understand basic baking techniques and terminology.
  • Learn to measure ingredients accurately for consistent results.
  • Familiarize with essential baking tools and equipment.
  • Follow simple baking recipes to produce quality baked goods.

Topics Covered

  1. Introduction to Common Baking Ingredients (flour, sugar, fats, eggs, leavening agents)
  2. Essential Baking Tools and Equipment
  3. Mixing Methods and Techniques (creaming, folding, whisking)
  4. Measuring and Scaling Ingredients Accurately
  5. Understanding Oven Temperatures and Baking Times
  6. Simple Recipes: Cookies, Muffins, and Basic Breads

Assignments & Practice

  • Practice measuring ingredients accurately using a kitchen scale.
  • Bake a batch of basic cookies following a provided recipe.
  • Document your process and note any adjustments or observations.
