Home Department Culinary Baking Class Module 1 Baking Tools Overview

Introduction

This lesson introduces the essential baking tools and equipment used in professional and home baking, with guidelines on usage and maintenance.

Essential Tools

  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Mixing bowls and spatulas
  • Whisks and hand mixers
  • Baking pans and trays
  • Rolling pins and pastry brushes
  • Ovens and thermometers

Best Practices

  • Keep tools clean and well-maintained.
  • Use correct tool for each task.
  • Store equipment properly to prevent damage.
  • Regularly check calibration of measuring tools.
