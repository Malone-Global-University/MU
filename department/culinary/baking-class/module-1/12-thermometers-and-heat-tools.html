Thermometers & Heat Tools

Understand different thermometers and heat tools, their uses, and how to maintain accurate temperatures for perfect baking results.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Module 1 Thermometers & Heat Tools

Introduction

Precise temperature control is key in baking. Thermometers and heat tools ensure your recipes come out perfectly every time.

Types of Thermometers

  • Instant-read digital thermometers
  • Candy and deep-fry thermometers
  • Oven-safe probe thermometers

Heat Tools

  • Oven mitts and silicone gloves
  • Baking sheets and pans for even heat distribution
  • Heat-resistant spatulas and utensils
