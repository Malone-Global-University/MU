Understanding Surface Tension in Dough

Surface tension is key to forming strong, elastic dough that holds its shape during baking. Students will learn:

  • How to handle dough to increase elasticity
  • Techniques to maintain a smooth, taut surface
  • Effects of proper tension on oven spring and crust formation
  • Identifying under- or over-tensioned dough

Learning Outcomes

After this lesson, students will be able to create dough with optimal surface tension, resulting in uniform loaves with excellent texture, shape, and appearance.
