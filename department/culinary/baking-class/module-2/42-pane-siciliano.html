Pane Siciliano

Learn to bake Pane Siciliano, a rustic Sicilian bread with a soft crumb and golden crust, perfect for traditional Mediterranean meals.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Pane Siciliano

Introduction to Pane Siciliano

Pane Siciliano is a traditional bread from Sicily, Italy. Known for its soft, airy crumb and thin crust, it is a staple in Mediterranean cuisine and often used for sandwiches or served with olive oil and cheeses.

Ingredients and Preparation

Learn about the typical ingredients, including high-protein wheat flour, olive oil, and natural yeast, as well as techniques for mixing, kneading, and shaping the dough.

Baking Techniques

Discover the baking methods that give Pane Siciliano its signature texture, including oven temperature management, proofing times, and tips for achieving the perfect golden crust.
