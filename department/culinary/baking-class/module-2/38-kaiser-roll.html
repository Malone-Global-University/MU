Kaiser Roll

Master the art of baking classic Kaiser Rolls at Malone Global University. Learn to create soft, fluffy rolls with the signature star pattern and golden crust for sandwiches or breakfast.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Kaiser Roll

Overview

Kaiser Rolls are iconic sandwich rolls known for their light, airy crumb and signature five-point star pattern. This lesson covers traditional shaping, proofing, and baking techniques.

Learning Outcomes

  • Prepare classic Kaiser Roll dough with proper hydration and yeast activity.
  • Shape rolls with the traditional star pattern.
  • Proof and bake rolls to achieve soft interiors and golden crusts.
  • Understand variations for sweet or savory applications.
