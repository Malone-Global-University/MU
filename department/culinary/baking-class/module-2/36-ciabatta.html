Ciabatta

Learn to create authentic Italian Ciabatta bread with its signature open crumb, chewy texture, and rustic crust, using high-hydration dough techniques.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Ciabatta

Overview

Ciabatta is a classic Italian bread known for its airy, open crumb and thin, crispy crust. This course focuses on high-hydration dough, proper folding techniques, and baking methods to achieve the perfect loaf.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the characteristics of Italian Ciabatta bread.
  • Master high-hydration dough handling and folding techniques.
  • Shape loaves to retain air pockets and achieve the signature crumb.
  • Bake for a golden crust with chewy interior texture.
