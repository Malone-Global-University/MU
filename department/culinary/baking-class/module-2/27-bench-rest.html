Bench Rest

Discover the bench rest method in bread making, where dough is allowed to relax after pre-shaping, improving elasticity, handling, and final shaping results.

Bench Rest Technique

The bench rest is a critical stage in bread preparation. After pre-shaping, the dough rests on the work surface, allowing gluten to relax and surface tension to even out. This results in:

  • Improved dough elasticity
  • Smoother final shaping
  • Consistent loaf texture
  • Better oven spring during baking

Learning Objectives

Students will learn to properly execute a bench rest, observe the changes in dough texture, and use this technique to prepare professional-quality bread shapes such as boule, batard, and baguette.
