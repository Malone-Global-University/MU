Vienna Bread

Discover Vienna Bread, a soft and slightly sweet European classic. Learn proper kneading, proofing, and baking techniques to create the perfect loaf every time.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Vienna Bread

Introduction to Vienna Bread

Vienna Bread is a classic European loaf, known for its soft texture, delicate crumb, and slightly sweet flavor. Perfect for sandwiches or elegant table settings.

Ingredients and Preparation

Use high-quality flour, butter, sugar, yeast, and milk. Proper mixing, kneading, and fermentation ensure the soft and airy crumb typical of Vienna Bread.

Baking Techniques

Master shaping, scoring, and baking at controlled temperatures to achieve the golden crust and soft, tender interior that defines authentic Vienna Bread.
