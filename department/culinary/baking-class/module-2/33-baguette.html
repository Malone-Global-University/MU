Baguette Shaping

Master the traditional baguette shaping technique to create long, thin artisan loaves with crisp crusts and consistent crumb structure.

Technique Overview

The baguette shaping method creates long, slender loaves ideal for crisp crusts and open crumb. Proper surface tension and gentle rolling are key.
