Final Shaping

Master the final shaping stage in bread making, where rested dough is formed into its final shape, including boule, batard, baguette, and braiding for baking perfection.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Final Shaping

Techniques for Final Shaping

The final shaping step ensures dough has the correct tension, form, and consistency for baking. Students will learn:

  • Forming boule and batard
  • Advanced baguette shaping
  • Braiding techniques
  • Maintaining surface tension and elasticity
  • Proper handling for consistent oven spring

Learning Objectives

Students will understand how to transform rested dough into professional-quality shapes, ensuring even baking, optimal texture, and visually appealing results.
