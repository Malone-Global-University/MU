Overview of Yeast

Yeast is a living organism used in baking to ferment sugars, producing carbon dioxide and alcohol that leavens dough. Choosing the right type ensures consistent results.

Active Dry Yeast

Comes as dehydrated granules. Needs to be dissolved in warm water before use. It provides a slower, steady fermentation suitable for many bread recipes.
