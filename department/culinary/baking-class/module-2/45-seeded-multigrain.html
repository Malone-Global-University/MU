Multigrain / Seeded Artisan Loaf

Master the art of baking a nutritious multigrain, seeded artisan loaf with a crisp crust, rich flavor, and wholesome texture perfect for modern culinary recipes.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Multigrain / Seeded Artisan Loaf

Introduction to Multigrain / Seeded Artisan Loaf

This loaf combines a variety of grains and seeds such as oats, sunflower seeds, flax, and sesame for a hearty, flavorful bread that is both nutritious and artisan in style.

Ingredients and Preparation

Discover the perfect blend of whole grains, seeds, high-protein flour, and natural yeast, along with proper kneading, fermentation, and shaping techniques for optimal texture.

Baking Techniques

Learn oven temperature management, proofing methods, and seed topping techniques that give this artisan loaf its signature crust and aromatic flavor.
