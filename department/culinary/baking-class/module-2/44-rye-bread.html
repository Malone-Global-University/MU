Master the art of baking hearty, flavorful rye bread with traditional techniques and fermentation processes.

Introduction to Rye Bread

Rye bread is known for its deep flavor, dense texture, and longer shelf life. Learn the essentials of using rye flour and incorporating traditional methods.

Mixing and Fermentation

Understand how rye flour affects gluten development and fermentation, including sourdough rye starter techniques.

Baking and Scoring

Learn proper shaping, scoring, and baking methods to achieve classic rye bread loaves with an artisanal crust.
