Finger-Poke Test

Learn the simple yet essential method to check if your dough is ready for baking.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Finger-Poke Test

What is the Finger-Poke Test?

The finger-poke test is a simple way to determine if dough has completed its final proof. It measures elasticity and readiness for baking.

How to Perform the Test

  1. Lightly flour your finger to prevent sticking.
  2. Gently press your finger about 1/2 inch into the dough.
  3. Observe how the dough responds:
    • Slow spring back: Dough is ready to bake.
    • Quick spring back: Dough needs more proofing.
    • No spring back: Dough may be over-proofed.

Tips for Accuracy

  • Perform the test gently to avoid deflating the dough.
  • Check multiple spots if the dough is large.
  • Combine with visual cues: dough should roughly double in size and feel airy.
