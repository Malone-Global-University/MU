Introduction to Preferments

Preferments are pre-fermented doughs that enhance the flavor, texture, and shelf-life of breads. Common types include Poolish, Biga, and Levain.

Feeding & Timing Tips

  • Always mix preferments thoroughly before use.
  • Adjust fermentation time based on room temperature.
  • Use preferments immediately when at peak activity for best flavor.
