Brioche

Master the art of baking Brioche, the rich, buttery French yeast bread, with Malone Global University. Learn to create tender, golden loaves with perfect crumb.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Brioche

Overview

Brioche is a classic French bread enriched with butter, eggs, and sugar, resulting in a soft, airy crumb and golden crust. This lesson covers traditional techniques and modern adaptations.

Learning Outcomes

  • Create authentic Brioche using rich yeast dough.
  • Master dough handling and proofing for optimal texture.
  • Develop shaping techniques for classic loaves and buns.
  • Understand baking methods for perfect color and softness.
