Hand Kneading

Master the tactile skill of hand kneading to develop gluten, shape dough, and achieve perfectly textured bread.

Introduction to Hand Kneading

Hand kneading is a fundamental bread-making technique where the baker uses their hands to develop the gluten network. This process ensures proper elasticity, structure, and texture in the dough.

Benefits of Hand Kneading

  • Direct tactile feedback on dough consistency
  • Improves gluten development naturally
  • Allows for controlled shaping and texture
  • Essential skill for artisanal baking

Steps to Hand Knead Dough

  1. Lightly flour a clean work surface.
  2. Place dough on the surface and fold it over itself.
  3. Push dough away with the heel of your hand and fold it back.
