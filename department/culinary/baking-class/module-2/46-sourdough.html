Master the art of making classic sourdough bread, from starter creation to fermentation and baking techniques.

Introduction to Sourdough

Sourdough is a traditional method of bread-making using naturally occurring wild yeasts and lactic acid bacteria. It produces bread with a distinctive flavor, texture, and crust.

Maintaining a Sourdough Starter

Learn to feed and care for your starter, which is essential for consistent fermentation and a lively rise.

Baking Techniques

Discover methods for mixing, proofing, scoring, and baking sourdough to achieve a professional-quality loaf.
