Pain de Campagne

Learn to bake authentic French country bread at Malone Global University, creating rustic, flavorful loaves using traditional artisan techniques.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Pain de Campagne

Overview

Pain de Campagne, also known as French country bread, is a rustic loaf with a thick crust and chewy interior. This course teaches proper mixing, fermentation, shaping, and scoring techniques.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the characteristics of French country bread.
  • Master the use of sourdough and levain in dough fermentation.
  • Shape and score loaves for optimal rise and appearance.
  • Bake consistently for a crisp crust and tender crumb.
