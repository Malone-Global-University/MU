Focaccia

Discover the art of baking Italian Focaccia with Malone Global University. Learn to create soft, airy bread with a golden crust, enriched with olive oil and herbs.

Home Department Culinary Baking Class Focaccia

Overview

Focaccia is a traditional Italian flatbread known for its olive oil richness, soft interior, and slightly crisp crust. This course teaches proper dough preparation, hydration, and flavoring techniques.

Learning Outcomes

  • Create authentic Italian Focaccia with a soft, airy interior.
  • Master olive oil-enriched dough and proper handling techniques.
  • Learn flavoring options such as herbs, olives, and toppings.
  • Develop baking techniques for an even golden crust.
