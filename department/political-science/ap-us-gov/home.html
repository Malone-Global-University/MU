Course Overview

Explore the foundations and functions of the U.S. government. Prepares for the AP exam with emphasis on constitutional principles, political behavior, and civil rights.

  • Resources: Constitution, Supreme Court cases, AP exam prep

  • Bill of Rights & Key Amendments
  • Landmark Supreme Court Cases

  • Freedom of Speech, Religion, and Privacy
  • Comparing US Government to Other Democracies
  • Globalization and US Policy
  • Current Events Analysis and AP Exam Prep
