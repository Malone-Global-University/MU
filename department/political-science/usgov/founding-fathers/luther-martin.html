Early Life & Background

Luther Martin was born in 1748 in Lower Marlboro, Maryland. He became a prominent lawyer and politician, known for his strong legal expertise and outspoken defense of state sovereignty. Martin was educated in law and quickly became influential in Maryland politics.

Political Beliefs & Philosophy

Martin was an Anti-Federalist, opposing a strong centralized government. He argued that the Constitution, as initially drafted, gave excessive power to the federal government and threatened individual liberties and state authority.
