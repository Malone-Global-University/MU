Early Life & Background

Nathaniel Gorham was born in 1738 in Charlestown, Massachusetts. A successful merchant and public official, he became involved in local and colonial politics, eventually gaining prominence as a statesman during the formation of the United States.

Political Beliefs & Philosophy

Gorham valued balanced government, strong legislative frameworks, and the protection of property rights. He supported measures that would strengthen national cohesion while protecting state interests.
