About Political Science at MGU

The Political Science Department at Malone Global University is the core hub for studying government, law, philosophy, diplomacy, and debate. We combine foundational texts with practical exercises: mock trials, debates, policy brief writing, and primary-document study.

Core Pathways

U.S. Government

Founding documents, federal structure, civics, and modern governance.

Political Literacy

Understand power, institutions, elections, and public policy.

Law

Study legal systems, constitutions, rights, and courtroom practice (mock trial ready).

Philosophy

Ethics, political theory, justice, and the ideas that shape law and policy.

Practical public speaking, argument structure, formats, and adjudication.
