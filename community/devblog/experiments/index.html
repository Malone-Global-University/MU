Experiments

Prototype ideas, exploratory tools, tests, and unfinished development trials.

Purpose

The Experiments page is for ideas that are being tested before they become official site standards. It gives MGU a place to explore new tools, layouts, scripts, workflows, and content structures without treating every attempt as final.

Use this section when the work is exploratory, provisional, or still being evaluated.

What to Record

Experiment notes should explain the hypothesis, the test setup, the expected benefit, the result, and whether the idea should be adopted, revised, paused, or rejected.

Useful entries may include prototype navigation systems, alternate index layouts, test scripts, new content formats, experimental course structures, image-generation trials, and possible automation workflows.

Suggested Entry Types

Prototype reports, test results, trial layouts, abandoned approaches, comparison notes, exploratory scripts, and future-feature ideas all belong here.

Even failed experiments are useful if they explain what was learned and what should not be repeated.

Connection to the Devblog

Experiments keeps unfinished work organized. It protects the main website from becoming cluttered while still preserving ideas that may become valuable later.
