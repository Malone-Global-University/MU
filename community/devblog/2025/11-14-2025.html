Details

I am using this new series to explore more than the usual biographies of the Founding Fathers. Of particular interest is how their beliefs changed forming factions that later became the first political parties from the resulting rivalries that developed.

Purpose

As a principled first look at the beginnings of government we see how revolution turns into stable peace, from rebellion to federalism, and defying government to having to establish respect for it. The purpose is to teach that destruction is not the goal (anti- Mad Max) but only a means to build a better union (government/cvilization 2.0). The good wars leave key infrastructure in place so that it can be quickly utilized by the conquering force. The best wars win without firing a shot.
