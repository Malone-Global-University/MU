Department restructing: The economics Department (EBF) had been renamed Corporate Science

February 24, 2026 | By Malone Global University | Department: Conspiracy

Quick Overview

The main department for all things money is now called Corporate Science. Under it are three sub-departments for economics, business, and finance.

What’s New

New structure and new Economics 101 course with 32 lessons.

Details

The reorganization merges the former Economics Department with complementary areas of study, creating a more integrated approach to corporate and financial education. Students can now navigate a clearer path through Economics, Business, and Finance courses, all under the Corporate Science umbrella.

Purpose

This restructuring is intended to streamline our curriculum, improve interdisciplinary collaboration, and better reflect the practical applications of economic and business knowledge in the corporate world.

Lessons Learned

Lessons Learned

From this process, we learned the importance of flexibility in department naming and structure, as well as the value of aligning academic offerings with real-world industry trends. Clear communication and planning are key when implementing major organizational changes.

Next Steps

  • Finalize the new Corporate Science curriculum and course schedules.
  • Update the website and learning management system to reflect the new department structure.
  • Communicate changes to students, faculty, and staff to ensure a smooth transition.
  • Monitor and assess the success of the new structure for continuous improvement.
