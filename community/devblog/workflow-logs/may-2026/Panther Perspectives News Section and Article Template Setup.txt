WORKFLOW NOTE

Title
Panther Perspectives News Section and Article Template Setup

Date
2026-05-19

Area
The Malone Panther / News / Panther Perspectives / Article Templates

Problem
The news area needed a clearer department structure for opinion-style content. The original section name “Opinion” was functional but generic, and the site needed a stronger branded identity for editorials, columns, letters, guest essays, and media criticism. The section also needed starter subpages and a reusable blank article template for future reporting and commentary.

Intended Goal
Create a clean, branded structure for the opinion/commentary side of The Malone Panther using the name “Panther Perspectives,” with organized subfolders, starter pages, correct page titles, and a reusable article template that follows the MGU news page pattern.

Root Cause
The newspaper structure was still evolving from a general department list into a more formal online newspaper system. Opinion-related content needed to be separated into clear editorial categories so that official positions, recurring commentary, reader letters, outside essays, and media criticism would not be mixed together.

Steps Taken

1. Reviewed the existing news department setup and recommended separating public-facing section names from clean folder names.
2. Renamed the concept from `/news/opinion/` to `/news/perspectives/` while keeping the public label “Panther Perspectives.”
3. Defined the five Panther Perspectives subsections: editorials, columns, letters, guest essays, and media criticism.
4. Created starter `index.html` pages for each subsection with the correct title format: `[Section] | Panther Perspectives | The Malone Panther | Malone Global University`.
5. Packaged the five starter pages into a downloadable ZIP bundle for placement under `C:\MGU\news\perspectives`.
6. Created a reusable blank article template for The Malone Panther with fields for headline, section, dek, byline, publication date, editor’s note, summary, article body, sources, and related articles.

Key Change
The generic Opinion section was converted into the branded Panther Perspectives structure with five clear editorial subcategories and reusable article scaffolding.

Outcome
The news site now has a stronger structure for commentary and opinion content. Panther Perspectives can serve as the branded home for official editorials, recurring columns, reader letters, guest essays, and media criticism. Future articles can now be created faster using a consistent HTML template that matches the MGU news layout.

Validation
The folder structure was checked against the intended path:

`C:\MGU\news\perspectives`

The generated pages match the requested title structure and use the shared MGU news components, including `/component/news.css`, header injection, breadcrumb placeholder, bottom navigation placeholder, and footer date handling.

Rules Going Forward
Use `/news/perspectives/` as the folder path and “Panther Perspectives” as the public-facing section name.
Use `editorials/` only for official newspaper positions.
Use `columns/` for recurring named voices.
Use `letters/` for reader and community responses.
Use `guest-essays/` for developed outside contributor pieces.
Use `media-criticism/` for analysis of framing, platforms, headlines, misinformation, propaganda, and coverage quality.
Use the blank article template as the starting point for new Malone Panther articles.

Notes
Older links using `/news/opinion/` should eventually be updated to `/news/perspectives/`. Individual articles can either live directly under `/news/perspectives/article-name/` or, preferably, inside the correct subsection such as `/news/perspectives/editorials/article-name/` or `/news/perspectives/media-criticism/article-name/`.
