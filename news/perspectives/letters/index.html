Letters Overview

Letters are short opinion submissions from readers, students, community members, and participants in public discussion. A letter may respond to a published article, correct a claim, raise a concern, or add a community perspective to an issue under discussion.

  • Student and community feedback
  • Corrections or clarifications from readers
  • Short public replies
  • Community concerns and open responses

Section Standard

Letters should be concise, civil, and focused. They may disagree strongly, but they should respond to ideas and claims rather than rely on insults or unsupported attacks.
