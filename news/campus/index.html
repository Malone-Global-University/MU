Campus Section Overview

The Campus Section of The Malone Panther covers the everyday institutional life of Malone Global University and the communities connected to it. Campus coverage should help students, faculty, staff, alumni, families, and visitors understand what is happening, why it matters, and how to participate responsibly in university life.

This section includes coverage of academics, student organizations, campus events, athletics, public lectures, campus services, facilities, policies, student concerns, leadership decisions, awards, traditions, and community announcements. Articles may report campus news, preview events, explain policy changes, profile student leaders, summarize meetings, or document the culture of learning and service.

Campus Coverage Standards

Campus articles should be useful, fair, timely, and specific. Coverage should distinguish official announcements, reported facts, student concerns, opinion, and interpretation. Readers should be able to tell what changed, who made the decision, when it applies, where to get help, and how the issue affects daily campus life.

The Campus Section exists to support informed participation in university community. It should document achievements without becoming promotional, examine problems without becoming careless, and treat students, faculty, staff, alumni, and visitors as members of a shared learning institution.
