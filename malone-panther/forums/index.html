What Are Forums?

Forums are conversatin thread groups. The purpose is a retrun to meaningful conversation that raises quality of life, monetize it through addictive algorithms. The two main forums are The Commons, which hosts casual everyday topics, and the Civic Center which hosts guided conversations about pressing developments across all sectors of life.

These forums are a fact finding and touching of basis place. This is where you state your position and learn about the positions of others to gauge alignment and discuss next steps of collaboration. They are not meant for oral arguments nor persuasion. Oral arguments are hosted by the MGU Debate Club, who also are active in the Political Science Department by participating in moderated debates there.

Guiding Principle

Forums operate on noncombativeness, honesty, and a sincere desire to collobrate with others for the greater good of the MGU Community, and by extension, the Black Community.
