Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering studies the generation, control, transmission, and application of electricity across power systems, electronics, communications, and embedded technologies. Students learn how electrical systems convert energy and information into functional devices and infrastructure.

  • Circuit Analysis

    Study voltage, current, resistance, impedance, and circuit behavior in electrical networks.

  • Electronics

    Examine semiconductors, transistors, amplifiers, and modern electronic device design.

  • Power Systems

    Learn how electricity is generated, transmitted, distributed, and managed across power grids.

  • Control Systems

    Study automated feedback systems used to regulate machines, industrial processes, and devices.

  • Embedded Systems

    Explore specialized computing systems integrated directly into hardware devices and machinery.
