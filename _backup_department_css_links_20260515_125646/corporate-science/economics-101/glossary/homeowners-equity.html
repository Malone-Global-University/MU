Homeowners Equity

Economics Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Corporate Science Economics Glossary Homeowners Equity

Definition

Homeowners equity is the portion of a home’s value that the owner actually owns, calculated as value minus debt.

Why It Matters

Equity represents wealth and borrowing power for homeowners.

Example

Owning $80,000 equity in a $300,000 home.
