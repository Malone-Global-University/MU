Online Only Bank

Economics Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Corporate Science Economics Glossary Online Only Bank

Definition

A bank that operates without physical branches and provides services entirely through digital platforms.

Why It Matters

Usually offers lower fees and higher interest rates due to reduced overhead.

Example

Customers open accounts and receive statements entirely online.
