Chain of Title

Real Estate Investing Glossary – Malone Global University

Home Department Professional Studies Real Estate Track Glossary Chain of Title

Definition

The Chain of Title is the chronological history of ownership transfers and recorded documents affecting a property, from the original owner to the current owner.

In simple terms: it’s the property’s ownership history.

What the Chain of Title Includes

  • Recorded deeds
  • Mortgages and releases
  • Liens and satisfactions
  • Easements and restrictions
  • Probate transfers
  • Foreclosure documents
  • Tax sale records

Why Chain of Title Matters

  • Confirms legal ownership.
  • Reveals title defects.
  • Ensures clean transfer at closing.
  • Protects lenders and buyers.

Breaks in the Chain of Title

A break in the chain of title occurs when ownership transfers are incomplete, improperly recorded, or missing.

  • Unrecorded deed.
  • Forgery or fraud.
  • Missing heir in estate transfer.
  • Incorrect legal description.

Breaks can create a cloud on title and require legal correction, sometimes through a quiet title action.

How Chain of Title Is Verified

  • Title search conducted by title company.
  • Review of county recorder records.
  • Examination of prior conveyances.
  • Preparation of title report or commitment.

Chain of Title vs. Title Insurance

  • Chain of Title: Historical ownership record.
  • Title Insurance: Financial protection against undiscovered defects.

Example

A property is transferred through multiple owners over 40 years. During due diligence, the title company discovers a prior mortgage was never formally released. This creates a defect in the chain of title that must be resolved before closing.

Impact on Investors

  • Delays in closing transactions.
  • Additional legal costs.
  • Potential refinancing issues.
  • Reduced property marketability.

Investor Checklist

  • Order a title report early in due diligence.
  • Review ownership transfers carefully.
  • Confirm lien releases are recorded.
  • Verify legal description accuracy.
  • Obtain title insurance at closing.

Chain of Title vs. Abstract of Title

  • Chain of Title: Ownership sequence itself.
  • Abstract of Title: Summary document detailing recorded history.
