Honor Code

Malone Global University expects all students, faculty, and staff to uphold the highest standards of academic integrity and ethical conduct.

1. Preamble

The Honor Code reflects our commitment to integrity, respect, and responsibility. All members of the Malone Global University community are expected to uphold these principles in their academic and personal conduct.

2. Academic Integrity

  • Honesty: All academic work must be the result of your own efforts unless properly cited.
  • Plagiarism: Copying or using others’ work without acknowledgment is prohibited.
  • Examinations: Cheating or unauthorized collaboration on assessments is strictly forbidden.
  • Research: Fabrication, falsification, or misrepresentation of research is prohibited.

3. Ethical Conduct

  • Respect the rights and property of others.
  • Maintain professional behavior in all university activities.
  • Adhere to all university policies and regulations.

4. Responsibilities of Students and Faculty

All members of the university community must:

  • Report violations of the Honor Code.
  • Participate honestly in academic and community activities.
  • Support an environment of trust, fairness, and accountability.

5. Consequences of Violations

Violations of the Honor Code may result in disciplinary action, including but not limited to:

  • Academic penalties (e.g., failing grade, course dismissal)
  • Disciplinary probation or suspension
  • Expulsion in severe cases

All cases are reviewed in accordance with university policies and procedures.

6. Commitment

By enrolling at Malone Global University, students agree to adhere to this Honor Code and contribute to a culture of integrity, respect, and academic excellence.
