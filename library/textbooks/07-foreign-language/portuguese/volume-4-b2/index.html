Portuguese Volume 4 (B2)

Volume 4 builds upper-intermediate proficiency through formal writing, analytical reading, professional interaction, public discussion, research, and structured presentations. By the end of the volume, students are prepared to use Portuguese with greater confidence and control at the B2 level.

Overview

Volume 4 corresponds to the CEFR B2 proficiency level and prepares learners to communicate with greater precision and flexibility across academic, institutional, professional, technical, and public-facing settings. The supplied inventory identifies the chapter sequence for this volume.

Lessons address professional correspondence, meetings, reports, media analysis, public policy, research, literature, translation, negotiation, and advanced presentations while continually integrating vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, reading, listening, speaking, writing, and cumulative review.
