Arabic Volume 2 (A2)

Volume 2 expands the learner's command of Modern Standard Arabic through longer conversations, practical reading, writing, and listening activities. Students build confidence communicating about everyday life, work, education, travel, news, and community while strengthening grammar and vocabulary introduced in Volume 1.

Overview

Volume 2 corresponds to the CEFR A2 proficiency level and develops elementary Arabic communication into connected speech and writing. Students learn to describe experiences, express preferences, explain plans, discuss daily responsibilities, and communicate confidently in familiar social and professional situations.

The twenty chapters emphasize practical communication while introducing increasingly sophisticated grammar, expanded vocabulary, longer reading passages, and project-based review activities that prepare learners for the intermediate (B1) level.
