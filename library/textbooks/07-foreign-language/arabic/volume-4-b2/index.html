Arabic Volume 4 (B2)

Volume 4 develops upper-intermediate proficiency through advanced reading, academic writing, formal communication, public discussion, media analysis, and research. Students learn to communicate with greater precision while engaging with complex social, professional, and academic topics in Modern Standard Arabic.

Overview

Volume 4 corresponds to the CEFR B2 proficiency level and focuses on sophisticated communication across academic, civic, professional, and public contexts. Learners strengthen their ability to analyze information, evaluate arguments, interpret sources, conduct research, and communicate effectively with diverse audiences.

The twenty chapters emphasize formal writing, media literacy, public speaking, research methods, documentation, critical reading, and advanced discussion skills while preparing students for advanced (C1) proficiency.

Chapters

  • Chapter 1 — Min B2 ilā C1: Al-Diqqah wa-al-Sijill

    Developing greater precision, register awareness, and advanced language control.

  • Chapter 2 — Al-Lughah al-Muʾassasiyyah wa-al-Ijrāʾāt

    Institutional language, formal procedures, and organizational communication.

  • Chapter 3 — Al-Taḥlīl wa-al-Muqāranah al-Mutaqaddimah

    Advanced analytical reading, comparison, and interpretation of information.

  • Chapter 4 — Al-Mawqif wa-al-Ḥijāj wa-al-Dalīl

    Constructing arguments, evaluating evidence, and defending positions.

  • Chapter 5 — Review 1: Khiṭāb Rasmī wa-Taḥlīl

    Integrated review focused on formal communication and analytical skills.

  • Chapter 6 — Qirāʾat al-Iʿlām bi-Ḥadhar

    Critical reading of media sources and responsible information evaluation.

  • Chapter 7 — Al-Muqābalāt wa-al-Niqāsh al-ʿĀmm

    Interviews, public discussion, and structured communication.

  • Chapter 8 — Qaḍāyā Ijtimāʿiyyah wa-Madaniyyah

    Exploring social issues, civic life, and public affairs.

  • Chapter 9 — Al-Lughah al-Qānūniyyah wa-al-Idāriyyah

    Legal, governmental, and administrative language in formal contexts.

  • Chapter 10 — Review 2: Malaff Iʿlāmī wa-ʿĀmm

    Comprehensive review emphasizing media, public communication, and civic discourse.

  • Chapter 11 — Al-Maṣādir wa-al-Tawthīq

    Using sources, citation practices, and documentation standards.

  • Chapter 12 — Al-Kitābah al-Akādīmiyyah

    Academic writing, organization, evidence, and scholarly communication.

  • Chapter 13 — Al-ʿArḍ al-Rasmī wa-al-Asʾilah

    Formal presentations, audience engagement, and question handling.

  • Chapter 14 — Al-Wasāṭah lil-Jumhūr al-Mukhtalif

    Adapting communication for different audiences and contexts.

  • Chapter 15 — Review 3: Mahammah Baḥthiyyah

    Integrated review through research, analysis, and formal communication tasks.

  • Chapter 16 — Al-Uslūb wa-al-Īqāʿ wa-al-Tawkīd

    Developing style, emphasis, tone, and rhetorical effectiveness.

  • Chapter 17 — Nuṣūṣ Thaqāfiyyah wa-Adabiyyah Qarībah

    Reading and interpreting accessible cultural and literary texts.

  • Chapter 18 — Al-Tārīkh wa-al-Dhākirah wa-al-Sard al-ʿĀmm

    Historical narratives, collective memory, and public storytelling.

  • Chapter 19 — Al-Tafāwuḍ wa-Ḥall al-Mushkilāt

    Negotiation, mediation, problem-solving, and conflict resolution.

  • Chapter 20 — Al-Malaff al-Nihāʾī: Kifāyāt C1

    Capstone portfolio demonstrating readiness for advanced C1-level Arabic proficiency.
