Arabic Volume 3 (B1)

Volume 3 develops independent proficiency in Modern Standard Arabic through extended reading, discussion, writing, listening, and formal communication. Students learn to analyze information, explain ideas, present arguments, interpret authentic materials, and communicate confidently across academic, professional, and everyday settings.

Overview

Volume 3 corresponds to the CEFR B1 proficiency level and marks the transition from elementary communication to independent language use. Learners develop greater fluency while strengthening reading comprehension, written expression, oral presentations, and critical listening through authentic texts and real-world communication.

The twenty chapters emphasize analysis, comparison, summarization, argumentation, media literacy, research, and practical communication while preparing students for upper-intermediate (B2) proficiency.

Chapters

  • Chapter 1 — Min B1 ilā B2

    Introduces the transition from intermediate communication toward upper-intermediate proficiency.

  • Chapter 2 — Tajārib wa-Taghyīrāt fī al-Ḥayāh

    Discussing life experiences, personal growth, and significant changes.

  • Chapter 3 — Al-Asbāb wa-al-Natāʾij

    Expressing causes, effects, reasoning, and logical relationships.

  • Chapter 4 — Talkhīṣ wa-Iʿādat Ṣiyāghah

    Summarizing, paraphrasing, and restating information accurately.

  • Chapter 5 — Review 1: Qiṣṣah wa-Taḥlīl

    Integrated review through storytelling, interpretation, and analysis.

  • Chapter 6 — Al-Akhbār wa-al-Maʿlūmāt al-ʿĀmmah

    Understanding news reports, public information, and current events.

  • Chapter 7 — Al-Khadamāt wa-al-Muʾassasāt

    Communicating with organizations, institutions, and public services.

  • Chapter 8 — Al-Tiqniyah wa-al-Ḥayāh al-Raqmiyyah

    Technology, digital communication, and modern life.

  • Chapter 9 — Qaḍāyā al-Mujtamaʿ

    Discussing contemporary social issues and community concerns.

  • Chapter 10 — Review 2: Malaff ʿĀmm

    Comprehensive review emphasizing practical communication across multiple contexts.

  • Chapter 11 — Al-Raʾy wa-al-Mawqif

    Expressing opinions, viewpoints, and personal positions.

  • Chapter 12 — Al-Muqāranah wa-al-Tafsīr

    Comparing ideas while explaining relationships and interpretations.

  • Chapter 13 — Al-Arqām wa-al-Bayānāt

    Interpreting numbers, statistics, charts, and factual information.

  • Chapter 14 — Al-ʿAmaliyyāt wa-al-Waṣf al-Rasmī

    Describing procedures, processes, and formal documentation.

  • Chapter 15 — Review 3: Uqaddimu Ḥujjah

    Integrated review focused on supporting arguments with evidence.

  • Chapter 16 — Al-Thaqāfah wa-al-Huwiyyah wa-al-Tafsīr

    Exploring culture, identity, and interpretation across diverse perspectives.

  • Chapter 17 — Al-Wasāṭah Bayna al-Nuṣūṣ

    Connecting, comparing, and synthesizing information from multiple texts.

  • Chapter 18 — Baḥth Qaṣīr wa-ʿArḍ

    Conducting short research projects and delivering oral presentations.

  • Chapter 19 — Al-Murājaʿah wa-al-Taḥrīr

    Editing, revising, and refining written communication.

  • Chapter 20 — Al-Malaff al-Nihāʾī: Kifāyat B2

    Capstone portfolio demonstrating readiness for upper-intermediate (B2) Arabic proficiency.
