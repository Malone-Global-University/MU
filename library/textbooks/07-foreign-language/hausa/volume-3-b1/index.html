Hausa Volume 3 (B1)

Volume 3 develops independent language use through extended reading, discussion, narration, explanation, presentation, and increasingly formal written communication. By the end of the volume, students are prepared to use Hausa with greater confidence and control at the B1 level.

Overview

Volume 3 corresponds to the CEFR B1 proficiency level and guides learners toward sustained communication across everyday, academic, professional, civic, and cultural contexts while strengthening accuracy and organization. The supplied inventory identifies 20 listed chapter files for this volume.

Lessons address education, careers, media, public information, culture, social issues, travel, institutions, argumentation, research, and extended storytelling while continually integrating vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, reading, listening, speaking, writing, and cumulative review.
