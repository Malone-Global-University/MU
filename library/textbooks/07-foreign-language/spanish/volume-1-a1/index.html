Spanish Volume 1 (A1)

Volume 1 introduces beginning learners to practical communication, foundational grammar, high-frequency vocabulary, pronunciation, reading, listening, speaking, and writing. By the end of the volume, students are prepared to use Spanish with greater confidence and control at the A1 level.

Overview

Volume 1 corresponds to the CEFR A1 proficiency level and serves as the foundation for the language curriculum and develops the core skills needed for simple communication in familiar everyday situations. The supplied inventory identifies 10 listed chapter files for this volume.

Lessons address greetings, personal information, family, home, routines, food, shopping, travel, health, study, work, and basic storytelling while continually integrating vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, reading, listening, speaking, writing, and cumulative review.
