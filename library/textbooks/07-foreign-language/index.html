Foreign Languages

The Foreign Languages shelf brings together practical language textbook seriess that develop speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills while introducing learners to the cultures and traditions of the communities where each language is spoken. These books emphasize real-world communication, grammatical foundations, vocabulary development, and cultural literacy.

Overview

This shelf organizes MGU textbook projects into a comprehensive language-learning pathway. Each textbook is designed to build practical communication skills through structured lessons that combine vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, conversation, reading, writing, listening, and cultural understanding.

Together, these books provide complete introductions to major world languages while encouraging learners to develop confidence in everyday communication and a broader appreciation for the diversity of global cultures.
