Yoruba Volume 2 (A2)

Volume 2 expands elementary communication through longer conversations, practical reading and writing, connected narration, and increasingly varied real-world situations. By the end of the volume, students are prepared to use Yoruba with greater confidence and control at the A2 level.

Overview

Volume 2 corresponds to the CEFR A2 proficiency level and builds on foundational knowledge and develops greater independence when handling routine personal, community, educational, and workplace communication. The supplied inventory identifies 20 listed chapter files for this volume.

Lessons address relationships, housing, services, travel, health, media, education, employment, problem solving, plans, opinions, and recent experiences while continually integrating vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, reading, listening, speaking, writing, and cumulative review.
