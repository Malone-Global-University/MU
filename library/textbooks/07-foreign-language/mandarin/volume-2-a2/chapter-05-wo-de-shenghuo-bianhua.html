综合任务一：我的生活变化

Volume II, Chapter 5: Unit I Assessment — My Life, My Changes · Speak and Write Mandarin Chinese: Everyday Independence and Connected Communication

Chapter Overview and Learning Objectives

Chapters 1 through 4 built an expanded personal profile: a fuller picture of your daily life, your relationships and community, the habits that have changed over time, and a connected personal story anchored in the past. This chapter does not teach anything new. Instead, it asks you to bring those four chapters together into one integrated task: a connected account of your life and how it has changed, moving from who you are now, to the people around you, to what used to be different, to how you got here.

By the end of this chapter, you will be able to:

  1. Combine self-introduction, relationship description, habit contrast, and personal narrative into one connected account.
  2. Use 的 modification, reference cohesion, and topic chains to keep a longer connected text clear.
  3. Distinguish 不 and 没/没有 negation accurately across different sentence types.
  4. Integrate change-of-state 了, perfective 了, and experiential 过 correctly in the same passage.
  5. Sequence events and stages of change using 以前/现在, 后来, and 然后.
  6. Complete an integrated listening, reading, speaking, and writing task at the Unit I level with pragmatically appropriate register.

This is an assessment chapter. No new grammar, no new character set, and no new pronunciation target is introduced here. Every task below draws only on material from Chapters 1–4 and prerequisite Volume I systems.

Communication Scenario

The "我的故事" exchange group is compiling a small collection called "我的生活变化" (Changes in My Life), where each member submits one connected profile that introduces who they are today and traces how they got there. Your task this chapter is to produce your own complete entry and to interact with two other members' entries.

  1. You will listen to 小林's spoken update about her life now and what has changed.
  2. You will read 陈先生's written "我的生活变化" entry.
  3. You will write your own connected "我的生活变化" entry.
  4. You will speak with a partner, introducing your changes and asking about theirs.

听力任务 — Listening Assessment

Listening Assessment

If no speaker is available, this is a Listening Script for Independent Use. Read it aloud yourself, slowly at first, then at a natural pace. Any spoken model should be verified by a fluent Mandarin speaker before being treated as a pronunciation standard.

小林的更新 — Xiao Lin's Update:
大家好，我是小林。我现在的生活跟以前很不一样。以前我一个人住，也不太认识附近的人。后来我搬家了，搬到了一个朋友很多的地方。现在我认识了很多新朋友，也常常跟他们一起吃饭。我以前不喜欢运动，但是最近开始每天早上跑步，慢慢习惯了。我大学的时候学了法语，然后决定学中文，我去过中国一次，是去年。现在我每天都练习说中文，我觉得我的生活变得更有意思了。

听力问题 — Listening comprehension questions:

QuestionAnswer

阅读任务 — Reading Assessment

Reading Assessment

Read 陈先生's entry, then answer the comprehension questions. Pinyin support remains reduced, as in Chapters 1 through 4; rely on familiar characters and context.

我叫陈明，是一名老师。我以前住在一个小城市，工作也很简单。后来我决定搬家，搬到了一个大城市工作。刚搬家的时候，我一个人也不认识，觉得有一点儿不习惯。后来我认识了几个同事，他们常常请我吃饭，我们也常常一起运动。现在我已经习惯了这儿的生活。我以前每天都很晚才睡觉，但是最近开始早一点儿睡，身体也好多了。我大学的时候去过法国一次，那次经历让我决定当老师，教别人学习新的东西。现在我每天都跟学生和朋友说很多话，生活比以前热闹多了。
My name is Chen Ming, and I am a teacher. I used to live in a small city, and my work was simple. Later I decided to move, and moved to a big city for work. When I first moved, I didn't know anyone, and felt a bit unaccustomed to it. Later I got to know a few colleagues, who often invited me to eat, and we also often exercise together. Now I've already gotten used to life here. I used to go to sleep very late every day, but recently I've started sleeping a bit earlier, and my health has improved a lot too. When I was in college I went to France once, and that experience is what made me decide to become a teacher, to teach others to learn new things. Now I talk a lot with students and friends every day, and life is much livelier than before.

理解问题 — Comprehension questions:

  1. 陈明以前住在哪儿？后来呢？
  2. 他刚搬家的时候觉得怎么样？后来怎么样了？
  3. 他最近的生活习惯有什么变化？
  4. 他为什么决定当老师？
口语任务 — Speaking Assessment

Speaking Assessment

控制性练习 — Controlled practice: guided interview

With a partner, take turns asking and answering: 你现在的生活跟以前有什么不一样？你认识了什么新朋友？你搬过家吗？你最近开始做什么新的事情？

交际任务 — Communicative task: 我的生活变化

With a partner, take turns telling a connected account of your life and how it has changed. Include a self-introduction, at least one relationship description using 的 modification, at least one habit contrast using 以前/现在, at least one perfective 了 and one experiential 过, and at least one sequencing word (后来 or 然后). Ask your partner at least one follow-up question about their story.

独立练习 — Solo alternative

Record yourself giving a two-minute connected account of your life now and how it has changed, using at least eight connected sentences that draw on Chapters 1 through 4.

写作任务 — Writing Assessment

Writing Assessment

Write a connected entry of eight to twelve sentences titled 我的生活变化 for the exchange group's collection.

写作任务 — Writing task: Type your entry with Pinyin input if possible, or handwrite the characters you have already been assigned for handwriting. Your entry must include:

  1. An opening self-introduction, recycling Chapter 1 material (name, where you live or work, basic profile).
  2. At least one relationship description using 的 modification and an appropriate title or name (Chapter 2).
  3. At least one habit-and-change contrast using 以前 and 现在, marked with change-of-state 了 (Chapter 3).
  4. At least one …的时候 time anchor, placing an event at a specific period (Chapter 4).
  5. At least one perfective 了 and at least one experiential 过, used correctly and distinctly (Chapter 4).
  6. At least one sequencing word (后来 or 然后) connecting two stages of your account (Chapter 4).
  7. A closing sentence connecting your account to the present.

修改清单 — Editing checklist:

  • All seven required elements are present and connected into one account, not a disconnected list of sentences.
  • 不 and 没/没有 are each used correctly for their own negation function.
  • 了 is used only for specific completed events or change of state, and 过 only for general life experience.
  • 的 modification is used accurately for possession and description, not omitted where it is required.
  • 后来 and 然后 are not confused with each other or overused in the same short entry.
  • A Chinese period (。) ends each sentence, and commas (，) separate connected clauses.
发音和读写复习 — Pronunciation and Literacy Check

Pronunciation and Literacy Check

This chapter introduces no new sounds and no new character set. Use the checklist below to confirm control of Unit I sound and literacy targets before moving on.

  • Third-tone sandhi in connected phrases such as 我以前 (wǒ yǐqián) and 我很好 (wǒ hěn hǎo).
  • Neutral tone in familiar words such as 朋友 (péngyou), 故事 (gùshi), and 时候 (shíhou).
  • Clear distinction between perfective 了 and experiential 过 when reading your own writing aloud.
  • Accurate reading of all Unit I recognition, typing, and scheduled handwriting characters without new unscheduled characters appearing in your own work.

Culture and Communication

Culture Note — Sharing a life update

When Mandarin speakers give a longer personal update in many contexts — to a friend group, a class, or a colleague — they commonly move through the account in a clear time order and often connect a personal change to the people around them, rather than focusing only on individual achievement. How much detail is shared about work, health, or personal routines, and how directly a change is framed as positive or difficult, varies by relationship, generation, and individual personality, so it is common to keep a life update warm and connective rather than purely factual.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Treating the whole entry as a list of separate facts: use topic chains, 的 modification, and sequencing words so that sentences connect into one account, not four unrelated chapters stapled together.
  • Confusing 不 and 没/没有: use 不 for general, habitual, or future statements and 没/没有 for completed events, existence, or possession.
  • Using 过 for a specific completed action, or 了 for a general life experience: keep the Chapter 4 distinction intact even when combining it with new material from earlier chapters.
  • Dropping 的 in required modification: a possessive or descriptive relationship between a person and a noun usually still needs 的 outside of a few close, fixed relationships.

You reviewed self-introduction and paragraph expansion, relationship description with 的 modification, habit-and-change contrast with 不/没 negation, and personal narrative built from perfective 了, experiential 过, …的时候, 后来, and 然后. Answer Key

Listening Assessment

  • Before, she lived alone and didn't know people nearby well; now she knows many new friends and often eats with them.
  • She later moved to a place with many friends.
  • She recently started jogging every morning.
  • Yes, she has been to China once, last year.

Reading Assessment — Comprehension

  1. Chen Ming used to live in a small city; later he moved to a big city for work.
  2. When he first moved he felt a bit unaccustomed to it; later he got to know colleagues and got used to life there.
  3. He used to go to sleep very late; recently he has started sleeping a bit earlier, and his health has improved.
  4. An experience going to France in college made him decide to become a teacher.
