单元二综合任务：一天的生活

Volume I, Chapter 10: Unit II Integrated Assessment — A Day in Life · Speak and Write Mandarin Chinese: Foundations of Practical Communication

Assessment Objectives

This chapter checks whether you can:

  1. Introduce yourself and talk about your family using 是, 有, and 的.
  2. Tell the day, date, and time and describe a daily routine using time-before-verb order.
  3. Talk about food and drink preferences using 喜欢, 想, and 要.
  4. Describe where things and people are using 在 and 有.
  5. Ask prices, count items with measure words, and react to a price with 太...了.
  6. Produce controlled but connected spoken and written Mandarin that combines several of these systems in one task.

This chapter introduces no new grammar, no new characters beyond the productive inventory of Chapters 1-9, and no new pronunciation system. It synthesizes and assesses what you have already studied.

Communication Scenario

莎拉, 大卫, and 小林 have finished their first weeks together — meeting each other, talking about family, learning the campus, eating together, and shopping near school. This chapter asks you to put those pieces together by following one full day in 莎拉's life, from morning to evening.

  1. You will listen to, read, write, and speak about a single connected day, using only the vocabulary, characters, and grammar introduced in Chapters 1-9.
  2. No new words, characters, or sentence patterns appear in this chapter. If something looks unfamiliar, it is meant to be recognizable from an earlier chapter — review that chapter before continuing.

Where This Chapter Fits

Chapter 10 closes Unit II. It does not add a new communicative domain. Instead, it asks you to move fluidly between the identity, family, time, food, routine, location, and shopping systems from Chapters 1-9 inside a single connected task.

Coming Into This Chapter

Identity and greetings (Ch. 1-2), family and possession with 的/有 (Ch. 3), numbers, dates, and time (Ch. 4), food and drink preferences (Ch. 6), daily routines (Ch. 7), location with 在 (Ch. 8), and shopping and quantities (Ch. 9).

New in This Chapter

Nothing grammatically new. The only new element is the demand to combine these systems in longer, connected listening, reading, writing, and speaking tasks.

Looking Forward

Unit III begins in Chapter 11 with ability and communication (会/能/可以), building directly on the confidence and control you demonstrate here.

Listening Assessment

Ask your instructor, a study partner, or a fluent Mandarin speaker to read the passage below aloud twice, at a natural pace, before you answer the questions.

If no speaker is available, this is a Listening Script for Independent Use. Read it aloud yourself, slowly at first, then at a natural pace. Any spoken model should be verified by a fluent Mandarin speaker before being treated as a pronunciation standard.

莎拉的一天 — Sarah's Day:
"大家好，我是莎拉。今天是星期六。我家有四个人：爸爸，妈妈，弟弟，和我。早上我七点起床，八点去学校。中午我很饿，我想喝茶，也喜欢吃面包。下午我和小林去商店买东西。我买了两个苹果，一共六块钱。我也想买一本书，可是太贵了，我没有买。晚上我在家，我的书在桌子上。今天是很好的一天！"

听力任务 — Listening task:

QuestionAnswer
莎拉家有几个人？都是谁？ (How many people are in Sarah's family? Who are they?)______
莎拉几点起床？几点去学校？ (What time does Sarah get up? What time does she go to school?)______
莎拉买了什么？多少钱？ (What did Sarah buy? How much?)______
莎拉为什么没有买书？ (Why didn't Sarah buy a book?)______

Reading Assessment

Read the short diary entry below and answer the comprehension questions.

大卫的日记 — David's Diary
今天是星期天。我每天七点半起床，可是星期天我八点起床。我家有三个人：爸爸，妈妈，和我。我没有弟弟，也没有妹妹。中午我在家，我喜欢喝茶，不喜欢喝咖啡。下午我去图书馆，图书馆在学校旁边。晚上我和朋友去商店。我朋友买了一杯茶，五块钱，不贵。我没有买东西，因为我没有钱。
Today is Sunday. I get up at 7:30 every day, but on Sundays I get up at 8:00. There are three people in my family: my dad, my mom, and me. I don't have a younger brother or a younger sister. At noon I'm at home; I like drinking tea, not coffee. In the afternoon I go to the library, which is next to school. In the evening my friend and I go to the shop. My friend bought a cup of tea, five kuai, not expensive. I didn't buy anything, because I don't have money.

理解问题 — Comprehension questions:

  1. 大卫平常几点起床？星期天呢？ (What time does David usually get up? And on Sundays?)
  2. 大卫家有几个人？他有弟弟吗？ (How many people in David's family? Does he have a younger brother?)
  3. 大卫为什么没有买东西？ (Why didn't David buy anything?)

Writing Assessment

Write a connected description of your own day, using only vocabulary, characters, and grammar from Chapters 1-9.

写作任务 — Writing task: Write eight to ten sentences in simplified characters, typing them with Pinyin input if possible, or copying/handwriting the characters you have already been assigned for handwriting. Your description must include:

  1. A self-introduction. Give your name and at least one fact about your family using 有 or 的.
  2. A time expression. State what time you do something, placed correctly before the verb.
  3. A preference. Use 喜欢, 想, or 要 to describe a food or drink preference.
  4. A location. Use 在 to say where you or something is.
  5. A shopping detail. Include a price, a measure word, or a reaction with 太...了.

修改清单 — Editing checklist:

  • Time expressions come before the verb, not after.
  • A measure word appears between every number and its noun.
  • 两, not 二, is used directly before a measure word.
  • 不 and 没/没有 are not used interchangeably.
  • A Chinese period (。) or question mark (？) ends each sentence.

Speaking Assessment

控制性练习 — Controlled practice: guided interview

With a partner, take turns asking and answering these questions aloud: 你叫什么名字？你家有几个人？你几点起床？你喜欢喝什么？你的书在哪儿？

交际任务 — Communicative task: describe your day

Without reading from a script, describe your typical day to a partner in five or six connected sentences: when you get up, what you like to eat or drink, where you go, and something you bought recently.

独立练习 — Solo alternative

Record yourself describing your day using the same five or six sentences. Listen back and check your tones, your 一 and 不 sandhi, and your measure-word choices.

Culture and Communication Check

Culture Review — Putting it together

Across Chapters 1-9 you have practiced culturally appropriate greetings, family reference, classroom etiquette, and shop interactions such as addressing a shopkeeper as 老板. As you complete this chapter's tasks, keep using these appropriately — for example, greeting a partner properly before an interview task, or using polite, friendly language when role-playing a shop exchange.

Chapter Review

部分 A — 理解 (Comprehension)

1. Based on the listening passage, answer in English:

  1. Who is in Sarah's family?
  2. What did Sarah do in the afternoon, and why didn't she buy a book?

部分 B — 语法 (Grammar)

1. Choose the correct negation, 不 or 没/没有:

  1. 我___喝咖啡。 (I don't drink coffee — habitual)
  2. 我___去学校。 (I did not go to school — completed event)
  3. 我___钱。 (I don't have money — possession)

  1. 大卫喜欢喝什么？不喜欢喝什么？ (What does David like to drink? What doesn't he like?)
