Business and Economics

The Business and Economics shelf brings together practical textbooks on enterprise, market systems, personal finance, and decision-making. These books support entrepreneurial thinking, financial literacy, and a working understanding of how organizations and economies operate.

Overview

This shelf organizes MGU textbook projects into a focused business and economics pathway. The collection emphasizes the foundations of business activity, the logic of markets, the role of institutions, and the personal financial skills required for long-term planning.

Each textbook is designed as a complete PDF learning resource with chapter-by-chapter structure, examples, reviews, and applied concepts. Together, these books create a clear entry point for students, entrepreneurs, and general readers who want a disciplined introduction to commerce and economics.
