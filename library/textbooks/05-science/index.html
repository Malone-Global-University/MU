Science Shelf

The Science Shelf organizes complete course-style learning PDF ebooks across the natural sciences. Each textbook is designed for sustained study, chapter-by-chapter instruction, conceptual development, and long-term reference.

Overview

This shelf gathers MGU science textbook projects into a single index. The collection includes astronomy and cosmology, biology, chemistry, Earth and environmental science, and physics.

These textbooks are designed to move learners from foundational observation and measurement into scientific reasoning, experimentation, models, and applied problem solving. They are complete PDF textbooks rather than short reference pages.

Science

Textbooks in this shelf introduce the natural sciences through observation, measurement, models, experiments, matter, energy, life, Earth systems, space, and physical law.

  • Astronomy and Cosmology Foundations

    An astronomy and cosmology textbook covering the sky, telescopes, planets, stars, galaxies, gravity, spacetime, cosmic history, and the structure of the universe.

  • Biology

    A biology textbook covering cells, genetics, evolution, ecology, organisms, anatomy, physiology, classification, biological systems, and the study of life.

  • Chemistry

    A chemistry textbook covering matter, atoms, molecules, reactions, stoichiometry, bonding, thermochemistry, solutions, acids and bases, equilibrium, and chemical systems.

  • Earth and Environmental Science

    An Earth and environmental science textbook covering geology, weather, climate, oceans, ecosystems, natural resources, hazards, and human-environment relationships.

Physics

Physics books study motion, forces, energy, waves, fields, matter, light, modern physics, and the mathematical laws that describe the physical universe.

  • High School Physics

    A high school physics textbook covering measurement, motion, forces, energy, momentum, waves, sound, light, electricity, magnetism, and modern physics foundations.
