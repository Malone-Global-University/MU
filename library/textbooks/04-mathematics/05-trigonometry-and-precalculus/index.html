05-Trigonometry and Precalculus

Trigonometry and Precalculus bridge algebra and calculus through the study of trigonometric functions, advanced algebraic techniques, analytic geometry, sequences, series, vectors, and mathematical modeling.

Overview

This textbook prepares students for calculus by integrating advanced algebra, trigonometry, and function analysis. Students investigate periodic behavior, mathematical modeling, conic sections, vectors, polar coordinates, and analytical techniques used throughout higher mathematics, engineering, computer science, economics, and the physical sciences.

Learning Outcomes

  • Analyze and graph trigonometric functions.
  • Apply trigonometric identities and equations.
  • Model periodic and cyclical phenomena.
  • Investigate advanced polynomial, rational, exponential, and logarithmic functions.
  • Use vectors, matrices, and analytic geometry to solve quantitative problems.
  • Analyze sequences, series, and mathematical patterns.
  • Develop the mathematical foundation necessary for success in calculus.
