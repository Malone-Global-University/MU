01-Arithmetic

Arithmetic is the foundation of all mathematics. This textbook develops number sense, operations, fractions, decimals, ratios, percentages, measurement, estimation, and quantitative problem solving.

Overview

This textbook introduces the core computational skills used throughout mathematics, science, business, finance, and everyday life. Students learn how numbers work, how calculations are performed, and how arithmetic supports higher-level mathematics.

Learning Outcomes

  • Perform arithmetic operations accurately.
  • Work confidently with fractions, decimals, and percentages.
  • Solve quantitative word problems.
  • Apply ratios, proportions, and measurement concepts.
  • Develop readiness for algebra and higher mathematics.
