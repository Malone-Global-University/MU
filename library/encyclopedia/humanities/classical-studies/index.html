Classical Studies Overview

Classical studies examine the civilizations, intellectual traditions, literature, philosophy, and cultural systems of the ancient Mediterranean world. The field focuses particularly on Greek and Roman societies while situating them within broader classical antiquity and Mediterranean cultural development.

Disciplines

  • Ancient Mediterranean Culture

    Study broader cultural systems of the ancient Mediterranean world.

  • Classical Literature

    Examine literary works and traditions of classical antiquity.

  • Classical Philosophy

    Study philosophical traditions of the ancient Greek and Roman worlds.

  • Greek Civilization

    Explore society, politics, culture, and thought of ancient Greece.

  • Roman Civilization

    Study institutions, culture, and development of ancient Rome.
