Philosophy Overview

Philosophy studies foundational questions concerning reality, knowledge, morality, reasoning, beauty, mind, science, and political order. The field uses rational analysis and argumentation to investigate the most basic principles underlying human thought and existence.

Disciplines

  • Aesthetics

    Study philosophical questions concerning beauty, art, and aesthetic judgment.

  • Epistemology

    Examine the nature, sources, and limits of knowledge.

  • Ethics

    Study moral principles governing right and wrong conduct.

  • Logic

    Explore principles of valid reasoning and formal argument structure.

  • Metaphysics

    Study the nature of reality, existence, and being.

  • Philosophy of Mind

    Examine philosophical questions concerning consciousness and mentality.

  • Philosophy of Science

    Study philosophical foundations and implications of scientific inquiry.

  • Political Philosophy

    Explore philosophical questions concerning state, justice, and governance.
