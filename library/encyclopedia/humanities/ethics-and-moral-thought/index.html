Ethics and Moral Thought Overview

Ethics and moral thought study principles governing right action, moral judgment, virtue, duty, and ethical reasoning. The field examines both theoretical moral philosophy and practical frameworks for applying ethical reasoning to real-world decisions and dilemmas.

Disciplines

  • Applied Ethics

    Study practical application of ethical principles to specific domains and dilemmas.

  • Ethical Frameworks

    Examine structured systems for evaluating moral choices and conduct.

  • Moral Philosophy

    Study philosophical foundations of morality and ethical judgment.

  • Normative Ethics

    Explore theories concerning standards of right and wrong action.

  • Virtue Ethics

    Study moral systems centered on character and virtuous conduct.
