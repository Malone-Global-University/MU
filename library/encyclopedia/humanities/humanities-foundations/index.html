Humanities Foundations Overview

Humanities foundations study the theoretical, methodological, and philosophical principles underlying the interpretation of human culture, thought, expression, and meaning. The field examines how humanistic disciplines analyze texts, ideas, symbols, and cultural artifacts across contexts and traditions.

Disciplines

  • Comparative Humanities

    Study humanistic themes and traditions across cultures and disciplines.

  • Critical Analysis

    Examine structured methods of evaluating texts, ideas, and cultural works.

  • Humanities Theory

    Study theoretical frameworks guiding humanistic scholarship and inquiry.

  • Interpretation Methods

    Explore approaches for interpreting meaning in texts, symbols, and artifacts.

  • Philosophy of Culture

    Study philosophical examination of culture, meaning, and human expression.
