Culinary Specializations Overview

Culinary specializations study focused areas of advanced food preparation that require distinct technical methods, ingredient knowledge, and specialized craftsmanship. These disciplines expand beyond general cooking into dedicated domains of preparation, preservation, and service. They represent specialized professional tracks within culinary arts and food production.

Disciplines

  • Butchery

    Study breakdown, trimming, fabrication, and preparation of meat cuts from whole animals.

  • Charcuterie

    Examine preparation and preservation of cured, smoked, and processed meat products.

  • Garde Manger

    Study cold food preparation including salads, terrines, platters, and decorative presentation.

  • Plant-Based Cuisine

    Explore culinary techniques focused on vegetables, grains, legumes, and non-animal ingredients.

  • Seafood Preparation

    Study cleaning, fabrication, cooking, and handling of fish and shellfish.
