Defense Technology Overview

Defense technology studies the systems, platforms, and technical tools used to enhance military capability, situational awareness, coordination, and battlefield effectiveness. The field examines communications, surveillance, electronic warfare, autonomous systems, and integrated combat technologies. These disciplines connect engineering innovation with operational military application.

Disciplines

  • Autonomous Systems

    Study unmanned and automated military systems capable of semi-independent operation.

  • Battlefield Systems

    Examine integrated technological systems supporting battlefield awareness and combat operations.

  • Communications Systems

    Study military communication networks, devices, and command information systems.

  • Electronic Warfare

    Explore military use of the electromagnetic spectrum for offense, defense, and disruption.

  • Surveillance Systems

    Study technologies used for reconnaissance, monitoring, detection, and observation.
