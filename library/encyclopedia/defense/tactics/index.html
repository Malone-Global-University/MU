Tactics Overview

Tactics study the methods by which military units engage and defeat opposing forces in direct combat. The field examines maneuver, fire, terrain use, formation, coordination, and battlefield decision-making at the unit and engagement level. Tactical disciplines govern how forces fight effectively in immediate operational contexts.

Disciplines

  • Armored Warfare

    Study tactical employment of armored vehicles and mechanized combat forces.

  • Artillery Employment

    Examine tactical use of indirect fire systems in support of combat operations.

  • Infantry Tactics

    Study maneuver and combat methods of infantry forces in battle.

  • Maneuver Warfare

    Explore tactical doctrine emphasizing speed, disruption, and positional advantage.

  • Small Unit Operations

    Study combat tactics and coordination of squads, platoons, and other small units.
