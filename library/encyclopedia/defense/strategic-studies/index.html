Strategic Studies Overview

Strategic studies examine how military, political, economic, and informational power are integrated to achieve long-term national or organizational objectives in conflict and competition. The field studies deterrence, escalation, strategic planning, campaign design, and the broader logic of force application. It governs high-level defense decision-making and strategic thought.

Disciplines

  • Campaign Design

    Study strategic structuring of campaigns to achieve operational and political objectives.

  • Deterrence Theory

    Examine use of threat and credibility to prevent adversary action.

  • Escalation Dynamics

    Study how conflicts intensify, expand, or de-escalate over time.

  • Grand Strategy

    Study integration of national resources and power toward overarching strategic goals.

  • Strategic Analysis

    Examine structured assessment of strategic options, environments, and risks.
