Weapons Systems Overview

Weapons systems study the design, function, deployment, and operational use of military weapon platforms across land, sea, and air domains. The field examines individual weapons, vehicle-mounted systems, missile technology, artillery, and integrated weapons platforms. These disciplines focus on the technical and tactical application of offensive and defensive armament systems.

Disciplines

  • Aerospace Weapons

    Study weapons systems deployed from or against air and space platforms.

  • Armored Vehicles

    Examine combat vehicles designed for protected battlefield mobility and firepower.

  • Artillery Systems

    Study indirect fire weapons and associated artillery delivery platforms.

  • Missile Systems

    Explore guided and unguided missile technologies and deployment systems.

  • Naval Weapons

    Study weapon systems employed in maritime combat operations.

  • Small Arms

    Study handheld and crew-served individual weapon systems.
