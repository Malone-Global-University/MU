Chemical Engineering Overview

Chemical engineering studies the design, optimization, and operation of processes that transform raw materials into useful chemical products. The field examines reaction systems, process flows, fluid and heat transport, separations, and industrial chemical production. It connects chemistry with industrial-scale engineering systems.

Disciplines

  • Industrial Chemicals

    Study large-scale production and engineering of industrial chemical substances.

  • Process Design

    Examine engineering of chemical processing systems and industrial production workflows.

  • Reaction Engineering

    Study design and optimization of chemical reaction systems and reactors.

  • Separation Processes

    Explore engineering methods for separating chemical mixtures and components.

  • Thermofluids

    Study combined heat transfer, thermodynamics, and fluid transport in process systems.
