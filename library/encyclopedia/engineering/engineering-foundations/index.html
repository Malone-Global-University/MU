Engineering Foundations Overview

Engineering foundations study the universal principles, tools, and conceptual frameworks that underpin all engineering disciplines. The field examines mathematical analysis, physical laws, technical communication, structured design methodology, and systems-level reasoning. These foundations provide the analytical and conceptual base for specialized engineering practice.

Disciplines

  • Engineering Design

    Study structured processes for developing engineered solutions to technical problems.

  • Engineering Mathematics

    Examine mathematical methods used in engineering analysis and modeling.

  • Engineering Physics

    Study physical principles and laws applied in engineering systems.

  • Systems Thinking

    Explore holistic analysis of interconnected engineering systems and components.

  • Technical Drawing

    Study graphical communication of engineering designs and specifications.
