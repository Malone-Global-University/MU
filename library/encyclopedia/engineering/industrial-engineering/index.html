Industrial Engineering Overview

Industrial engineering studies the optimization of complex systems involving people, processes, materials, equipment, and information. The field examines efficiency, workflow, operations research, quality systems, ergonomics, and production design. It focuses on improving system performance and operational effectiveness in industrial environments.

Disciplines

  • Ergonomics

    Study design of systems and environments for safe and efficient human use.

  • Operations Research

    Examine mathematical optimization and decision-making methods for complex operations.

  • Process Optimization

    Study systematic improvement of workflows and operational processes.

  • Production Systems

    Explore engineering of manufacturing and production workflows.

  • Quality Engineering

    Study systems for ensuring consistency, reliability, and product quality.
