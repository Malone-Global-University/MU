Electrical Engineering Overview

Electrical engineering studies the generation, control, transmission, and application of electricity and electrical signals. The field examines circuits, electronics, power systems, control systems, and signal processing across electrical technologies. It governs the engineering of systems powered by or operating through electrical phenomena.

Disciplines

  • Circuit Analysis

    Study behavior and design of electrical circuits and network systems.

  • Control Systems

    Examine automated regulation and feedback systems in engineered devices.

  • Electronics

    Study electronic components, semiconductor devices, and circuit applications.

  • Power Systems

    Explore generation, transmission, and distribution of electrical power.

  • Signal Processing

    Study analysis and transformation of analog and digital signals.
