Intelligence and Covert Operations Overview

Intelligence and covert operations study secret information-gathering, clandestine operational methods, covert intervention, and counterintelligence practices used by intelligence services and covert actors. The field examines both the practical mechanics and strategic purpose of hidden operations. It provides operational context for understanding clandestine statecraft and covert organizational behavior.

Disciplines

  • Clandestine Networks

    Study hidden organizational and logistical structures supporting covert activity.

  • Counterintelligence

    Examine defensive operations against espionage, infiltration, and intelligence compromise.

  • Covert Action

    Study concealed intervention activities intended to influence foreign or domestic outcomes.

  • Espionage

    Explore secret intelligence collection through agents, surveillance, and infiltration.

  • Intelligence Tradecraft

    Learn methods, tools, and operational practices used in intelligence work.
