Data Systems Overview

Data systems study the storage, organization, processing, retrieval, and analysis of structured and unstructured information. The field focuses on designing systems that manage data reliably, efficiently, and at scale. Across databases, modeling, engineering, retrieval, big data, and data science, these disciplines govern how information is transformed into usable knowledge.

Disciplines

  • Big Data Systems

    Study distributed infrastructure for storing and processing large-scale datasets.

  • Data Engineering

    Learn construction of pipelines and infrastructure for reliable data movement and transformation.

  • Data Modeling

    Study structured representation of data entities, relationships, and schemas.

  • Data Science

    Examine computational analysis and interpretation of data for insight generation.

  • Database Systems

    Study structured storage, querying, indexing, and management of persistent data.

  • Information Retrieval

    Explore systems for indexing, searching, and ranking stored information.
