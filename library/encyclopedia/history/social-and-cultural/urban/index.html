Urban History Overview

Urban history studies the growth, structure, and transformation of cities across time. It explores how migration, economic change, policy decisions, and social forces shape urban environments and the lived experiences of their residents.

Within Social and Cultural History, urban history focuses on how everyday life, institutions, and communities develop within metropolitan settings.
