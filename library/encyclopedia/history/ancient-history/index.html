Ancient History Overview

Ancient history studies the civilizations, political systems, cultures, and historical events of the ancient world from the rise of early states through the decline of classical antiquity. The field examines foundational societies whose institutions, ideas, and developments shaped later human history across multiple world regions.

Disciplines

  • Ancient Americas

    Study pre-Columbian civilizations and societies of the ancient Americas.

  • Ancient China

    Examine early Chinese civilizations, dynasties, and historical development.

  • Ancient Egypt

    Study civilization, governance, religion, and society of ancient Egypt.

  • Ancient Greece

    Explore Greek civilization, politics, philosophy, and culture in antiquity.

  • Ancient India

    Study early Indian civilizations, empires, and cultural development.

  • Ancient Near East

    Examine early civilizations of Mesopotamia and surrounding regions.

  • Ancient Rome

    Study Roman civilization from republic through empire and decline.
