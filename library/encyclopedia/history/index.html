History Overview

History is the systematic study of past human events, societies, institutions, and ideas through the analysis of evidence, sources, and chronological development. It seeks to understand how civilizations emerge, transform, and decline by examining political, military, economic, social, cultural, and intellectual change over time. Historical study combines methodological rigor with interpretive analysis to reconstruct the past and explain the forces that shape long-term human development.
