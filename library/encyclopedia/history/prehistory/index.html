Prehistory Overview

Prehistory studies the human past prior to written documentation through archaeological, anthropological, and material evidence. The field examines technological, social, and cultural development across early human societies from the earliest stone tools through the rise of literate civilizations.

Disciplines

  • Bronze Age

    Study societies characterized by bronze metallurgy and early complex civilization.

  • Iron Age

    Examine societies marked by iron metallurgy and expanding state systems.

  • Mesolithic

    Study transitional hunter-gatherer cultures between Paleolithic and Neolithic periods.

  • Neolithic

    Explore the agricultural revolution and emergence of settled societies.

  • Paleolithic

    Study earliest human societies and Stone Age hunter-gatherer cultures.
