Style and Influence

Paperboy is known for smooth, upbeat West Coast rap delivery that emphasized melody, accessibility, and early 1990s radio-oriented hip-hop production.

His breakout single “Ditty” helped define a transitional moment when West Coast hip-hop expanded into mainstream pop charts while maintaining regional identity.

Legacy

Paperboy remains a notable figure in early 1990s hip-hop for his crossover success during the genre’s commercial expansion period.

Though his catalog is relatively compact, his influence persists through his contribution to the smooth, melodic West Coast sound that shaped early rap radio programming.
