Style and Influence

Iggy Azalea emerged as a prominent figure in early 2010s pop-rap, combining Southern-influenced trap production with mainstream pop structures and highly polished visual branding.

Her success reflected the growing globalization of hip-hop and the increasing influence of digital platforms in shaping rap stardom.

Legacy

Iggy Azalea’s career highlights the intersection of internet-era music promotion and pop-rap crossover success in the 2010s.

Her impact is most visible in discussions of global hip-hop diffusion and the evolution of commercially driven rap aesthetics.
