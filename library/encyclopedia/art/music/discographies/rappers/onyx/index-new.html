Style and Influence

Onyx is known for their raw, high-energy delivery style, marked by shouted vocals, aggressive cadence, and an abrasive sonic identity that defined hardcore East Coast rap in the early 1990s.

Their music helped expand the aesthetic boundaries of hip-hop performance, emphasizing intensity, crowd participation, and street-oriented storytelling.

Legacy

Onyx remains a defining group in hardcore hip-hop, with their debut album Bacdafucup considered a landmark release in 1990s rap history.

Their influence continues through later revival projects and their impact on aggressive rap performance styles across multiple generations.
