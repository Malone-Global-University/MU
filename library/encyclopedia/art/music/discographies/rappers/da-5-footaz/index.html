Style and Influence

Da 5 Footaz are associated with West Coast gangsta rap, delivering raw street narratives from a female perspective within a heavily male-dominated era of hip-hop.

Their presence helped broaden visibility for women in underground Los Angeles rap during the late 1990s and early 2000s.

Legacy

The group is remembered for their contribution to West Coast underground culture and for representing female voices in gangsta rap during its commercial peak.

Their work continues to be revisited in discussions of overlooked contributors to 1990s Los Angeles hip-hop.
