Style and Influence

Da Brat is known for her fast-paced flow, playful lyricism, and ability to bridge hardcore rap with radio-friendly appeal during the 1990s.

She helped define the early success of So So Def Records and opened doors for commercially successful female rappers in hip-hop.

Legacy

Funkdafied marked a historic moment as one of the first platinum-certified solo female rap albums.

Da Brat’s catalog remains influential in discussions of 1990s female rap and the Atlanta hip-hop movement.
