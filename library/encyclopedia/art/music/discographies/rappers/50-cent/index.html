Certifications and Legacy

50 Cent emerged as one of the defining rap stars of the 2000s, combining hard-edged street narratives with a highly commercial sound that reached a wide mainstream audience. His breakout success helped shape the sound and marketing of hip-hop in the early twenty-first century.

Get Rich or Die Tryin' and The Massacre are among the most important rap albums of their era, while his work with G-Unit extended his influence across group projects, mixtapes, and artist development.
