Style and Influence

Montell Jordan is recognized for merging smooth R&B vocals with hip-hop production aesthetics, creating a crossover-friendly sound that helped define 1990s urban radio.

His work at Def Jam contributed to a broader shift in R&B toward beat-driven, club-oriented arrangements while maintaining traditional soul-influenced vocal delivery.

Legacy

Montell Jordan’s impact is anchored by the enduring success of This Is How We Do It, one of the most recognizable R&B singles of the 1990s and a defining track of its era.

His catalog reflects the evolution of mainstream R&B during a key transitional period between classic soul and modern hip-hop–influenced production styles.
