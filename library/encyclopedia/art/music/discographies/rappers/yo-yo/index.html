Style and Sound

Yo-Yo’s music combines socially conscious lyricism with West Coast G-funk production, often addressing gender equality, empowerment, and street-level realities.

Her delivery blends assertive storytelling with melodic hooks influenced by early 1990s West Coast hip-hop production styles.

Legacy

Yo-Yo is regarded as a pioneering voice for women in West Coast hip-hop, helping define a space for female MCs in a male-dominated genre.

Her collaborations with major West Coast artists and her advocacy work have made her an enduring figure in hip-hop culture.
