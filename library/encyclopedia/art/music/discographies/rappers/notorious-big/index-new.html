Style and Influence

The Notorious B.I.G. is widely recognized for his smooth, conversational flow and vivid storytelling, blending street narratives with charisma and technical precision.

His work defined the sound of mid-1990s East Coast hip-hop and helped elevate Bad Boy Records into a dominant force in mainstream rap music.

Legacy

Despite a brief recording career, The Notorious B.I.G.’s catalog is considered one of the most influential in hip-hop history, with both studio albums achieving landmark status.

His storytelling approach, flow structure, and posthumous cultural presence continue to shape generations of MCs and producers.
