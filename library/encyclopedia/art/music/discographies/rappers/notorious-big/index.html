Certifications and Legacy

The Notorious B.I.G. remains one of the most acclaimed and influential figures in hip-hop history. Despite a recording career that lasted only a few years, his technical lyricism, storytelling ability, and commercial success helped define the sound of East Coast rap during the 1990s.

Albums such as Ready to Die and Life After Death are widely regarded as classics of the genre, while his posthumous releases introduced his music to new generations of listeners and further cemented his place in popular culture.
