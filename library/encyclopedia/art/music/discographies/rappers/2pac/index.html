Certifications and Legacy

Tupac Shakur remains one of the most studied and influential figures in hip-hop history, with a catalog that spans socially conscious early work, mainstream crossover success, and posthumous releases that extended his cultural impact well beyond his lifetime.

Albums such as Me Against the World and All Eyez on Me are widely regarded as landmark recordings, while his later posthumous catalog helped shape how unfinished recordings are curated and released in modern hip-hop.
