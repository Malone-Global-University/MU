Style and Influence

Digable Planets are known for their smooth jazz-infused production, laid-back lyrical delivery, and intellectual approach to hip-hop rooted in Afrocentric philosophy and urban storytelling.

Their work helped define the jazz rap movement of the early 1990s and expanded the boundaries of alternative hip-hop aesthetics.

Legacy

Reachin’ (A New Refutation of Time and Space) remains one of the most influential jazz rap albums ever released.

Digable Planets continue to be cited as foundational figures in alternative hip-hop, bridging jazz traditions with modern rap innovation.
