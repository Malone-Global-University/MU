Certifications and Legacy

Cardi B emerged from mixtape culture and social media visibility to become one of the defining rap stars of the 2010s and 2020s. Her music blended sharp wit, bold personality, and pop crossover appeal, helping her build one of the most visible careers in contemporary hip-hop.

Invasion of Privacy established her as a major album artist, while her later singles and collaborations kept her at the center of mainstream rap and pop conversation.
