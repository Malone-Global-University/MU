Style and Sound

Wiz Khalifa’s music blends laid-back delivery, melodic hooks, and stoner rap aesthetics shaped by Pittsburgh underground influences and West Coast collaboration.

His work moves between mixtape experimentation and polished mainstream production, often emphasizing vibe-driven songwriting over lyrical density.

Legacy

Wiz Khalifa is one of the most successful mixtape-era rappers to transition into mainstream global pop-rap success.

His influence is strongly tied to the blog-era hip-hop movement and the commercialization of internet-driven rap careers.
