Style and Influence

Sean Combs helped define the sound and business model of 1990s hip-hop commercialization, combining polished production, sampling-heavy beats, and mainstream-friendly songwriting.

His work as both performer and executive established a blueprint for hip-hop entrepreneurship and artist branding in the modern music industry.

Legacy

Sean Combs remains one of the most influential figures in hip-hop business and culture, credited with expanding rap’s global commercial reach through Bad Boy Records and crossover pop success.

His catalog reflects both his solo work and his broader impact as a producer shaping multiple generations of artists.
