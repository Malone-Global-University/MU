Style and Influence

Project Pat is known for his laid-back but gritty Memphis flow, combining hypnotic delivery with street-centered storytelling and a strong connection to the Hypnotize Minds sound.

His style helped define early Southern trap aesthetics and influenced later Memphis and Atlanta rap movements.

Legacy

Project Pat remains a foundational figure in Memphis hip-hop, closely associated with the rise of Three 6 Mafia and the broader Southern rap explosion of the early 2000s.

His catalog continues to be referenced in modern trap music for its rhythmic flow patterns and dark, minimal production style.
