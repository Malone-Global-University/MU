Style and Influence

Gangsta Boo was known for a direct, vivid Memphis delivery that combined grit, confidence, and melodic repetition in a way that made her one of the most distinctive voices in Southern rap.

Her solo work and group appearances helped expand the range of female voices in horrorcore, crunk, and Southern hip-hop more broadly.

Legacy

Gangsta Boo’s catalog remains central to the history of Memphis rap, especially through her work on Enquiring Minds and her defining contributions to Three 6 Mafia.

Her later collaborations with newer artists kept her influence visible across generations, and she was reportedly working on The BooPrint at the time of her death.
