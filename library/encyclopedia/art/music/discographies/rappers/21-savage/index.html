Certifications and Legacy

21 Savage rose from Atlanta’s underground rap scene to become one of the defining voices of modern trap music. His understated delivery and cold, minimalist production style helped shape a major shift in mainstream hip-hop aesthetics during the late 2010s.

Projects like Savage Mode and I Am > I Was established him as both a commercial force and a critically respected artist, while his collaborations with Metro Boomin are considered landmark trap recordings of the streaming era.
