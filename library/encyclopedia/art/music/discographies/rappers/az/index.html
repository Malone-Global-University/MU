Style and Influence

AZ is widely known for his smooth, controlled delivery and highly detailed street narratives, often emphasizing realism, introspection, and lyrical precision over aggressive performance styles.

His early collaboration with Nas on Illmatic helped cement his reputation as one of the most respected lyricists in East Coast hip-hop during the 1990s.

Legacy

AZ remains an influential figure in underground and lyrical rap circles, with Doe or Die frequently cited as a classic example of mafioso rap storytelling.

His consistency across decades has earned him a reputation as one of hip-hop’s most dependable lyricists.
