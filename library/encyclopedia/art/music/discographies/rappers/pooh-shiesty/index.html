Style and Influence

Pooh Shiesty is recognized for a hard-edged Memphis trap sound built around minimalist production, aggressive vocal delivery, and street-oriented storytelling.

His rise through 1017 Records helped define a new wave of Southern rap in the early 2020s, closely tied to Atlanta and Memphis collaborative trap movements.

Legacy

Pooh Shiesty’s breakout success with “Back in Blood” marked him as one of the most prominent new-generation trap artists, with a rapid ascent fueled by viral singles and high-profile collaborations.

Despite legal setbacks interrupting his career, his early catalog remains influential within modern Southern hip-hop.
