Style and Influence

DJ Screw is known for pioneering the chopped and screwed technique, slowing down tempos and reworking hip-hop records into hypnotic, bass-heavy soundscapes that defined Houston’s underground music identity.

His mixtapes established a new form of DJ authorship where tape culture, regional identity, and remix aesthetics became central to Southern hip-hop.

Legacy

DJ Screw’s work laid the foundation for Houston’s Screwed Up Click and influenced generations of Southern hip-hop artists.

His tape culture remains one of the most important independent music movements in American hip-hop history.
