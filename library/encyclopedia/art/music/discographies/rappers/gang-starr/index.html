Style and Influence

Gang Starr helped define the sound of East Coast hip-hop through DJ Premier’s chopped, jazz-heavy production and Guru’s calm, authoritative lyrical delivery.

Their music established a blueprint for jazz-rap and influenced generations of producers and lyricists across underground and mainstream hip-hop.

Legacy

Gang Starr remains one of the most important duos in hip-hop history, shaping the Golden Era sound and elevating DJ-based production as a core artistic force.

Even after Guru’s passing, the group’s catalog continues to be regarded as foundational to modern rap production aesthetics.
