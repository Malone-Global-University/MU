Style and Sound

TQ’s music blends soulful R&B vocals with hip-hop influenced production, often reflecting emotional storytelling rooted in West Coast street and relationship narratives.

His work is known for bridging mainstream R&B radio appeal with underground rap sensibilities.

Legacy

TQ is recognized for helping define late-1990s and early-2000s West Coast R&B crossover music, particularly through his breakout single “Westside.”

His catalog remains a reference point for melodic rap-influenced R&B artists who followed.
