Style and Influence

Cypress Hill are known for their dark, atmospheric production, distinctive bilingual influence, and early mainstream representation of cannabis culture in hip-hop.

Their sound helped bridge West Coast rap with Latin hip-hop influences, creating a unique and globally recognized style.

Legacy

Black Sunday is widely regarded as one of the most influential hip-hop albums of the early 1990s.

Cypress Hill remain one of the most enduring groups in hip-hop, with a catalog spanning over three decades and multiple stylistic evolutions.
