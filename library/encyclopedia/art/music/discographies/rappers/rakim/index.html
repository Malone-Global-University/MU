Style and Influence

Rakim is known for revolutionizing rap lyricism through internal rhyme schemes, multisyllabic flows, and a calm, controlled delivery that contrasted sharply with early hip-hop’s more rhythmic shouting styles.

His work with Eric B. established a new standard for MC technique that influenced nearly every major rapper who followed in the late 1980s and 1990s.

Legacy

Rakim is widely regarded as one of the greatest rappers of all time, often credited with transforming MCing into a more complex and literary art form.

Albums like Paid in Full remain foundational texts in hip-hop history.
