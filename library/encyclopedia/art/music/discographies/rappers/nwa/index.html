Certifications and Legacy

After achieving regional success as a member of Playaz Circle, 2 Chainz reinvented himself as a solo artist and became one of the most visible rappers of the 2010s. His combination of humor, wordplay, and memorable guest appearances helped make him a fixture of mainstream hip-hop.

Albums such as Based on a T.R.U. Story and Pretty Girls Like Trap Music established him as a successful solo performer, while his collaborations with artists across multiple generations helped broaden his influence within the genre.
