Certifications and Legacy

Nas is widely regarded as one of the greatest emcees in hip-hop history. His debut album Illmatic is frequently cited among the most influential rap albums ever recorded, while later releases such as It Was Written, Stillmatic, Life Is Good, and the King's Disease series helped establish one of the longest and most critically acclaimed careers in rap music.

Throughout multiple decades, Nas has remained a major cultural figure, earning Grammy Awards, platinum certifications, and widespread recognition for his storytelling, lyrical craftsmanship, and influence on generations of hip-hop artists.
