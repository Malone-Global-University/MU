Style and Influence

Juvenile is known for a commanding voice, simple but memorable phrasing, and a style that helped make New Orleans rap a national force in the late 1990s and early 2000s.

His work with Cash Money, the Hot Boys, and later independent releases helped define the sound and swagger of Southern mainstream rap.

Legacy

Juvenile’s catalog is anchored by 400 Degreez, a landmark Southern rap album that remains one of the most important releases in hip-hop history.

His career spans the Cash Money rise, Hot Boys legacy, successful solo albums, and the 2026 release of Boiling Point.
