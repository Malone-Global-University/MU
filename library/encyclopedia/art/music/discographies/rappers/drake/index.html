Style and Influence

Drake is known for blending melodic singing with rap delivery, shaping a modern crossover style that dominates global streaming-era popular music.

His work helped redefine mainstream hip-hop’s emotional range, commercial structure, and international reach.

Legacy

Drake’s catalog represents one of the most commercially successful and culturally influential bodies of work in modern music history.

He remains a central figure in shaping 21st-century hip-hop and pop convergence.
