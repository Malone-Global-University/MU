Style and Influence

Big Pun is widely regarded as one of the most technically advanced rappers of all time, known for dense internal rhymes, breath control, and fast-paced delivery that pushed lyrical complexity in mainstream hip-hop.

His success helped elevate Latino representation in rap while maintaining strong roots in Bronx hip-hop culture.

Legacy

Big Pun’s influence continues to be felt in lyrical rap circles, with Capital Punishment often cited as a classic debut album in hip-hop history.

His style set a benchmark for technical MC performance and remains widely studied by rappers and lyricists.
