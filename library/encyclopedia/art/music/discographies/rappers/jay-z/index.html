Certifications and Legacy

JAY-Z's discography traces a progression from the street-level storytelling of Reasonable Doubt to the large-scale, chart-topping releases that followed across the Roc-A-Fella, Roc Nation, and collaborative eras. His catalog includes landmark albums such as The Blueprint, The Black Album, and 4:44, along with major partnership projects with R. Kelly, Linkin Park, Kanye West, and Beyoncé.

His work has been central to the sound and business identity of contemporary hip-hop. Across three decades, JAY-Z has remained one of the genre's defining figures, with a discography recognized for its influence, commercial success, and long-term cultural impact.
