Style and Influence

Chief Keef is widely recognized as a foundational figure in drill music, known for minimalist beats, aggressive delivery, and a raw vocal aesthetic that reshaped Chicago hip-hop.

His early viral success influenced an entire generation of internet-driven rap artists and producers, particularly within the drill and trap movements.

Legacy

Chief Keef’s debut album Finally Rich is considered a landmark release in modern rap, helping bring Chicago drill into mainstream awareness.

His independent catalog continues to influence global hip-hop production styles, especially in underground and digital-first music scenes.
