Style and Sound

TrU’s music reflects the signature No Limit Records sound, characterized by heavy bass, minimalist Southern production, and direct street narratives.

Their work helped define the commercial explosion of Southern rap in the late 1990s.

Legacy

TrU remains an important part of No Limit Records’ rise, contributing to one of the most commercially successful independent rap movements in hip-hop history.

Their catalog represents the foundation of the New Orleans rap industry’s national expansion.
