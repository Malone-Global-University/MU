Cardi B

Bronx rapper and media personality known for bold delivery and mainstream hip-hop success

Biography

Cardi B, born Belcalis Marlenis Almánzar in New York City, is a rapper and media personality known for her outspoken personality and energetic delivery style. She first gained public attention through social media and reality television before transitioning into music. Her personality-driven brand helped her quickly stand out in the hip-hop industry.

Her breakthrough single “Bodak Yellow” became a major cultural hit, leading to her debut album Invasion of Privacy, which received widespread critical acclaim. The project included multiple chart-topping singles and established her as one of the leading voices in modern rap. Her music blends trap influences with bold lyrical expression.

Cardi B has maintained strong mainstream visibility through collaborations, performances, and cultural influence across fashion and entertainment. Her impact extends beyond music into broader pop culture.

Legacy

Cardi B’s legacy is defined by her rise from social media personality to global hip-hop superstar. She is widely recognized as one of the most influential female rappers of the modern era. Her influence continues across rap, pop culture, and entertainment media.
