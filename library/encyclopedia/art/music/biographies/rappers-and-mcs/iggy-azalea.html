Iggy Azalea

Australian rapper known for global pop-rap hits, viral success, and 2010s crossover hip-hop presence

Biography

Iggy Azalea, born Amethyst Kelly in Sydney, Australia, is a rapper known for her international success in the pop-rap and mainstream hip-hop scene. She gained early attention through online releases and later moved to the United States to pursue her music career. Her style blends Southern hip-hop influences with pop-oriented production and catchy hooks.

She achieved major commercial success in the mid-2010s with chart-topping singles and widespread global recognition. Her music became closely associated with viral pop culture moments and radio dominance during that period. She remains a prominent figure in discussions of global hip-hop crossover artists.
