Styles P

Yonkers rapper known for The LOX, hardcore lyricism, and East Coast street rap influence

Biography

Styles P, born David Styles in Yonkers, New York, is a rapper known for his gritty lyricism, introspective street narratives, and consistent presence in East Coast hip-hop. He rose to prominence as a member of The LOX alongside Jadakiss and Sheek Louch. His style blends raw street storytelling with reflective and philosophical themes.

He gained recognition through group success with The LOX as well as a steady solo career featuring numerous albums and mixtapes. His writing often focuses on survival, personal growth, and street wisdom. He is also known for his dedication to traditional hip-hop lyricism and independent music output.

Styles P is widely respected as one of the most consistent lyricists in New York hip-hop.

Legacy

Styles P’s legacy is defined by hardcore lyricism and authentic East Coast street rap. He is widely regarded as one of the most reliable and respected voices in The LOX and Yonkers hip-hop. His influence continues across underground rap, street storytelling, and lyrical hip-hop culture.
