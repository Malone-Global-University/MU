Luniz

Hip-hop duo from Oakland known for their West Coast classic “I Got 5 on It”

Biography

Luniz is an American hip-hop duo from Oakland, California, composed of Yukmouth and Numskull. Emerging in the mid-1990s, they became part of the broader Bay Area rap movement, which was gaining national attention for its independent spirit, regional identity, and distinctive West Coast sound. Their music combined laid-back storytelling, street narratives, and melodic production that reflected the Bay Area’s evolving hip-hop culture.

The duo achieved international recognition with their 1995 single “I Got 5 on It,” a track that became a defining anthem of 1990s West Coast hip-hop. Built around a memorable production and relatable themes, the song crossed over into mainstream success while maintaining strong regional authenticity. It remains one of the most recognizable hip-hop records of its era and has been widely referenced in popular culture.

Following the success of their debut album Operation Stackola, Luniz continued releasing music throughout the late 1990s and beyond. Their work reflected the diversity of Bay Area rap, blending humor, social observation, and street-level perspective. While their commercial peak was centered around their breakout single, the duo maintained a lasting presence within West Coast hip-hop circles.

Legacy

Luniz’s legacy is anchored in their contribution to Bay Area hip-hop and the enduring popularity of “I Got 5 on It.” The song remains a cultural touchstone of 1990s rap and is frequently cited as one of the most iconic West Coast tracks of the decade. Their success helped further establish Oakland as an important center in hip-hop history and continues to influence regional rap identity.
