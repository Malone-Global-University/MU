Slim Thug

Houston rapper known for Southern rap leadership, chopped-and-screwed influence, and mainstream breakout success

Biography

Slim Thug, born Stayve Jerome Thomas in Houston, Texas, is a rapper and entrepreneur known for his deep voice, commanding presence, and leadership in the Houston hip-hop scene. He first gained attention through the Houston underground before achieving mainstream recognition in the mid-2000s. His music is closely associated with the city’s chopped-and-screwed and chopped-not-slopped culture.

He rose to national prominence with hits like “Like a Boss,” which showcased his smooth yet authoritative delivery. His debut album Already Platinum helped establish him as one of Houston’s key mainstream breakout artists. He has also remained active in independent releases and collaborations within Southern hip-hop.

Slim Thug is recognized for his consistency and influence in shaping Houston’s modern rap identity.

Legacy

Slim Thug’s legacy is defined by Houston rap leadership and mainstream Southern hip-hop expansion. He is widely regarded as one of the key figures in bringing Houston’s underground sound into national awareness. His influence continues across Southern rap culture and independent hip-hop entrepreneurship.
