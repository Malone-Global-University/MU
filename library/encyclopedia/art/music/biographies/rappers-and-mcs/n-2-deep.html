N 2 Deep

Bay Area hip-hop duo known for smooth G-funk influenced West Coast rap

Biography

N 2 Deep is a hip-hop duo from Vallejo, California, composed of Jay Tee and TL. Emerging in the early 1990s, they became part of the expanding Bay Area rap scene that emphasized regional identity, independent production, and G-funk influenced soundscapes. Their music reflects a smooth yet street-oriented style characteristic of Northern California hip-hop during the era.

The duo gained recognition with early releases that circulated strongly within the Bay Area underground scene before achieving broader West Coast attention. Their debut album Back to the Hotel featured the title track that became their most recognizable song, helping them break into national visibility. The track’s laid-back production and melodic hook made it a staple of 1990s West Coast rap.

Throughout the 1990s and beyond, N 2 Deep continued releasing music that reflected the evolving sound of Bay Area hip-hop. They maintained a consistent presence in regional rap culture, collaborating with other West Coast artists and contributing to the growth of Northern California’s hip-hop identity.

Legacy

N 2 Deep’s legacy is tied to the expansion of Bay Area G-funk and early 1990s West Coast rap. They are remembered for helping bring Vallejo and Northern California artists into broader hip-hop recognition. Their music remains part of the foundational soundscape of 1990s West Coast hip-hop culture.
