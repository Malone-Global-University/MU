Rico Nasty

Rapper known for aggressive delivery, punk-influenced style, and experimental hip-hop energy

Biography

Rico Nasty, born Maria-Cecilia Simone Kelly in Maryland, is a rapper known for her high-energy delivery and punk-influenced hip-hop style. She emerged in the late 2010s through viral tracks and a rapidly developing underground following. Her sound blends rap with elements of punk rock aggression and experimental production.

She gained recognition through projects such as Nightmare Vacation, which showcased her versatility across aggressive rap, melodic tracks, and alternative influences. Her collaborations with producers in the trap and experimental scenes helped define her signature sound. She is known for switching between hard-hitting rap and playful melodic styles.

Rico Nasty is also recognized for her bold visual aesthetic, often incorporating punk, goth, and DIY influences into her artistic identity.

Legacy

Rico Nasty’s legacy is defined by punk-rap fusion and experimental energy in hip-hop. She is widely regarded as a key figure in alternative rap’s modern evolution. Her influence continues across experimental hip-hop, punk rap, and genre-blending performance styles.
