La Chat

Memphis rapper known for aggressive delivery and early Southern rap innovation

Biography

La Chat is a Memphis-based rapper who emerged from the same Southern hip-hop environment that helped define the city’s influential underground rap movement in the 1990s and early 2000s. Known for her sharp tone and confrontational delivery, she developed a distinct voice within a scene dominated largely by male performers.

Growing out of the Memphis rap ecosystem, her style reflected the city’s signature sound—dark, bass-heavy production paired with intense, rhythm-driven vocal performance. Her work helped expand the range of voices representing Southern hip-hop during its rise to national recognition.
