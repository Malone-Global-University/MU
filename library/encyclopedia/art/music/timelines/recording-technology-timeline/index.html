Recording Technology Timeline

Trace recording technology through capture, storage, editing, mixing, mastering, distribution, streaming, and listening formats.

Overview

The Recording Technology Timeline helps students understand how technologies changed music creation, preservation, production, distribution, business, and listening habits.

This page belongs to the Music Timelines area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Timeline pages should connect dates and periods to listening, artists, recordings, technologies, communities, institutions, and cultural context.
