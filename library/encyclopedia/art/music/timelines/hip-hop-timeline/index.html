Hip-Hop Timeline

Trace hip-hop through DJing, MCing, breakbeats, sampling, production, regional scenes, lyricism, and global culture.

Overview

The Hip-Hop Timeline connects hip-hop to neighborhood culture, DJs, MCs, breakbeats, sound systems, sampling, dance, visual art, recording technology, regional scenes, and global media.

This page belongs to the Music Timelines area of the Malone Global University Music Department. Timeline pages should connect dates and periods to listening, artists, recordings, technologies, communities, institutions, and cultural context.
