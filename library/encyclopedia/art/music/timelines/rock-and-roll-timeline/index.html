Overview

Musical Roots

Rock and roll developed from blues, R&B, gospel, country, boogie-woogie, dance music, and electric performance practice.

Early Rock and Mass Media

Radio, television, records, touring, and youth audiences helped rock and roll become a major popular force.

Subgenres and Expansion

Rock expanded into folk rock, psychedelic rock, punk, metal, progressive rock, alternative rock, indie rock, and many global scenes.
