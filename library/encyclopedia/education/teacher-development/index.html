Teacher Development Overview

Teacher development studies the preparation, growth, certification, and ongoing improvement of educators throughout their professional careers. The field examines teacher training systems, classroom practice, professional learning, licensure, and reflective improvement processes. It focuses on building and sustaining effective teaching capability.

Disciplines

  • Certification and Licensure

    Study formal qualification systems governing teacher credentialing and authorization.

  • Classroom Practice

    Examine day-to-day instructional execution and practical teaching behavior.

  • Professional Development

    Study continuing education and growth systems for educators.

  • Reflective Practice

    Explore structured self-evaluation and improvement methods for teachers.

  • Teacher Training

    Study preparation systems and instructional training for new educators.
