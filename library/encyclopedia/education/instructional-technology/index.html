Instructional Technology Overview

Instructional technology studies the use of digital tools, software platforms, analytics, and media systems to support teaching and learning. The field examines how technological systems enhance educational delivery, accessibility, engagement, personalization, and measurement across instructional environments. It integrates pedagogy with digital infrastructure.

Disciplines

  • Digital Learning Platforms

    Study software environments used to deliver, organize, and manage digital learning.

  • Educational Software

    Examine applications and programs designed specifically for instructional use.

  • Learning Analytics

    Study measurement and analysis of learner data to improve educational outcomes.

  • Multimedia Instruction

    Explore use of audio, video, graphics, and multimedia formats in teaching.

  • Online Learning

    Study delivery of instruction through internet-based and remote education systems.
