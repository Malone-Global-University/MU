Malone Global University Library

Established 2025

Collections

  • Dictionary

    A structured reference system for precise definitions of terms, phrases, and specialized vocabulary used across the library. Each entry focuses on clarity, linguistic meaning, and standardized usage so that concepts can be understood without ambiguity or contextual dependency.

  • Encyclopedia

    A curated knowledge base organized by topics and subjects, providing detailed explanatory articles that expand beyond definitions. Entries focus on background, context, development, and interconnected concepts to give a broader understanding of each subject area.

  • Navigation Guides

    Orientation and instructional support for using the MGU Library. Guides explain how the library is organized, where major collections are located, how departments and subdomain areas connect, and how users can find definitions, articles, tutorials, reference materials, courses, and research pathways without searching blindly.

  • Textbooks

    Complete course-style learning books organized into chapters, sections, examples, reviews, and extended explanations. Textbooks provide sustained instruction across major subjects, helping students move from foundational concepts to deeper understanding through structured lessons, practice, vocabulary, diagrams, and progressive academic development.

  • Vocational Trade Manuals

    Comprehensive field-oriented training manuals designed to teach practical skills in construction, skilled trades, and technical occupations. Each manual combines foundational knowledge, tools, materials, safety practices, procedures, and project-based instruction to help learners develop real-world capabilities through structured hands-on training.

  • Tutorials

    Step-by-step instructional resources designed to help users complete specific tasks or develop practical skills. Guides focus on applied learning, breaking down processes into clear, sequential actions with examples, tips, and best practices for implementation.

  • Malonicus Rex Educare

    A comprehensive educational framework that integrates structured learning materials, doctrine-based content, and interdisciplinary instruction. It functions as an overarching system for deep study, combining philosophy, strategy, history, and applied knowledge into a unified curriculum.

  • Reference

    A technical and structured repository of supporting materials used for quick lookup and verification. It includes data tables, standards, specifications, formulas, and authoritative sources intended for precise consultation rather than narrative explanation.

  • Wiki (Planned 2027/2028)

    A continuously evolving collection of interconnected pages designed for collaborative expansion and rapid updates. Content emphasizes cross-linking between topics, iterative refinement, and flexible structure for fast knowledge growth and revision.
