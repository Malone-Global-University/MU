MGU Textbooks Index

The MGU Textbooks Index organizes extended course-style references by subject, volume, part, chapter, and lesson sequence for students who need complete learning paths.

MGU Textbooks Overview

MGU Textbooks are long-form instructional works designed to provide structured learning beyond individual lessons, encyclopedia articles, or short reference pages. They can include front matter, course maps, chapters, examples, review sections, glossaries, answer keys, and other textbook-style supports.

This section is intended for complete or developing textbook projects across the university. Textbooks may exist as HTML books, downloadable documents, manuscript drafts, chapter sets, course readers, or reference handbooks. Each textbook should eventually have its own internal table of contents and navigation structure.

Use this index as the main directory for textbook-level resources. Individual textbook folders can be organized by subject area, course level, volume, part, chapter, and supporting back matter.
