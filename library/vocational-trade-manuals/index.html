Vocational Trade Field Manuals

The Vocational Trade Field Manuals are project-based guides designed for hands-on construction and skilled trades education. Rather than serving as traditional textbooks, these manuals walk learners through complete field projects using professional procedures, industry-standard practices, construction sequencing, safety requirements, and real-world workflows.

Overview

Each field manual focuses on practical execution. Projects are presented from planning through final inspection using step-by-step procedures, required tools, material lists, measurements, safety practices, quality-control checkpoints, troubleshooting guidance, and professional standards. Together these manuals form a comprehensive construction training program covering residential construction, building systems, site work, project management, and contractor operations.

Learning Outcomes

  • Develop practical skills through complete construction projects.
  • Apply industry-standard safety procedures on active jobsites.
  • Read construction documents and execute work according to plans and specifications.
  • Select appropriate tools, equipment, and materials for each project.
  • Perform work that meets professional quality standards and applicable building codes.
  • Understand how individual trades integrate into complete residential construction projects.
  • Build the knowledge and experience needed for employment, apprenticeships, or independent contracting.
