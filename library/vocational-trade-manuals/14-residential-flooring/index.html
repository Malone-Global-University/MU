14-Residential Flooring

This field manual provides practical, project-based instruction for residential flooring installation, preparation, repair, and finishing. Students learn to evaluate floor conditions, prepare substrates, install multiple flooring systems, complete transitions, and perform professional flooring repairs.

Overview

Residential Flooring emphasizes hands-on skill development through complete flooring installation projects. Beginning with subfloor inspection and surface preparation, students progress through underlayment installation, laminate, luxury vinyl plank, sheet vinyl, hardwood, engineered flooring, tile, carpet, transitions, stair flooring, repairs, and a complete practice room flooring installation.

Learning Outcomes

  • Inspect subfloors and plan residential flooring installations.
  • Prepare safe and organized flooring installation work areas.
  • Repair, level, and prepare subfloor surfaces.
  • Install underlayment and moisture protection systems.
  • Install laminate, luxury vinyl, hardwood, engineered wood, and tile flooring.
  • Install carpet systems and complete flooring transitions.
  • Fit flooring around doors, trim, stairs, and complex areas.
  • Repair common residential flooring damage.
  • Perform professional flooring quality inspections.
  • Complete a full residential flooring installation project.
