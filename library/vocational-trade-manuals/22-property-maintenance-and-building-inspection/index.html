22-Property Maintenance and Building Inspection

This field manual provides practical, project-based instruction for residential property maintenance, inspection, and preventive care. Students learn to evaluate building systems, document conditions, perform routine maintenance, identify problems, and create long-term property care plans.

Overview

Property Maintenance and Building Inspection emphasizes hands-on skill development through complete residential evaluation and maintenance projects. Beginning with full property walkthroughs and documentation systems, students progress through exterior inspections, roofing, drainage, foundations, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, insulation, appliances, seasonal maintenance, repair planning, and a complete property inspection capstone project.

Learning Outcomes

  • Perform complete residential property walkthrough inspections.
  • Create organized maintenance records and inspection documentation.
  • Inspect roofing, drainage, foundations, and exterior building systems.
  • Evaluate windows, doors, weather sealing, and interior finishes.
  • Perform basic preventive maintenance on plumbing systems.
  • Inspect electrical devices, lighting, and safety equipment.
  • Evaluate HVAC systems, insulation, ventilation, and moisture conditions.
  • Identify maintenance needs and prioritize repairs.
  • Create seasonal property maintenance schedules.
  • Complete a full residential inspection and maintenance plan.
