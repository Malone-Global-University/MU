18-Hardscaping

This field manual provides practical, project-based instruction for residential hardscape construction. Students learn to design, prepare, install, repair, and maintain outdoor surfaces including paver patios, walkways, retaining walls, drainage systems, and landscape structures.

Overview

Hardscaping emphasizes hands-on skill development through complete outdoor construction projects. Beginning with project planning and site preparation, students progress through layout, excavation, base preparation, paver installation, retaining walls, drainage systems, repairs, maintenance, and a complete paver patio or walkway capstone project.

Learning Outcomes

  • Inspect sites and plan residential hardscaping projects.
  • Prepare safe and organized hardscape work areas.
  • Layout walkways, patios, and paver installation areas.
  • Excavate and prepare stable hardscape subgrades.
  • Install compacted gravel bases and bedding sand systems.
  • Install paver walkways, patios, borders, and accent features.
  • Build segmental retaining walls and drainage systems.
  • Construct outdoor steps using block, stone, and pavers.
  • Repair, clean, seal, and maintain hardscape surfaces.
  • Complete a residential paver patio or walkway project.
