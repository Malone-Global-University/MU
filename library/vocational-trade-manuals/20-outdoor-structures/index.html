20-Outdoor Structures

This field manual provides practical, project-based instruction for residential outdoor structure construction. Students learn to plan, lay out, frame, build, repair, and maintain decks, fences, pergolas, arbors, sheds, and other small outdoor structures.

Overview

Outdoor Structures emphasizes hands-on skill development through complete residential exterior construction projects. Beginning with site evaluation and layout, students progress through post foundations, framing, decking, stairs, railings, fences, gates, pergolas, arbors, sheds, storage structures, repairs, and a complete outdoor structure capstone project.

Learning Outcomes

  • Inspect sites and plan residential outdoor structure projects.
  • Prepare safe and organized outdoor construction work areas.
  • Layout posts, footings, and structural reference lines.
  • Install posts, framing systems, decking, stairs, and railings.
  • Build privacy fences, gates, pergolas, and garden structures.
  • Construct small shed floors, walls, roofs, siding, and doors.
  • Build outdoor storage structures and utility enclosures.
  • Repair and maintain residential exterior wood structures.
  • Apply proper construction techniques for outdoor durability.
  • Complete a complete small outdoor structure construction project.
