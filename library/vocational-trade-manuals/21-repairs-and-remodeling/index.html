21-Repairs and Remodeling

This field manual provides practical, project-based instruction for residential repair and remodeling work. Students learn to evaluate existing conditions, perform selective demolition, repair damaged building components, update interior spaces, and complete small-scale renovation projects.

Overview

Repairs and Remodeling emphasizes hands-on skill development through realistic residential renovation projects. Beginning with project evaluation and safe work preparation, students progress through demolition, drywall repairs, flooring repairs, door and trim updates, bathroom and kitchen improvements, minor framing, water damage repairs, room refreshes, final inspections, and a complete small room remodel capstone project.

Learning Outcomes

  • Inspect and scope residential repair and remodeling projects.
  • Prepare safe remodeling work areas with proper dust control.
  • Perform selective demolition and material removal.
  • Repair drywall, flooring, trim, doors, and interior finishes.
  • Complete basic bathroom and kitchen improvement projects.
  • Update cabinets, hardware, fixtures, and interior components.
  • Perform minor framing and finish work for remodeling applications.
  • Repair minor water damage and restore affected areas.
  • Complete remodeling punch lists and quality inspections.
  • Execute a complete small residential room remodel project.
