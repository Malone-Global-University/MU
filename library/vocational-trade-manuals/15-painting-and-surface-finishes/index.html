15-Painting and Surface Finishes

This field manual provides practical, project-based instruction for residential painting, staining, coating, and surface preparation. Students learn to prepare surfaces, apply professional paint finishes, refinish materials, repair defects, and complete interior and exterior finishing projects.

Overview

Painting and Surface Finishes emphasizes hands-on skill development through complete residential finishing projects. Beginning with surface inspection and preparation, students progress through cleaning, patching, caulking, masking, priming, interior and exterior painting, cabinet and wood finishing, specialty coatings, repairs, quality inspection, and a complete practice room capstone project.

Learning Outcomes

  • Inspect surfaces and plan residential painting projects.
  • Prepare safe and organized painting work areas.
  • Clean, repair, patch, sand, and prepare surfaces for finishing.
  • Apply caulk, primer, and protective surface preparation products.
  • Apply professional interior wall and ceiling paint finishes.
  • Paint doors, trim, cabinets, and built-in components.
  • Apply stains, clear coats, and wood finishing techniques.
  • Complete exterior painting and specialty coating projects.
  • Repair paint failures and perform finish corrections.
  • Perform final quality inspections and complete a professional painting project.
