08-Residential Electrical

This field manual provides practical, project-based instruction for residential electrical work. Students learn to install, troubleshoot, and repair common residential electrical systems while following safe work practices and applicable electrical codes.

Overview

Residential Electrical emphasizes hands-on skill development through real-world installation projects. Beginning with electrical safety and circuit identification, students progress through wiring methods, receptacles, switches, lighting, circuit protection, troubleshooting, and subpanel installation before completing a fully wired residential practice wall.

Learning Outcomes

  • Work safely around residential electrical systems.
  • Install electrical boxes, wiring, switches, receptacles, and lighting devices.
  • Apply proper grounding and bonding techniques.
  • Install AFCI and GFCI protection devices.
  • Troubleshoot common residential electrical faults.
  • Install a residential subpanel and branch circuits.
  • Complete a fully wired residential practice wall.
